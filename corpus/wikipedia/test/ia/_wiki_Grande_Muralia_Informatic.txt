Grande Muralia Informatic o GMI  esse un expression pro describer le combination de technologia e action legislative imperate per le Republica Popular de China pro regular le uso domestic del interrete.
In le vaste systema de censura chinese, su rolo esse blocar le accesso a selecte cybersitos extere e relentar le traffico de datos trans-frontiera.
Le actuation include: limitar le accesso al information extere, blocar instrumentos clave e applicationes mobile del extero , o requirer a companias extere adaptar se a restrictive normas domestic.
