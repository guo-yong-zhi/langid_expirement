Mohammed Auwal Mu'azu dan asalin jihar Gombe ne kuma mazaunin jihar Kaduna . Ya sami takardar shaidar makarantar firamare daga makarantar firamare ta Demonstration. Daga nan ya wuce Bright International College na SSCE, inda ya samu babbar difloma ta kasa  a fannin Gudanarwa da Nazarin Kasuwanci a Kwalejin Fasaha ta Kaduna da ke Jihar Kaduna .  4 
Kimanin shekaru goma da suka gabata, Muhammad mu'azu ya kafa shahararren shafin yanar gizo na Hausa – Fulani a matsayin daya daga cikin shahararrun masu yada labarai a Najeriya domin nunawa, ilimantarwa da kuma sanar da mai niyyar amfani da al'adun Hausa – Fulani da kuma kimar su ga duniya. Tun lokacin da aka kafa shi, Mohmmed Auwal koyaushe yana aiki don amfani da dandamalinsa don cike gibin da ke tsakanin Arewa da kudu. 5  6 
Ya kuma shirya tarurruka da dama ga dan kasuwa wanda ya kirkiro tare da inganta wayar da kan jama'a ga masu son cinikayya, wasu daga cikinsu wasannin kwaikwayo ne,bikin baje kolin dangi, baje kolin abinci, da dan kasuwa masu haduwa da gaishe-gaishe, da sauransu.  7  Ya yi aiki tare da ofishin mataimakin shugaban kasa Yemi Osinbajo kan harkokin yada labarai a lokacin yakin neman zaben 2019, shi dan kungiya ne na kungiyar yada labaran shugaban kasa, sannan yana daga cikin tawagar da ke inganta ayyuka da ayyuka a karkashin ma'aikatar kula da jin kai, kula da bala'i, da cigaban zamantakewar da Sadiya Umar Farouq ta kula .  8
