An kafa Kwalejin Rainbow a shekara ta 1996 a Najeriya . Tana nan a km 39 Lagos / Ibadan expressway, jihar Ogun .  1  Yana hidimar shirya samari da 'yan mata don ƙarin ilimi ko dai a wata jami'a a Najeriya ko kuma a wasu wurare na duniya. Kwaleji ce da ke ba da shirye -shiryen ilimi ga ɗaliban dukkan ƙasashe.
Ilimin ilimi a kwalejin yana ɗaukar shekaru shida kuma ya ƙunshi shekaru uku a Makarantar Sakandare ta Junior sannan shekara uku a Babban Sakandare.  2  Wasu daga cikin yarukan da ake bayarwa a kwalejin Rainbow sun haɗa da yaren Ingiliziyya, Faransanci, yaren Yoruba, da yaren Igbo
Babbar Makarantar Sakandari ta mai da hankali kan shirye -shirye kan manyan jarabawa uku: Jarrabawar Takaddun Makarantar Sakandari ta Yammacin Afirka , Majalisar Jarrabawa ta Kasa   Babban Makarantar Sakandari ta Makaranta , da Babban Takaddar Babbar Jagora ta Duniya  .
