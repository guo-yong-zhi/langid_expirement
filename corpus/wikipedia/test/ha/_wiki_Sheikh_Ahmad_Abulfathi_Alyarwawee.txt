Sheikh Ahmad Abulfathi  babban malami ne wanda ya koyar kuma yayi umurni da a koyar da addinin islama ta hanyar sufaye. Shehi  ne na darikar Tijjaniya wanda kuma ya kuma rayu a Æ™asashen; Nijeriya, Nijar, Cadi da kuma Senegal tsakanin shekarun 1919 da shekara ta 2003 miladiya. Shi mutumin Konduga ne a Jihar Borno, Nijeriya.
Maula Sheikh Abulfathi  dan Aliyu ne, bin Ahmad bin Aliyu bin Muhammad Mustapha bin Muhammad Mukhtar bin Adam bin Dawud bin Abdulganiyyu bin Zubair bin Abbas bin Hissain bin Sulaiman bin Ishaq bin Ali Zainnul Abideen bin Ahmad bin Muhammad Nafsu-Zakiyya bin Abdullahi Kamill bin Hassanul Musanna bin Hassannul Sibdi bin Aliyu bin Abi-Dalib .

Shehi yayi wafati a ranar Laraba 21 ga watan Safar 1424 A.H. wanda yayi daidai da 23 ga watan April 2003. Ya bar matan aure hudu da yaya arbain da uku.
