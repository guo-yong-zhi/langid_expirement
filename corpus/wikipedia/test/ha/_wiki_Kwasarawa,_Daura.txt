Kwasarawa ya taba zama yankin Daura, Ya zama wani bangare na sabuwar karamar hukumar Sandamu da aka kafa   Masarautar Daura, Jihar Katsina, Najeriya.
A shekarar 1805 lokacin yakin Fulani, Jarumin Fulani Malam Ishaku ya karbe Daura, wanda ya kafa masarautun Musulunci . Hausa ta kafa kishiyar jihohin nan kusa, kuma mai mulkin daya, Malam Musa, aka sanya sabon Sarkin Daura da Birtaniya a shekara ta 1904,  Duk da yake Fulani sarakuna mulki da kuma kafa wata Musulunci masarautu a Daura karkashin Sokoto Khalifanci, a 1903–04, bayan da Turawan Ingila da Faransa suka raba mulkin Daura guda uku, Turawan mulkin mallaka sun nada Sarkin Zango Malam Musa a matsayin sabon sarkin gargajiya na masarautar Daura.
