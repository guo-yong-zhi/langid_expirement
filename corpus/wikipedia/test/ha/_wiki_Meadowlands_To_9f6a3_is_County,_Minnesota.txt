Garin Meadowlands birni ne, da ke cikin gundumar Saint Louis, Minnesota, Amurka. Yawan jama'a ya kai 304 a ƙidayar 2010. 
Babban titin Saint Louis County 7  yana gudana daga arewa zuwa kudu ta yankin gabas na garin. Babban Hanyar County 133  yana gudana gabas – yamma ta tsakiyar Garin Meadowlands.
Gundumar 5  da County 29  duk sun ratsa yankin yamma na garin da ke gudana daga arewa zuwa kudu.
Garin Meadowlands yana cikin garin a yanayin yanki amma yanki ne na daban.
Ƙungiyar da ba ta da haɗin gwiwa ta Payne tana cikin kusurwar arewa maso gabashin Meadowlands Township.
A cewar Ofishin Kididdiga na Amurka, garin yana da yawan yanki na 60.5 square miles    59.9 square miles    ƙasa ce kuma 0.6 square miles   , ko 0.98%, ruwa ne.
Kogin Whiteface yana gudana ta yammacin yankin garin.
Dajin Jihar Whiteface River yana cikin garin Meadowlands.
Kogin Saint Louis yana gudana arewa zuwa kudu tare da layin yamma na Garin Meadowlands tare da kusancin garin Elmer.
Wadannan suna kusa da Garin Meadowlands :
Dangane da ƙidayar  na 2000, akwai mutane 315, gidaje 128, da iyalai 92 da ke zaune a cikin garin. Yawan yawan jama'a ya kasance mutane 5.3 a kowace murabba'in mil . Akwai rukunin gidaje 165 a matsakaicin yawa na 2.8/sq mi . Tsarin launin fata na garin ya kasance 96.19% Fari, 1.90% Ba'amurke, da 1.90% daga jinsi biyu ko fiye.
Akwai gidaje 128, daga cikinsu kashi 30.5% na da ‘ya’ya ‘yan kasa da shekara 18 suna zaune tare da su, kashi 54.7% ma’aurata ne da ke zaune tare, kashi 7.8% na da mace mai gida babu miji, kashi 28.1% kuma ba na iyali ba ne. Kashi 23.4% na dukkan gidaje sun kasance mutane ne, kuma kashi 8.6% na da wanda ke zaune shi kaɗai wanda ya kai shekaru 65 ko sama da haka. Matsakaicin girman gidan ya kasance 2.46 kuma matsakaicin girman dangi shine 2.90.
A cikin garin yawan jama'a ya bazu, tare da 25.1% a ƙarƙashin shekaru 18, 6.7% daga 18 zuwa 24, 29.5% daga 25 zuwa 44, 24.4% daga 45 zuwa 64, da 14.3% waɗanda shekarunsu suka kai 65 ko sama da haka. . Tsakanin shekarun ya kasance shekaru 39. Ga kowane mata 100, akwai maza 138.6. Ga kowane mata 100 masu shekaru 18 zuwa sama, akwai maza 136.0.
Matsakaicin kuɗin shiga na gida a cikin garin shine $31,250, kuma matsakaicin kuɗin shiga na dangi shine $35,250. Maza suna da matsakaicin kudin shiga na $34,286 sabanin $30,000 na mata. Kudin shiga kowane mutum na garin shine $16,321. Kimanin kashi 9.3% na iyalai da 12.4% na yawan jama'a sun kasance ƙasa da layin talauci, gami da 18.4% na waɗanda ke ƙasa da shekaru 18 da 9.3% na waɗanda shekarunsu suka kai 65 ko sama da haka.
