Abraham Yeshayahu  Kushnir  ɗan wasan barkwanci ne na Isra'ila, ɗan wasan kwaikwayo kuma mai masaukin baki.
A lokacin makarantar sakandare Kushnir ya yi karatu a kauyen matasa HaKfar HaYarok .  1  Bayan haka, a lokacin aikin soja na tilas, Kushnir ya yi aiki a rukunin sojoji. Bayan da ya yi aikin soja Kushnir ya yi aiki a matsayin ɗan wasa kuma daga baya ya fara jagorantar wasannin kwaikwayo.
Ayyukansa da ƙwararrun ɗan wasan kwaikwayo ya fara ne a farkon 1980s lokacin da ya fara yin wasan kwaikwayo a tsaye. A cikin 1985 Kushnir ya karbi bakuncin Festigal, nunin waka na Isra'ila na shekara-shekara tare da Gadi Yagil .
A cikin shekarar 1987, yayin da yake ɗan shekara 27, Kushnir ya wakilci Isra'ila a wata gasar waƙar Eurovision ta 1987 tare da Nathan Dattner, a matsayin wani ɓangare na duo mai ban dariya HaBatlanim , tare da waƙar ban dariya Shir Habatlanim wadda ta ƙare a matsayi na 8.  1 
Kushnir yana da aure kuma yana da 'ya'ya maza biyu da mace guda. Dan Kushnir Yotam - actor, kuma ya taka leda a cikin sauran TV jerin "Alifim" .
