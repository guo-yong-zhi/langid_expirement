Mohammed Buba Marwa  Soja ne kuma ɗan siyasar Najeriya.  
Yayi gwamna  jihar Borno, da jihar Lagos 1  daga Agusta a shekarar 1996 zuwa Mayu a shekarar 1998 . Kuma Buba Marwa shine shugaban Hukumar hana sha da fataucin miyagun ƙwayoyi ta ƙasa  ta Tarayyar Najeriya wanda shugaban ƙasa Muhammadu Buhari ya naɗa shi, Ranar sha bakwai ga watan Janairun shekara 2021.
