Effium ƙasa mara kyau ta mutanen Efuim, ƙabilar Orring na Najeriya da aka samu a jihar Ebonyi. 1   Ita ce kuma babban kungiya mai cin gashin kanta a jihar Ebonyi dake yankin kudu maso gabashin Najeriya. Effium yana cikin karamar hukumar Ohaukwu a jihar Ebonyi. An san mutanen asalin da Effiumite ko Uffiom, kuma suna magana da yaren Kuffiom na harshen Korring. 2   Harshen Igbo da Ezza kuma ana magana.
