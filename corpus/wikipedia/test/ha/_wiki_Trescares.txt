Trescares ta kasan ce kuma tana ɗaya daga cikin majami'u guda takwas  a cikin Peñamellera Alta, wata ƙaramar hukuma a cikin lardin da kuma communitiyan yankin Asturias, a arewacin Spain . Tana cikin Picos de Europa National Park .
Yawan jama'a 55 .
A cikin wannan ƙauyen, akwai manyan mahimman gine-gine biyu na wannan ikon cin gashin kansa: coci kuma, mafi mahimmanci, La Vidre gada , wanda aka gina a lokacin romanic akan wata gada da Romawa suka gina. Gadar yau sanannu ne a duk duniya, don haka, ana yin ɗigon ruwa da yawa a ciki.
Anan ya wuce ɗayan mahimman kogunan Asturias, kogin Cares , wanda ya ƙare a cikin kogin Deva wanda ya raba al'ummar Asturias da Cantabria. Daya daga cikin wasannin da aka fi taka rawa shine kamun kifi,
Kamar wani ƙauye, Trescares yana da liyafa: Saint Fausto. Ana yin wannan bikin ne a ranar Asabar mai zuwa na The Pilar , inda mutanen Trescares ke sa rigunan asturian kuma suna ba da tsayin dina mai tsayin mita biyu ga Saint.
