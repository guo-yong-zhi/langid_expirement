
Antoine Gakeme  ɗan wasan tseren matsakaicin zango ne ɗan ƙasar Burundi wanda ya fi yin gasa a cikin mita 800.  Ya wakilci kasar Burundi a gasar cin kofin duniya a shekarar 2013 kuma ya kasance dan wasan karshe a gasar cin kofin Afrika ta 2014.
Gakeme ya wakilci Burundi a tseren mita 800 a gasar cin kofin duniya ta 2013; ya kafa mafi kyawun sa a duka wasannin zafi  da kuma na kusa da na karshe , amma ya kasa samun damar zuwa wasan karshe. 1   2   A gasar cin kofin Afrika na 2014 a Marrakech ya zo na bakwai.  2  3  A shekarar 2015, Gakeme ya lashe tseren mita 800 a gasar cin kofin zakarun kulob-kulob na Turai, inda ya kafa tarihin gasar 1:45.77; shi ma yana cikin tawagar wasan gudun mita 4×400 na Playas de Castellón, wanda ya yi nasara a 3:07.92.
