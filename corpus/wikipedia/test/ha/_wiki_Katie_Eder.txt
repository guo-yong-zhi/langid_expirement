An haifi Eder kuma ta girma a Milwaukee, Wisconsin .  Katie ta kammala karatu a makarantar sakandare ta Shorewood a shekara ta 2018 kuma za ta halarci jami’ar Stanford a daminar shekara ta 2020.  Ita ce ƙarama a cikin yara biyar. 
Lokacin da Katie take da shekaru 13, ta kafa wata ƙungiya mai zaman kanta, Kids Tatsuniyoyi, don kawo bita na, kuma wanda yara ke koyarwa, ga yara waɗanda basu da damar yin rubutu a waje da makaranta.   A yayin taron karawa juna sani na Yammacin yara, yara suna rubuta wani gajeren labari wanda aka buga shi a wani littafin tarihi, littafi na gaske.   Yara dari da goma sha biyar a cikin kasashe tara sun halarci bitar yara ta yara.   Tatsuniyoyin yara sun haɗu da sama da malamai matasa 400 kuma sun wallafa anthologies 90.  
Bayan taron Maris na Rayuwarmu na 2018 ya ƙare a ranar 24 ga watan Maris, Katie da sauran ɗaliban makarantar sakandarenta suka shirya tafiyar mil 50 daga Madison, WI zuwa Janesville, WI, garin tsohon Shugaban Majalisar Wakilan Amurka Paul Ryan, don kiran shi saboda rawar da ya taka wajen toshewa da binne dokar bindiga.   Wannan 50 Miles More Maris ya jagoranci Katie da tawagarsa don ƙaddamar da kamfen a duk faɗin ƙasar da ake kira # 50more a cikin # 50states don ƙalubalantar sauran jihohin 49 su riƙe 50 Mile Marches zuwa garinsu ko ofishin ɗaya daga cikin zaɓaɓɓun jami'an NRA da suke marawa baya don neman su ɗauka aiki don kawo karshen tashin hankalin bindiga.   50 Miles didari ya yi tafiyar mil 50 a Massachusetts a watan Agusta 2018. 50 Miles More kuma ya ƙaddamar da wani yunƙuri na haɗakar da matasa za su yi don ƙaddamar da waɗannan sabbin mahaɗan don sa su zuwa rumfunan zaɓe a cikin zaɓen rabin matsakaici na 2018 .  
Katie ta jagoranci Miles 50 don kulla kawance tare da sauran kungiyoyin da matasa ke jagoranta a duk fadin kasar don kafa Kungiyar Hadin kai ta nan gaba, cibiyar sadarwar kasa da al'umma ga matasa da kungiyoyin da matasa ke jagoranta da nufin sanya makoma ta zama mafi kyau, mai aminci, da ƙari wuri kawai ga kowa.   Hadin gwiwar nan gaba ya hada kungiyoyin da matasa ke jagoranta da shugabannin matasa a duk fadin Amurka don raba albarkatu da dabaru.   Hadin gwiwar Future da aka ƙaddamar a watan Satumba na shekara ta 2018 tare da yakin neman zaɓen Walkout zuwa Vuri'a. Sama da makarantu 500 a duk fadin kasar sun fita daga aji suka yi maci zuwa rumfunan zabe.
