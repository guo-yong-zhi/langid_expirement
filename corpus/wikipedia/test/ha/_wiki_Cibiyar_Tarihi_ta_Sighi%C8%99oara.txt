Cibiyar Tarihi ta Sighișoara  tsohuwar cibiyar tarihi ce ta garin Sighișoara , Romania, wanda mazauna Saxon suka gina a karni na 12. Wani katafaren kagara ne wanda, a cikin 1999, aka sanya shi Wurin Tarihin Duniya na UNESCO don shedarta na shekaru 850 ga tarihi da al'adun Saxons na Transylvanian.
Wurin Haihuwar Vlad III the Impaler , Sighișoara mai masaukin baki, kowace shekara, bikin na da, inda zane-zane da fasaha ke haɗuwa da kiɗan dutse da wasan kwaikwayo. Garin yana nuna kan iyakar ƙasar Sachsen. Kamar manyan ’yan’uwansa, Sibiu  da Braşov , Sighișoara yana baje kolin gine-gine da al'adun Jamus na Tsakiyar Tsakiya waɗanda aka kiyaye su ko da a lokacin Kwaminisanci.Hasumiyar Tinsmiths
