Kanigowo  is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Bodzanów, within Płock County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. 1  It lies approximately 7 kilometres  north-west of Bodzanów, 18 km  east of Płock, and 80 km  north-west of Warsaw.
