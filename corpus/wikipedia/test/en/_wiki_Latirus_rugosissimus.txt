
Clathurella rugosissima Locard, 1897
Latirus rugosissimus is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusc in the family Fasciolariidae, the spindle snails, the tulip snails and their allies. 2 
The length of the shell attains 27.2 mm.
This marine species occurs off Madeira.
