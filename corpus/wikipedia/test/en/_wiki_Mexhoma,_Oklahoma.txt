
Mexhoma is an unincorporated community in Cimarron County, in the U.S. state of Oklahoma. 1   Located at the crossroads of N0020 Rd and E0200 Rd, 2  the town is about 27.3 miles west of the county seat of Boise City, 3  and just one mile east of the New Mexico border. 2 
A post office called Mexhoma was established in 1909, and remained in operation until its closure in 1942. 4  Located near the state line, the community's name is a portmanteau of New Mexico and Oklahoma. 5
