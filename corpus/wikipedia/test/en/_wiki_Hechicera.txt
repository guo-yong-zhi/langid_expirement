"Hechicera"  is the second radio single and the first track from Maná's fifth studio album, Sueños Líquidos in 1997. On the week of December 13, 1997, the song debuted and lasted only one week at the number thirty-six spot on the U.S. Billboard Hot Latin Tracks. 1  It was nominated for a Lo Nuestro Award for Video of the Year. 2
