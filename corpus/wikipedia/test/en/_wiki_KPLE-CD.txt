KPLE-CD, virtual channel 30 , is a low-powered, Class A TCT-affiliated 1  television station licensed to Killeen, Texas, United States. The station is owned by the Killeen Christian Broadcasting Corporation.
The station's transmitter is located on Old Florence Road in downtown Killeen.
