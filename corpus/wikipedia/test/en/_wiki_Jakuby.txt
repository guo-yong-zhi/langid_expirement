Jakuby   is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Biała Piska, within Pisz County, Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. 1  It lies approximately 12 kilometres  south of Biała Piska, 18 km  south-east of Pisz, and 104 km  east of the regional capital Olsztyn.
