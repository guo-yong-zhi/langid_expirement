Tawment  d tawsit n tzumbiyin tettemɣay-d deg tamiwin timnummsanin n wezgen agafan n tegnit. Tawsit-a teṭṭafar tawacult n taydiwin. Tezmer ad tili tukza  fell-asent tarrayt s wacu i ttenṭaṭen isegnan ɣef tadra, s talɣiwin-is yemgaraden ɣef tumert yakk d ikawsaren-is ibedden, ibeṭṭun mi ara ad wwen.
Tawment tga d amur seg twacult n taydiwin, yakk d wadu-twacult n tewmawin. Dɣa idgel d tsugiwin d isekla i d-yettilin s waṭas i tewment.
Asesmel n telmas s agensu n tewsit yegguni s talɣa tamensayt ɣef tisnalɣiwin n tikawsarin tiwetmin. Dɣa ufraren-tt-d 10 n tgezmiyin. Maca sin akin, tizrawin tijiniyin sseṭren-tt-d uṭṭun ɣer 9 n tgezmiyin. S usegrew n tgezmiyin n Grandis d Oiamel. Akken i d-yella usentem d akken yakk tilmas tiẓaranin n Temrikt n ugafa ṭṭafarent yiwen n uynazɣer.
