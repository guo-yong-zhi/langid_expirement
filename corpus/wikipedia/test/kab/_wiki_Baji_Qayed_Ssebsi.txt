Baji Qayed Ssebsi , d aselway n Tegduda n Tunes. D argaz n tsertit yerna d abugaṭu atunsi. Deg wass n 27 fuṛaṛ 2011, aselway Fuad Mebazaɛ yerra-t d Aneɣlaf Amezwaru n Tunes deg wemkan n Muḥemmed Ɣennuci. Sakkin, yesnulfa akabar Nida Tunes deg 2012, i yernan aṭas n isutal deg Tifranin Tisnagalanin n 2014 deg Tunes .
