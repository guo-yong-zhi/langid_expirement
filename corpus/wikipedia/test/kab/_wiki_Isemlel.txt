Isemlel  d tawsit n tmedkin d yimɣan isaɣuren yeṭṭafaren tawacult n tnarafin. Imahilen ineggura deg tennagrawt ssrusayen tawsit-a di twacult n tduksiwin. Isemlalen n wesɣar d timedkin ilan  ijeǧǧigen mellulen neɣ s yini afraran, sin akin ttuɣalen d timeqqunin meẓẓiyen n tgemmutin zeggaɣen neɣ zegzawen neɣ berriken. Dɣa tigemmutin-a tettentent yigṭaṭ, akken i ttekkayen deg usnezwi n yifsan n yimɣan-a.
