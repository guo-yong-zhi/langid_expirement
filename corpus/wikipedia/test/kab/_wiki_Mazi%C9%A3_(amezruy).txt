Maziɣ u kenɛan u yeqcan , nnan-d fella-s daɣen d berr neɣ yesɛa gma-s i waumi qqaren berr , d ababat amezzwaru i yessulin aẓar n yimaziɣen , yesɛa-d sin n warrac Madɣis d Brins , kra nnan-d belli Brins d mmi-s n gma-s Berr 
Ṭṭabari yenn-d belli iumaziɣen d arraw n Maziɣ u yeqcan u yebrahim  . Ma d Ben Xeldun yewwi-d awal n ugerram Ugesṭin i yuran : " ma testeqsam ifellaḥen-nneɣ ad ak d--inin nekkni d ikenɛaniyen " imi d arraw n Maziɣ u Kenɛan  . amussnaw n umezruy Gazbriyal kambs yenna-d belli d Maziɣ i d baba-s n yimaziɣen
