Masteɛbar yella d agellid n Numidya n umalui . D mmi-s n ugellid Guda , anembaḍ n Numidya , d gma-s n Himsal II , anembaḍ n Numidya n utaram . Neẓra s tallit-is s kra n tira it-id-ibedren . Iban-d belli Guda , mi yemmut deg 88 ST , yefreq tagelda-s gar sin n warraw-is , Himsal yuwi aḥric meqqren ma d Masteɛbar yuwi win meẓẓiyen .
Deg 82 St , yeddukel ujiniral arumani Dumityus , d ugellid anumidi iwumi qqaren Hyarabas , iban belli yekkes leḥkem seg yifassen n dderya n Guda . Ttwaɣeḍlen sin agi sɣur Bumbey , Himsal yuɣal ɣer unbaḍ-is ma d Masteɛbar yettwabeddel s Masensen . Wagi d mmi-s . Ayen texdem Rum yemmal-d asteɛref-ines s unbaḍ n Masteɛber ɣef tgelda-s .
Mmi-s n mmi-s n Masteɛbar , Ɛerbayun , yella d agellid ilelli aneggaru ɣef Numidya deg 42 ST.
