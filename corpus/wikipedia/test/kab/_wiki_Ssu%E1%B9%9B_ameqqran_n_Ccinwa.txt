Ssuṛ ameqqran n Ccinwa , d leḥṣin bnan-t s amenkad amezwaru n Ccunwa Qin Shi Huang deg 220 uqbel ɛisa, ssuṛ-agi yezga-d deg agafa n Ccinwa ed wenẓul n Mungulya, taḍrist tɣezzifent n ssuṛ-nni i tella gar 1368 ed 1644 akked ahat n 8 850 km n teɣzi. Ssuṛ ameqqran bnan-t em anagraw n aḥuddu mgal yizdemen n ugafa 1 , 2 .
Deg 1987, Ssuṛ ameqqran n Ccinwa yekcem ɣar wumuɣ n Tigemmi tamaḍlant n UNESCO 2 , ed yettwaxtar-d em yiwet seg Sa n leεǧubat timaynutin n umaḍal 3 .
