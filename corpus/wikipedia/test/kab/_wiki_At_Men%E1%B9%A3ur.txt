At Menṣur  d yiwet taɣiwant n Tmurt Leqbayel deg twilayt n Tubiret. Tezga-d ar 46 km seg weɣrem n Tubiret ed 101 km seg weɣrem n Bgayet. 
At Menṣur d taɣiwant icudden ɣer tɣiwant n Ccerfa, Imceddalen, Aḥnif d Raffur . Ur teggug ara daɣen seg tiɣwanin tiqbayliyin n Burǧ Bu Aɛririǧ d twilayt n Bgayet.
Llan 4 tuddar deg At Menṣur : 
Ager  igerrez deg wakal n At Menṣur. Llan daɣen aṭas n azara  i seqdacen.
