Igezennayen d yiwet n tqebilt di yeffus  n Arif.
Imdanen-ines qqaren-asen Igezennayen, nutni ssawalen  s tutlayt Tamaziɣt.
Tuddar imeqqranen di tmurt n igezennayen d : Icaweyyen ,Ajdir, Tizi Wesli, Bured,Ineḥnaḥen, Tala Tazeggwaɣt. 
Igezennayen mutyen  aṭas ɣer Turuft d timdinin n Merruk am Taza, Fas , Ameknas , Rbaṭ ... Taza d tamdint deg illan aṭas n Igezennayen.
Igzennay tella deg wammas n "userdas n tlelli" neɣ Armée de libération s trumit. Irumiyen semman tamawin n Aknul, Tizi Wesli , Bured"  s "Akraḍan n Tmeṭṭant" neɣ s tefransist triangle de la mort.
