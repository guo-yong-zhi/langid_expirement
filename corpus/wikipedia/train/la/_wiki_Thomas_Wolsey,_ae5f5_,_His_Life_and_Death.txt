Thomas Wolsey, Late Cardinal, His Life and Death, orthographia auctoris Thomas Wolsey, Late Cardinall, his Lyffe and Deathe, est opus biographicum Anglice ante annum 1558 perfectum a Georgio Cavendish qui fuerat minister Wolseii inter annos 1522 et 1530. Haec biographia utilissima est quibus regnum Henrici VIII intellegere velint divortiumque Catharinae Aragonensis et res politicas inter annos fere 1522 et 1530 gestas.
