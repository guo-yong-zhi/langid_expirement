Dylan Marlais Thomas  era un poeta gallese cuje obras include le poemas "Do Not Go Gentle Into That Good Night"  e "And Death Shall Have No Dominion" ; le "ludo pro voces" Under Milk Wood; e contos e radiodiffusiones como "A Child's Christmas in Wales"  e Portrait of the Artist as a Young Dog .
Su vidua era Caitlin Macnamara . Illes habeva duo filios, Llewelyn Edouard Thomas  e Colm Garan Hart Thomas , e un filia,
Aeronwy Bryn Thomas .
