H, N, Inertinės dujos
Ag, As, Bi, Cd, Cu, Ga, Hg, In, Pb, S, Sb, Se, Te, Tl, Zn
Al, B, Ba, Be, Br, Ca, Cl, Cr, Cs, F, I, Hf, K, Li, Mg, Mn, Na, Nb, O, Rb, Sc, Si, Sr, Ta, Th, Ti, U, V, Y, Zr, W, Lantanoidai
Au, C, Co, Fe, Ge, Ir, Mo, Ni, Os, P, Pd, Pt, Re, Rh, Ru, Sn
