Hoshioki Station  is a railway station in Teine-ku, Sapporo, Hokkaidō, Japan, operated by Hokkaido Railway Company .The station is numbered S09. 1 
Hoshioki Station is served by the Hakodate Main Line.
The station consists of two ground-level opposed side platforms serving two tracks, with the station situated above the tracks. The station has automated ticket machines, automated turnstiles which accept Kitaca, 2  and a "Midori no Madoguchi" staffed ticket office.
