Chomranice  is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Chełmiec, within Nowy Sącz County, Lesser Poland Voivodeship, in southern Poland. It lies approximately 8 kilometres  north-west of Chełmiec, 10 km  north-west of Nowy Sącz, and 64 km  south-east of the regional capital Kraków. 1 
The village has a population of 931.
