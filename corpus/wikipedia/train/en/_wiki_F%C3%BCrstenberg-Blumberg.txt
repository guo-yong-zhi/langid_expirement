Fürstenberg-Blumberg was a County located in Blumberg, southern Baden-Württemberg, Germany. It was created as a partition of Fürstenberg-Baar in 1559. It suffered two known partitions: between itself and Fürstenberg-Möhringen in 1599, and between Fürstenberg-Messkirch and Fürstenberg-Stühlingen in 1614. During  some branches merged with the Baltic Blumberg’s mainly in Courland.
