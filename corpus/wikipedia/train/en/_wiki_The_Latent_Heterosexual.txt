The Latent Heterosexual is a play by Paddy Chayefsky. 1  The author would not permit it to be performed on Broadway, 2  but it opened at the Dallas Theater Center in March 1968, 1  directed by Burgess Meredith, with a cast that included Zero Mostel and Jules Munshin. 3
