
Nobuo Aoyagi   was a Japanese film director and film producer. Born in Kanagawa Prefecture, he attended Meiji University but left before graduating. 1  He worked at the Zenshinza theater troupe before joining the Toho studio in 1937. 1  He debuted as a director in 1940 and was known for a series of live action films starring the manga character Sazae-san. 1  He also helped produce the Mito Komon series on television. 1
