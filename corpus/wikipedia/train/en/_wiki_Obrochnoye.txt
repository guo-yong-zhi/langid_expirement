Obrochnoye  is a rural locality  in Mikhaylovskoye Rural Settlement, Kharovsky District, Vologda Oblast, Russia. The population was 11 as of 2002. 2 
Obrochnoye is located 22 km east of Kharovsk  by road. Verkhnyaya Gorka is the nearest rural locality. 3
