
The F Street Bridge in Salida, Colorado is a closed spandrel concrete arch bridge built in 1907 by the Pueblo Bridge Company.  The design also known as a Luten Arch bridge was patented in 1907 by Daniel Luten. The bridge has two spans each with length of 60 feet , and 128 feet  in total. 2   It is believed to be the first bridge of this type built by the company.
It spans the Arkansas River in a park setting. 2
