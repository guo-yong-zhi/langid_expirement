
Dragoljub Jeremić  was a Serbian footballer who played as a defender.
Jeremić spent most of his career at Partizan, making 59 league appearances and scoring one goal. 1  He also spent some time on loan to Radnički Kragujevac, Panserraikos , and Rabotnički . After leaving Partizan, Jeremić briefly played for Bežanija, before retiring.
At international level, Jeremić made four  appearances for FR Yugoslavia at the Millennium Super Soccer Cup hosted by India in January 2001. 2 
Jeremić died on 12 March 2022, aged 43. 3
