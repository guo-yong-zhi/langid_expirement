
Subhonil Ghosh  in Kolkata, West Bengal, is an Indian professional footballer who played as a midfielder for Garhwal FC on loan from East Bengal. 1 
Subhonil started his career with the youth academy of East Bengal F.C. in 2017 and played for the East Bengal F.C. U18 team in the U18 Elite League. citation needed 
In 2019, Subhonil was promoted to the senior side by Alejandro Menendez and made his debut against George Telegraph S.C. in the 2019-20 Calcutta Premier Division League.
He was included in the squad for the 2019-20 I-League. 2  On 31 January 2020, he was released by the club. citation needed
