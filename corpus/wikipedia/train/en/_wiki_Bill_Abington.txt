William Henry Abington  was an American politician. He served in the Texas House of Representatives, representing Tarrant County, from 1949 to 1953. He was a Democrat. 1  He was born in Childress, Texas and died at Austin, Texas. 2  Abington was also active in the oil industry, serving as president of Texas Oil & Gas Association.
