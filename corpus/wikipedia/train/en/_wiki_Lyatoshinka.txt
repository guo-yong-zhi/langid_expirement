Lyatoshinka  is a rural locality  and the administrative center of Lyatoshinskoye Rural Settlement, Staropoltavsky District, Volgograd Oblast, Russia. The population was 632 as of 2010. 2  There are 11 streets. 3 
Lyatoshinka is located on the left bank of the Yeruslan River, 21 km northeast of Staraya Poltavka  by road. Lugovskoye is the nearest rural locality. 4
