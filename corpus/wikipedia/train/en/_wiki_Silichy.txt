Silichy or Silichi  is a ski resort situated in Lahojsk , Belarus, located 30 km  away from Minsk. Silichy was opened on January 29, 2005. The length of 3 routes for skiing is 2.5 km , and it has a 4-chair elevator. 
The mountain skiing season starts in December and lasts until March . 1 
This resort is open all year round, with other activities than skiing. 2
