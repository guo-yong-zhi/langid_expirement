Sieraków  is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Przyrów, within Częstochowa County, Silesian Voivodeship, in southern Poland. 1  It lies approximately 6 kilometres  south of Przyrów, 28 km  east of Częstochowa, and 66 km  north-east of the regional capital Katowice.
The village has a population of 242.
