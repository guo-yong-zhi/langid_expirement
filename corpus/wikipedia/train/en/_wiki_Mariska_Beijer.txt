
Mariska Beijer is a Dutch wheelchair basketball player.
Beijer competed at the Paralympic Games where she won a gold medal at the 2020 games and bronze medals at the 2012 and 2016 games. 1 
She has been part of the Netherlands team at the Wheelchair Basketball World Championships where they came 3rd at the 2014 Championship 1  and 1st at the 2018 2  and 2022 Championships. 3  She participated in the European Wheelchair Basketball Championship where the Netherlands team came first in 2013, 2017, 1  2019 4  and 2021 5  and 2nd in 2009, 2011 and 2015. She was also the lead scorer in 2015, 1  2017 6  and 2019. 4 
She was shortlisted for the 2019 Team Sportswoman of the Year Award. 4
