Roberto Strauss  is a Mexican former swimmer who competed in the 1972 Summer Olympics. 1   At the 1969 Maccabiah Games in Israel he won a gold medal in freestyle, and at the 1973 Maccabiah Games he won three bronze medals in freestyle, at 100, 400, and 800 meters. 2  3 
Strauss attended the University of Miami on a swimming scholarship, and later opened a swim gym in South Florida. 4
