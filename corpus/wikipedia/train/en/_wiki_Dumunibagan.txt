
Dumunibagan, commonly known as Doomni and Dumuni, 1  is a census village in Baksa district , Assam, India. citation needed  The Dumni Tea Estate, tea cultivation area, is located in the village. 2  3 
As per the 2011 Census of India, Dumunibagan village has a population of 6,855 people, including 3,412 males and 3,443 females with a literacy rate of 80.29%e. 4
