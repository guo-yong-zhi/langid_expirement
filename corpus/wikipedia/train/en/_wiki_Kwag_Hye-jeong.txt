Kwag Hye-Jeong  is a South Korean handball player who competed in the 1996 Summer Olympics. In 1996 she was part of the South Korean team which won the silver medal. 1  2  She also participated in the winning South Korean team in the 1995 World Women's Handball Championship. 3
