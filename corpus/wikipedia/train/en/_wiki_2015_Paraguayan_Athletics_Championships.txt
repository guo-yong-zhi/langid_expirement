The 2015 Campeonato de la Victoria or 2015 Torneo de la Victoria was held at the Secretaria Nacional de Deportes in Asunción, organized by Federación Paraguaya de Atletismo. It was the 65th edition. 1 
The competition serves as the Paraguayan Athletics Championships in track and field for the Republic of Paraguay, being the country's most important national athletics competitions. 2  3 
Paraguay Marathon Club was crowned champion of the championships, where it achieved a score of 139. 4  Larson Giovanni Diaz Martinez and María Caballero were the best athletes of the championships. 5
