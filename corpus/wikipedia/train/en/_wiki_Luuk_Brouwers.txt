
Luuk Brouwers  is a Dutch professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Eredivisie club Utrecht. 1 
Brouwers made his professional debut in the Eerste Divisie for FC Den Bosch on 10 April 2015 in a game against Jong PSV. 2 
On 5 February 2020, Brouwers signed a three-year contract effective from 30 June 2020 with Go Ahead Eagles. 3 
On 23 March 2022, Brouwers agreed to join Utrecht on a five-year contract, beginning in July 2022. 4
