
Justice T. K. Tukol  was known for his scholarly work on Jainism, education and judiciary. He was the judge of High court of Mysore. He headed the Mysore pay commission . 1  He also served as the vice-chancellor of the Bangalore University. His contribution to judiciary and books on Jainism , Yoga, Meditation and Mysticism in Jainism, Translation of Saman Suttam  are remarkable.
Tukol was born in Gudur village of Hungund Taluka, Bagalkote district. He was a student of Karnatak College Dharwar and Fergusson College, Pune.
