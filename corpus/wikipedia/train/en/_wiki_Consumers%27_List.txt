The Consumers' List  was a political party in Italy. The party was founded in 2004 by Carlo Rienzi, President of the Codacons association .
In the 2004 EP election the party got only 0.5% of the vote, without gaining any seats.
In the 2006 general election the party was affiliated to The Union , the centre-left coalition led by Romano Prodi. It got 0.2% of the vote, but thanks to its result in Calabria  1  it gained one seat in the Senate .
