Çamlıca  is a village in the Adıyaman District, Adıyaman Province, Turkey. 1  The village is populated by Kurds of the Kawan tribe and had a population of 210 in 2021. 2  3 
The hamlets of Alıheri, Bahçe, Çiftlice and Gedik are attached to the village. 1
