The Oppidum de Verduron is an oppidum in the 15th arrondissement of Marseille, Southern France. It was built in the Iron Age. 1  It was discovered in 1906, but not fully excavated until 1911. 1  It has been listed as a historical monument  since 24 August 2004. 1
