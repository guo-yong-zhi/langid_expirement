Minarets is a former settlement in Madera County, California. 1  It was located 5 miles  southeast of North Fork. 1 
A post office operated at Minarets from 1925 to 1933. 1  Minarets was the terminus of the Minarets and Western Railroad. 1 
The name Minarets was applied to a geologic feature in the county; see Minarets . The name was applied to a city planned as the county seat for the then newly established Madera County located 15 miles  east of Madera. 1
