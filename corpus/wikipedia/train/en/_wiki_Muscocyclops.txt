
Muscocyclops is a genus of copepod crustaceans in the family Cyclopidae, comprising three species 1  found only in South America. 2  Two of the species – Muscocyclops bidentatus Reid, 1987 and Muscocyclops therasiae Reid, 1987 – are endemic to the Distrito Federal in Brazil, and are listed as conservation dependent on the IUCN Red List. 3  4  The third species is Muscocyclops operculatus . 1
