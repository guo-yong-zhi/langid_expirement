Savikovo  is a rural locality  in Krasnooktyabrskoye Rural Settlement, Gus-Khrustalny District, Vladimir Oblast, Russia. The population was 11 in 2010. 2 
Savikovo is located on the Sentur River, 41 km south of Gus-Khrustalny  by road. Aksyonovo is the nearest rural locality. 3
