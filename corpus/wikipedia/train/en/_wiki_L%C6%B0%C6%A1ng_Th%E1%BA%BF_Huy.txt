Lương Thế Huy  is a Vietnamese jurist and LGBT rights activist.
He study laws at Ho Chi Minh City University of Law 1  and University of California, Los Angeles. 2 
He has been doing LGBT-related social work since 2008. 1  He was listed as one of Forbes 30 Under 30 in Vietnam in 2016 3  and "Asia 21 Young Leaders" of Asia Society in 2018. 1  In 2019, he became director of Institute for Studies of Society, Economy and Environment . In May 2021, he contested the Vietnam's National Assembly elections, became first openly LGBT candidate. 4  He was not elected despite being one of the most prominent independent candidates for Hanoi city. 5
