Łączki  is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Łyse, within Ostrołęka County, Masovian Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland. 1  It lies approximately 12 kilometres  north of Łyse, 45 km  north of Ostrołęka, and 144 km  north of Warsaw.
