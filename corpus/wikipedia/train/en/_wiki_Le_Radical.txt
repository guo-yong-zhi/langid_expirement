Le Radical  was a French language conservative daily newspaper published from Port Louis, Mauritius. 1  The newspaper was founded in 1898 by F. L. Morel, who served as its editor-in-chief during its initial period. 1  2  Le Radical was mainly dedicated to politics. It contained some articles in English. In 1909 a second daily edition was launched. During this period it had a circulation of around 2,000, out of which a large share were sold at the Port Louis central train station. 2
