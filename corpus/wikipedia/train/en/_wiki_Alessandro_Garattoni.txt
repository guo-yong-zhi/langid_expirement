Alessandro Garattoni  is an Italian footballer who plays as a defender for Serie C Group C club Foggia.
He made his Serie C debut for Imolese on 18 September 2018 in a game against AlbinoLeffe. 1 
On 3 October 2020, he joined Juve Stabia. 2 
On 30 July 2021, he signed a two-year contract with Foggia. 3
