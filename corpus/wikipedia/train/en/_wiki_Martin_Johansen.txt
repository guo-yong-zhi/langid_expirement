
Martin Bro Johansen  is a Danish former football  player. He played usually as a midfielder but could also play as a forward. During his career he played for FC Copenhagen, B 1903 and most notably Coventry City in the FA Premier League.
Johansen appeared once for the Danish national team in his career. 1  His twin brother Michael Johansen was also a professional footballer.
