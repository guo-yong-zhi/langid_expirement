
Nix on Dames is a 1929 American pre-Code comedy film directed by Donald Gallaher and written by Maude Fulton and Frank Gay. The film stars Mae Clarke, Robert Ames, William Harrigan, Maude Fulton, George MacFarlane and Frederick H. Graham. The film was released on November 24, 1929, by Fox Film Corporation. 1  2  3
