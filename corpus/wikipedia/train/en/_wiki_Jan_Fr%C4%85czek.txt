Jan Frączek  is a former Polish slalom canoeist who competed in the 1970s.
He won three medals in the C-2 team event at the ICF Canoe Slalom World Championships with a gold  and two bronzes .
Frączek finished fifth in the C-2 event at the 1972 Summer Olympics in Munich.
