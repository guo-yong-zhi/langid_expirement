Menace to Society is the second studio album by Los Angeles metal band Lizzy Borden.
Released in 1986 by Metal Blade Records, 3  this would become a success for the band appearing on the Billboard 200 for 10 weeks and peaking at 144. 4  The album was re-released in 2002 with 4 bonus tracks, and again on vinyl in 2018. 5 
All tracks are written by Gene Allen, Lizzy Borden, Mike Davis, Joey Scott Harges, Alex Nelson, except where noted
