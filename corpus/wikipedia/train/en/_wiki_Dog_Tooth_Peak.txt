Dog Tooth Peak ) is located in the southern Wind River Range in the U.S. state of Wyoming. 3  Dog Tooth Peak sits along the Continental Divide, less than 1 mi  northwest of Big Sandy Mountain.
Encountering bears is a concern in the Wind River Range. 4  There are other concerns as well, including bugs, wildfires, adverse snow conditions and nighttime cold temperatures. 5 
Importantly, there have been notable incidents, including accidental deaths, due to falls from steep cliffs  and due to falling rocks, over the years, including 1993, 6  2007 , 7  2015, 8  and 2018. 9  Other incidents include a seriously injured backpacker being airlifted near SquareTop Mountain 10  in 2005, 11  and a fatal hiker incident  in 2006 that involved state search and rescue. 12  The U.S. Forest Service does not offer updated aggregated records on the official number of fatalities in the Wind River Range.
