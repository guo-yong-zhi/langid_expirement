Moskvino  is a rural locality  in Kisnemskoye Rural Settlement, Vashkinsky District, Vologda Oblast, Russia. The population was 39 as of 2002. 2 
Moskvino is located 26 km northwest of Lipin Bor  by road. Istomino is the nearest rural locality. 3
