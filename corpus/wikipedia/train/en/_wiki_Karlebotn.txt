Karlebotn  is a village in Nesseby Municipality in Troms og Finnmark county, Norway.  The village lies on the southern shore of the inner part of the Varangerfjorden, about 8 kilometres  southeast of the municipal centre of Varangerbotn.  The statistical area of Karlebotn, which also includes the peripheral parts of the village, as well as the surrounding countryside, has a population of 93. 2  3
