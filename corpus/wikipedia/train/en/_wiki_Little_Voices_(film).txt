
Little Voices is a 2011 short student film that was directed and written by Christopher McFall. 1  The film was released on 23 March 2011 and stars David Bower as a deaf man struggling to cope with losing his partner. 2  Filming took place during August 2010. 3 
Quint  is a deaf-mute who has recently lost the love of his life. The death sends Quint reeling and he finds himself incapable of coping with this reality. The film spans a day in Quint's life and shows his obsession with typography, as he imagines people speaking in subtitles.
