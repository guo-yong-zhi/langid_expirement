Gogolin  is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Sadkowice, within Rawa County, Łódź Voivodeship, in central Poland. 1  It lies approximately 3 kilometres  east of Sadkowice, 22 km  east of Rawa Mazowiecka, and 76 km  east of the regional capital Łódź.
