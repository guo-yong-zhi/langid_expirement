Pleshkovo  is a rural locality  and the administrative center of Pleshkovsky Selsoviet, Zonalny District, Altai Krai, Russia. The population was 1,280 as of 2013. 2  There are 14 streets.
Pleshkovo is located 23 km northwest of Zonalnoye  by road. Bulanikha is the nearest rural locality. 3
