Henny Thijssen  is a Dutch singer-composer. clarification needed  He is the elder brother of singers Wilma and Reiny Landkroon.
In 1988, Thijssen received 1st place public jury and the 3rd place specialized jury with Save Our Planet in the World Songfestival in Bratislava, Slovakia. In 1998, he published Tastbaar and, in 2008, he published Levensecht.
His last singles were Tabbe Tabbe Tab , Die Nacht is mijn Leven  and Dans nog een keer met mij  what was a nice success in the Netherlands Charts. Thijssen writes songs for many Dutch artists. 1
