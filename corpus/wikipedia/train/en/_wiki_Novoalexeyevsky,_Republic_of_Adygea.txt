Novoalexeyevsky  is a rural locality  in Dmitriyevskoye Rural Settlement of Koshekhablsky District, Adygea, Russia. The population was 325 as of 2018. 2  There are 7 streets.
Novoalexeyevsky is located 13 km northwest of Koshekhabl  by road. Plodopitomnik is the nearest rural locality. 3
