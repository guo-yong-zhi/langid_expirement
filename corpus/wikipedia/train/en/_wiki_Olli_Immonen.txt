Olli Immonen  is a Finnish politician who served as member of the Finnish Parliament for the Finns Party from 2011 to 2023. 1  He is also the former chairman of the nationalist organization Suomen Sisu. 2 
Immonen was born in Nivala, and received national attention in July 2015 after writing a controversial anti-multicultural text on his Facebook page. 3
