Cave of the Bells is located in Sawmill Canyon on the eastern slopes of the Santa Rita Mountains of Santa Cruz County, Arizona.  The cave contains a lake which is 80 meters below the entrance level.  The lake has been measured at 76 °F  and is believed to be warmed by sources below. The nearby Onyx Cave is also of interest. 1 
Access to the cave is by reservation and a key security deposit. Permits and keys are accessible at the Forest Supervisor's Office.
