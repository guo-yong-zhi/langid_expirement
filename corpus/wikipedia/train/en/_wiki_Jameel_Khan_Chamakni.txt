Jameel Khan Chamakni is a Pakistani politician who is member-elect of the Provincial Assembly of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa.
Chamakni contested 2019 Khyber Pakhtunkhwa provincial election on 20 July 2019 from constituency PK-108  as an independent. He won the election by the majority of 429 votes over the runner up Muhammad Riaz Bangash of Jamiat Ulema-e-Islam . He garnered 8,075 votes while Bangash received 7,646 votes. 1
