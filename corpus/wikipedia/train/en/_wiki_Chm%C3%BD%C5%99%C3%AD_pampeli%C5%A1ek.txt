"Chmýří pampelišek" is a song by the Czech pop music group Slza. 1  It was released as a music video on 29 April 2018, but originally appeared on their second album Holomráz . 2  3  The music was composed by Lukáš Bundil and Dalibor Cidlinský Jr. and the lyrics were written by Xindl X. 4 
A music video was also shot on this song. 5  The video clip includes footage from the Holomráz Tour 2018 . 6  The video was shot and edited by Leoš Brabec from PrimeTime. 7  8
