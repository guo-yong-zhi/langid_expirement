
St. Aignan's trumpet-eared bat  is a species of vesper bat in the family Vespertilionidae.
It is found only in Papua New Guinea, and is only endemic to the islands, not the mainland. 2  The species has been recorded on the islands of Fergusson, Vanatinai, Woodlark, and Misima. 1 
Its natural habitat is subtropical or tropical dry forests.
