In Greek mythology, Astrabacus  is a Spartan, the son of Irbus . He and his brother Alopecus found a wooden image of the goddess Artemis Orthia under a bush of willows; it was surrounded in such a way that it stood upright. Astrabacus had a hero-shrine in Sparta 1
