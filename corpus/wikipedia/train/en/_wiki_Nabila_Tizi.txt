Nabila Tizi-Sadki  is an Algerian team handball player. She plays for the club Brest Bretagne Handball, and on the Algerian national team. She competed at the 2013 World Women's Handball Championship in Serbia, where Algeria placed 22nd, and Tizi was top scorer for the Algerian team.
