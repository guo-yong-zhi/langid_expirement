Franklin Junior High School is a public middle school in Franklin, Ohio, part of the Franklin City School District. The building was constructed in 1921 for grades 6-12. In 1948 the building became a four-year high school until 1954, when the Hampton Bennett School was opened. It remained a high school  until 1969, when Franklin High School  was built. The building then became Franklin Junior High School housing grades 7 and 8. 1
