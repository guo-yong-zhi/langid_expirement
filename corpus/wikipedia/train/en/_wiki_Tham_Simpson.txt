Tham Simpson  1  is a paralympic athlete from Canada competing mainly in category 1C events. She was born in Vietnam but adopted by Canadian parents in Toronto. 2 
Simpson competed in the 1984 Summer Paralympics in athletics aged just 15, winning five gold medals.
