
The 1952 Baylor Bears football team represented Baylor University in the 1952 college football season. They finished with a 4-4-2 record and placed fifth in the Southwest Conference for the year. Four players – Jack Sisco , Robert Knowles , Bill Athey  and Jerry Coody  – were selected as All-Conference players. 1
