
"Straight Back Down" is a song by Australian singer and songwriter Dean Lewis. The song was released to commercial hit radio in August 2019 as the fourth single from Lewis' debut studio album, A Place We Knew. It was the most added song to radio the following week. 1 
In an album review, Cameron Adams from Herald Sun said "'Straight Back Down' adopts the familiar Vance Joy stance and Lewis gets to showcase his upper register." 2  Island Records said "The climactic chorus of 'Straight Back Down' is tailor made for festivals." 3
