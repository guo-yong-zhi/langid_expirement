
Sukradasa is a 1977 Indian Malayalam film, directed by Anthikkad Mani. The film stars Jayabharathi, Adoor Bhasi, Jose Prakash and Sreelatha Namboothiri in the lead roles. The film has musical score by M. K. Arjunan. 1  2  3 
The music was composed by M. K. Arjunan and the lyrics were written by Mankombu Gopalakrishnan.
