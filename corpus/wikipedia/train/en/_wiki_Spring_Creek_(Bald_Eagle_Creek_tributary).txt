Spring Creek is a 25.2-mile-long  1  tributary of Bald Eagle Creek in Centre County, Pennsylvania in the United States. 2  3 
Spring Creek passes through a water gap in Bald Eagle Mountain and joins Bald Eagle Creek at Milesburg.
The stream is the site of a kayak and canoe slalom training center, located along Sunnyside Boulevard in Bellefonte. Removal of the McCoy & Linn dam in 2007 now permits a Class I whitewater run of about 3 miles  from Bellefonte to Milesburg.
