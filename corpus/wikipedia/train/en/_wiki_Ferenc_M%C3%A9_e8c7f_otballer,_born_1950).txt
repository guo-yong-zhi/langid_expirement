
Ferenc Mészáros  was a Hungarian footballer who played as a goalkeeper. 2  3 
Born in Budapest, Mészáros played club football in Hungary and Portugal for VM Egyetértés, Vasas SC, Sporting CP, Farense, Győri ETO and Vitória Setúbal, 1  4  5 
Mészáros earned a total of 29 caps for Hungary, 1  and represented them at the 1978 and 1982 World Cups. 6
