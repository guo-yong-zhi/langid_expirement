
Can Serdar  is a German-Turkish footballer who plays as a midfielder for Oberliga Niederrhein club FSV Duisburg. 1 
Serdar made his professional debut for Fortuna Köln in the 3. Liga on 25 July 2015, coming on as a substitute in the 74th minute for Julius Biada in the 1–2 away loss against Stuttgarter Kickers. 2
