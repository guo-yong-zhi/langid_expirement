
Natronolimnohabitans innermongolicus is a species of archaea in the family Natrialbaceae. 1  It has been proposed that Haloterrigena turkmenica be reclassified as Natronolimnohabitans innermongolicus due to the genome sequence of Haloterrigena turkmenica being contaminated in a previous study. 2  3
