
Sir Frederick Henry Stewart FRS FRSE FGS  was a Scottish geologist and academic who was a professor at the University of Edinburgh. 1 
Stewart was born in Aberdeen on 16 January 1916, the son of Frederick Robert Stewart, a lecturer in engineering 2  at Aberdeen University, 3  and his wife, Hester Alexander.
He was educated at Fettes College and Robert Gordon's College. 4  He earned a BSc in Zoology at Aberdeen University. He followed this with a doctorate  in Geology at Emmanuel College, Cambridge, while studying the geology of the Isle of Skye and the village of Belhelvie, Aberdeenshire. 2 
In 1945, Stewart married Mary Rainbow,  whom he met whilst working in Durham. She survived him, dying in 2014. There were no children. 3
