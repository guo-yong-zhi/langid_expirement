Magoula  is a suburban town of Athens and former community  of West Attica, Greece. Since the 2011 local government reform, it is part of the municipality Elefsina, of which it is a municipal unit. 2  The municipal unit has an area of 18.134 km2. 3 
Magoula is located 2 km east of Mandra, 5 km north of Elefsina and 21 km northwest of central Athens. The municipal unit of Magoúla also includes the village of Néos Póntos . Magoula is served by a station on the Athens Airport–Patras railway. Motorway 6 runs southeast of the town.
