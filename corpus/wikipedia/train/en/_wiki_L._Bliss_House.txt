The L. Bliss House is a historic house located at 90 West Main Street in Westfield, Chautauqua County, New York.
It is a two-story, wood-framed Italian Villa style dwelling built in 1853. It is believed to have been built for Lorenzo Bliss, a local brewer. 2 
It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places on September 26, 1983. 1
