Timbiqui Airport  is an airport serving Timbiqui, a town and municipality in the Cauca Department of Colombia. The runway angles northwest from the center of the town. The town and airport are on the east bank of the  Timbiqui River, 8 kilometres  inland from the Pacific Ocean.
