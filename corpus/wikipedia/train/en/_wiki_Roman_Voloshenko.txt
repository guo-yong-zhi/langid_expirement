Roman Voloshenko  is a Belarusian-born Russian retired professional ice hockey left winger.
Though born in Brest, Belarus, Voloshenko represented Russia at under-18 and under-20 level. 1  He began his professional career with Krylya Sovetov Moscow in 2001. He was drafted by the Minnesota Wild in the 2004 NHL Entry Draft with the 42nd overall pick in the second round. He then moved to the United States in 2005 and skated for the Wild's AHL farm club the Houston Aeros for two seasons before returning to Russia in 2007.
