
Galeton is an unincorporated community and U.S. Post Office in Weld County, Colorado, United States.  The Galeton Post Office has the ZIP Code 80622. 2 
A post office called Galeton has been in operation since 1910. 3  The community was named for one Miss Gale, the daughter of a local family of settlers. 4 
Galeton is located at 40°31′16″N 104°35′05″W  /  40.52111°N 104.58472°W  / 40.52111; -104.58472 .
