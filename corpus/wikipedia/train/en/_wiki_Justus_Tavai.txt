Justus Tavai  is an American football defensive tackle for the New England Patriots of the National Football League . He was signed by the Patriots as an undrafted free agent after the 2023 NFL Draft. 1  He played college football at El Camino, Hawaii and San Diego State.
He is the younger brother of current Patriots linebacker Jahlani Tavai and defensive end J. R. Tavai. 2 
After going undrafted in the 2023 NFL Draft, Tavai signed with the New England Patriots of the National Football League  on June 5, 2023. 1
