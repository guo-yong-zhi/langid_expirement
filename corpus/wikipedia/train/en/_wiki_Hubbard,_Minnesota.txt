
Hubbard is an unincorporated community and census-designated place  in the western part of Hubbard Township, Hubbard County, Minnesota, United States. It is in southern Hubbard County, along Minnesota State Highway 87, at the outlet of Long Lake. Park Rapids is 8 miles  to the northwest.
Hubbard was first listed as a CDP prior to the 2020 census. 2
