WRYC  is a radio station licensed to serve the community of Frisco City, Alabama. The station is owned by Damon L. Collins, through licensee Blackbelt Broadcasting, Inc. It airs a country music format.
The station was assigned the WRYC call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on November 22, 2016. 1
