Amy Barham Westbrook
Louisiana State University
Erle Edwards Barham  was a Republican member of the Louisiana State Senate. He first won the Senate seat in December 1975 beating L. B. Loftin by just 89 votes. 1 
Barham was a certified flight instructor, and was killed when he struck a perimeter fence while taxiing an airplane on the runway. 2
