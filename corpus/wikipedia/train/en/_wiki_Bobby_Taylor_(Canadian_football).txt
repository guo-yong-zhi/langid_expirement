Bobby Taylor  was a Canadian Football League receiver who played for the Calgary Stampeders, Toronto Argonauts, Hamilton Tiger-Cats, and Edmonton Eskimos.  He attended Clarkson University from 1961-1962.
In his fourteen-year career he caught 521 receptions for a total of 8,203 yards and 50 touchdowns.  Taylor's 56 catches led the CFL in 1968. 1 
He was Toronto's nominee for Most Outstanding Canadian player in 1968 2  and 1969. 3 
Taylor played minor league hockey, as well, from 1962-1970 in the EHL, WHL, AHL, and CPHL. 4
