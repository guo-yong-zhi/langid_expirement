Mt. Horeb Presbyterian Church and Cemetery is a historic Presbyterian church and cemetery located near Elizabethtown, Bladen County, North Carolina.  It was built in 1845, and is a frame Greek Revival-style church with a pedimented front portico added in 1932. 2 
It was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 1987. 1
