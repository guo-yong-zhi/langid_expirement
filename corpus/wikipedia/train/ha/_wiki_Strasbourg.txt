Strasbourg  birnin kasar Faransa ce. A cikin birnin Strasbourg akwai mutane 773,447 a kidayar shekarar 2014. Strasbourg a kan kogin Rin ce, kuma a kan iyaka tsakanin Faransa da Jamus ce. 
A shekaran 2020, garin da ya dace yana da mazauna 290,576 kuma duka Eurométropole de Strasbourg  da Arrondissement na Strasbourg suna da mazauna 511,552. 1  Yankin babban birni na Strasbourg yana da yawan jama'a 853,110 a cikin 2019,  2  wanda ya sa ya zama yanki na takwas mafi girma a cikin Faransa kuma gida ga 14% na mazauna yankin Grand Est. Yankin Eurodistrict Strasbourg-Ortenau yana da yawan jama'a kusan 1,000,000 a cikin 2022. Strasbourg na ɗaya daga cikin manyan manyan biranen Tarayyar Turai guda huɗu ,  3  domin ita ce wurin zama na cibiyoyin Turai da yawa, kamar su.
