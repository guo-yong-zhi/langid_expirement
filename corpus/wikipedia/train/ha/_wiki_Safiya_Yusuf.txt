Safiya Yusuf  
Jaruma ce a masana antar fim ta Hausa wato kanniwud inda ta fito a fim na Tashar arewa 24 Mai suna KWANA CASA IN inda ta fito a suna safarau,u nura dalibai yar makaranta. 1 
safiya Yusuf wacce aka Fi sani da safara,u ta kwana casa,in , jaruma ce a Shirin Tashar arewa 24, tayi suna dalilin fim din ,shi ya fito da ita a idon duniya . Haifaffiyar jihar kano ce an haifeta a ranar 26 ga watan Afirilu shekarar 1999 a cikin garin Kano, tana zaune a gidan  2 iyayen e Yan uwanta kaf suna Kano. Ta fara fim a shekarar 2019 ta fara da fim din Kwana casa,in na Tashar arewa 24, 3
