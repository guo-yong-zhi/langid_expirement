Miguel Kiala , ƙwararren ɗan wasan ƙwallon kwando ne na Angola. Kiala, mai shekaru 204 cm  tsayi kuma yayi nauyi 91 kg , yana wasa azaman Cibiyar. Ya wakilci Angola a gasar FIBA ta Afirka a shekarar 2011. Kiala shine babban mai sake dawowa a Gasar Cin Kofin Duniya na shekarar 2009 U-19 FIBA a New Zealand, tare da matsakaicin 13.6 rpg.
