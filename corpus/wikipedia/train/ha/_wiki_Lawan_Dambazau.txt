Malam Lawan Dambazau  a dambazawa, ƙaramar hukumar ɗan batta dake garin Kano. ya dawo kano wajen ƴan uwan mahaifinsa lokacin yana da shekara biyar kuma a nan ya girma 1 .
Ya fara karatun addini a garin kano, daga baya yayi kaura zuwa Maiduguri don neman ilimin addinin Musulunci.  ya rubuta littafi da yawa kamar su “ A true Northerner Aminu Kano , the struggle for Nepu and PRP , politics and region in Nigeria  2 .
Bayan kammala karatunsa, ya dawo garin Kano inda ya haɗa gangami don wayar da kan mutane akan addini, zamantakewa dakuma siyasa, shi da manyan attajiran lokacinsa kamar su Ibrahim Waziri , Babban mawaƙi Aliyu Aƙilu dakuma Qadi Baffa Mahmud 3 .
Mallam lawan dambazau ya fara hidimar neman ƴanci tun kafin kafa NEPU a shekara ta 1950 sannan bayan kafa NEPU ya zama ɗaya daga cikin ƴan ƙungiyan. A lokacin sabon abu ne ga al’umma don suna tsoron yin gaba da gaba da turawan mulkin mallaka. Mallam Dambazau ya sami cikas da maqiya musamman a Maiduguri inda ya kafa jama’atul islamiyya . Shine sakataren janar na ƙumgiyar na farko, inda ya dsamu horo da azabtarwa matuƙa daga turawa, da maƙiyansa.
Mallam Dambazau  da Abokansa kamar su Aminu Kano  da Mallam Sa’ad Zungur , sunyi yaƙin neman ƴancin ƙasan nan tsakanin .
Mallam lawan mutum ne mara tsoro mai ɗumbin kwarjini da wanda wasu daga cikin shuwagabannin soja lokacin Gen. Olusugun Obasanjo, kan nemi shawara wurinsa kafin neman kujeran mulkin ƙasan 4 .
Mallam Lawal Dambazau ya rasu a safiyar laraba 13th ga watan satimba shkara ta 2000. A asibitin horo na Aminu Kano a garin Kano , bayan yayi jinya na ɗan lokaci. An burne shi misalin ƙarfe goma na safe a maƙabartar “kukar buluƙiya” a bisa tsarin musulunci. Ya rasu ya bar ƴaƴa 8 da mata guda  5 .
