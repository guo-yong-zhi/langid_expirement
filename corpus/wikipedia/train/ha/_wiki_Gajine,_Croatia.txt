Gajine   ƙauye ne a cikin kasar Kuroshiya .
Bisa ga ƙidayar jama'a a shekara ta 2011, Gajine tana da mazauna 116. 
Lura : Daga shekara ta 1857 zuwa shekara ta 1880 bayanai sun kasance a cikin garin Donji Lapac . A cikin shekara ta 2001 wani ɓangare na sasantawa , mai suna Boričevac, ya zama sulhu mai zaman kansa.
