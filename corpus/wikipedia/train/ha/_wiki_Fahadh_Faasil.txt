Fahadh Faasil 1 
An haifi Fahadh Faasil 8 Agusta 1982 ɗan wasan Indiya ne kuma mai shirya fina-finai wanda ya fi yin aiki a sinimar Malayalam  kuma ya fito a cikin fina-finan Tamil  da Telugu  kaɗan. Ya yi fina-finai sama da 50 kuma ya sami lambobin yabo da dama, gami da lambar yabo ta Fina-Finai ta ƙasa.  , Kyautar Fina-Finan Jahar Kerala guda huɗu da lambar yabo ta Filmfare ta Kudu.
