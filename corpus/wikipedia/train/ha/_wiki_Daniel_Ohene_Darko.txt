An haifi Daniel Ohene Darko a ranar 18 ga watan Yunin 1961 kuma ya fito daga Ayanfuri a yankin tsakiyar kasar Ghana. Ya samu shaidar kammala sakandare  a shekarar 1977. Ya kuma samu digirinsa na farko a 1977 da Advanced Level a shekarar 1983. Ya ci gaba da samun digirin digirgir  daga 2001 zuwa 2003, Barrister of Law a 2003 da Executive Master na Business Administration  - Talla a cikin 2011. 3  4  5 
Daniel Ohene Darko ya yi aiki a matsayin Mataimakin Lauya "Kwasi Blay and Associates" a Ghana. Ya sake yin aiki a Amicus Legal Consult  a Ghana. Shi ne Manajan Abokin Hulɗa na Betta Law Consult. A yanzu haka yana aiki a matsayin dan majalisar wakilai mai wakiltar mazabar Upper Denkyira ta yamma a yankin tsakiyar kasar Ghana a kan tikitin jam'iyyar National Democratic Congress. 3  4  5
