Carla Abellan  ɗan wasan polo ne na Sipaniya, wanda ya taka leda a CN Sabadell .
Ta halarci gasar cin kofin mata na Len Super Cup na shekarar 2013, Kofin Super Cup na mata na shekarar 2014, 2014–15 LEN Euro League Women, da Gasar Mata ta Turai U19 na shekarar 2014 . 
Ta yi aiki a Jami'ar Hawaii .
