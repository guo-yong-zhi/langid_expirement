Kagara wani kauyene dake dauke da al'umma a cikin jihar Sokoto, Nigeria, kusa da garin Goronyo..Kagara tana gefen gefen kudu na Sahel, wani yanki ne da ke kudu da hamadar Sahara .  Kauyen yana gangarowa daga Kogin Goronyo da ke Kogin Rima .  A lokacin damina a shekara ta 2010, wani mummunan hadari ya tilasta sakin ruwa mai yawa don hana fashewar madatsar ruwan. Ambaliyar da ta biyo baya ta lalata yawancin gidaje a Kagara kuma ta lalata kusan dukkanin amfanin gona.  Daga baya dam din a zahiri ya balle, wanda ya haifar da ambaliyar ruwa da yawa. 1
