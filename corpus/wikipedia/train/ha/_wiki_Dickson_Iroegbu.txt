Dickson Iroegbu
Daraktan fina-finan Najeriya ne kuma furodusa. Ya shirya fina-finan Nollywood da dama. A shekarar 2005, A bugu na farko na African Movie Academy Awards, Iroegbu ya lashe kyautuka uku na fim din The Mayors, ciki har da kyaututtuka na Mafi kyawun Hotuna, Mafi kyawun Fim da Darakta mafi kyau.
