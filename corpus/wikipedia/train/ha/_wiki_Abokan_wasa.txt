Abokan wasa Ba wai ana nufin abokan da muke wasa dasu ba, ko abokanmu na yarinta ko abokan da muke gobza wasa a rukuni ba, Abokan wasa a al'adar bahaushe wasu mutane ne a dangi wanda muka hada wata alaka ta jini dasu ta dalilin iyayen maza ko iyayen mata.
A wannan mukala zanyi takaitaccen bayani ne akan wadannan mutane wanda suke a matsayin abokan wasan al'adar Bahaushe.
Ana samun abokan wasa ta bangare biyu,bangaren mahaifi da kuma ɓangaren mahaifiya.
Bangaren Mahaifi= ƴaƴan Gwaggonka wato kanwa ko yayar mahaifi sune abokan wasan ka/ki.
Bangaren Mahaifiya= Ƴaƴan kawu wato yayu ko kannan mahaifiya sune abokan wasan ka/ki.
