Muhammad ya bar mu hijira ta goma ya koma ga sarkinta mai kaddarawa da zata cika sai da yayi jawabi yana ta su Ali ya ga mai daukawa YBhg Tan Sri Dato"Salleh bin Haji Mohd Nor , Negeri Sembilan) ɗan ƙasar Malaysia ne, mai kiyaye muhalli kuma masanin kimiyya. Salleh shine darektan janar na farko na Cibiyar Kula da dazuzzuka ta Malaysia  kuma ya yi aiki a matsayin shugaban kungiyar Malaysian Nature Society  na tsawon shekaru 30. 1  2  3  4 
Tan Sri Dr Salleh ya sami tallafin karatu na Colombo Plan don nazarin gandun daji a Adelaide daga 1961-1962, kuma daga baya a Makarantar Forestry ta Australia , Canberra, daga inda ya kammala karatu tare da BSc  daga Jami'ar Adelaide da kuma Diploma na Forestry daga AFS. Salleh ya sami Ph.D. da Masters Degree daga Jami'ar Jihar Michigan .
Salleh ita ce mai karɓar lambar yabo ta Merdeka a cikin rukunin 'Makilai'. 5  Ya sami girmamawa masu zuwa:  
Salleh ta jagoranci Shirin Antarctic na Malaysia kuma ta ziyarci Antarctica sau biyu. Ya yi aiki a matsayin Pro-Chancellor na Universiti Teknologi Malaysia . An zabe shi shugaban kungiyar International Union of Forest Research Organizations . Ya kasance memba na Hukumar Kare Hakkin Dan Adam ta Malaysia , shugaban Universiti Malaysia Terengganu , shugaban kungiyar masana'antu ta Malaysia , kuma babban sakatare na Kwalejin Kimiyya ta Malaysia . Shi ɗan'uwan Ƙungiyar Kimiyya ta Malaysia ne.
A matsayinsa na shugaban MNS, kuma daga baya a matsayin babban mai ba da shawara ga al'umma, Salleh ya kasance mai aiki a cikin kamfen don adana gandun daji da yawa a Malaysia. 6  Kamfen ɗin da suka yi nasara sun haifar da kariya ga Endau-Rompin National Park, Royal Belum State Park, Kota Damansara Community Forest Park, Bukit Kiara park da sauran shafuka masu daraja ga bambancin halittu. 6
