Krasnoyarsk  birni ne, da kuma cibiyar gudanarwa na Krasnoyarsk Krai, Rasha, wanda yake kan Kogin Yenisei . Shine gari na uku mafi girma a cikin Yankin Tarayyar Siberia bayan Novosibirsk da Omsk . Ya zuwa shekarar 2020, mutane 1,095,286 suke zqma a cikin garin. 
Krasnoyarsk muhimmiyar mahaÉ—a ce ta Trans-Siberian Railway kuma É—ayan manyan Rasha masu kera alminiyon .
Garin sananne ne saboda yanayin shimfidar yanayin sa; marubuci Anton Chekhov ya yanke hukuncin Krasnoyarsk a matsayin mafi kyawun birni a Siberia.  1
