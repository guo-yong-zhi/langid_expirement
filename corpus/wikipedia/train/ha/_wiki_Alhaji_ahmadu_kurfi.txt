An haifi Alhaji Ahmadu Kurfi, Maradin Katsina, Hakimin Kurfi,  a shekarar 1931 a garin Kurfi, ya fara aikinsa na Gwamnati da Native Authority a matsayin malamin makaranta a shekarar 1951, ya soma aiki da gwamnayin Arewa a shekarar 1958, ya kuma yi ritaya a matsayin babban sakataren dindindin na gwamnatin tarayya a shekarar 1986.
