Clay Township birni ne, da ke a cikin gundumar Hubbard, Minnesota, Amurka. Yawan jama'a ya kasance 49 a ƙidayar 2000.
An sanya sunan garin Clay don ajiyar yumbu na halitta a cikin iyakokinsa. 
A cewar Ofishin Kididdiga na Amurka, garin yana da yawan yanki na 36.1 square miles   , wanda daga ciki 33.4 square miles    ƙasa ce kuma 2.7 square miles     ruwa ne.
Dangane da ƙidayar  na 2000, akwai mutane 49, gidaje 23, da iyalai 16 da ke zaune a cikin garin. Yawan yawan jama'a ya kasance mutane 1.5 a kowace murabba'in mil . Akwai rukunin gidaje 116 a matsakaicin yawa na 3.5/sq mi . Tsarin launin fata na garin ya kasance 97.96% Fari da 2.04% Ba'amurke .
Akwai gidaje 23, daga cikinsu kashi 13.0% na da ‘ya’ya ‘yan kasa da shekara 18 suna zaune tare da su, kashi 65.2% ma’aurata ne da ke zaune tare, kashi 4.3% na da mace mai gida babu miji, kashi 30.4% kuma ba iyali ba ne. Kashi 26.1% na dukkan gidaje sun kasance na mutane ne, kuma babu wanda ke da wanda ke zaune shi kaɗai wanda ya kai shekaru 65 ko sama da haka. Matsakaicin girman gidan ya kasance 2.13 kuma matsakaicin girman dangi ya kasance 2.56.
A cikin garin an bazu yawan jama'a, tare da 8.2% a ƙasa da shekaru 18, 4.1% daga 18 zuwa 24, 20.4% daga 25 zuwa 44, 51.0% daga 45 zuwa 64, da 16.3% waɗanda ke da shekaru 65 ko sama da haka. . Matsakaicin shekarun ya kasance shekaru 52. Ga kowane mata 100, akwai maza 88.5. Ga kowane mata 100 masu shekaru 18 zuwa sama, akwai maza 95.7.
Matsakaicin kuɗin shiga na gida a cikin garin shine $36,250, kuma matsakaicin kuɗin shiga na dangi shine $45,625. Maza suna da matsakaicin kudin shiga na $30,750 sabanin $23,750 na mata. Kudin shiga kowane mutum na garin shine $28,130. Babu iyalai da 5.4% na yawan mutanen da ke zaune a ƙasa da layin talauci, gami da waɗanda ba ƙasa da goma sha takwas ba kuma babu ɗaya daga cikin waɗanda suka haura 64.
