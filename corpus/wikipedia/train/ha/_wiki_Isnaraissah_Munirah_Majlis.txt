Isnaraissah Munirah binti Majilis @ Fakharudy  ɗan siyasan Malaysia ne kuma injiniyan lantarki wanda ya yi aiki a matsayin memba na majalisar  na Kota Belud tun daga watan Mayu shekara ta 2018. Ta yi aiki a matsayin Mataimakin Ministan Makamashi, Kimiyya, Fasaha, Muhalli da Canjin Yanayi a cikin gwamnatin Pakatan Harapan  a karkashin tsohon Firayim Minista Mahathir Mohamad da tsohon Minista Yeo Bee Yin daga Yuli 2018 zuwa faduwar gwamnatin PH a watan Fabrairun 2020. Ita memba ce ta Jam'iyyar Heritage  kuma ta kasance memba na Jam'iyyar Adalci ta Jama'a , wata jam'iyya ce ta PH da tsohuwar hadin gwiwar Pakatan Rakyat . Har ila yau, ita ce mace daya tilo 'yar majalisa ta WARISAN.
An haifi Munirah a garin Kota Belud na Sabah . Ita ce dan uwan Salleh Said Keruak na biyu . 1 
Munirah ta kasance tana da hannu sosai a siyasa tun 2011 a karkashin Jam'iyyar Adalci ta Jama'a . 2  Ta kasance koyaushe tana nuna fatan cewa za a sami sabon babi ga Malaysia tare da sabuwar gwamnati kuma tana so ta kasance wani ɓangare na canjin don ta iya kawo garinsu ƙarin damar ci gaba. 1  A shekara ta 2016, ta bar jam'iyyar don shiga sabuwar jam'iyyar Heritage Party  da ke Sabah. Baya ga aiki sosai a siyasa, Munirah kuma injiniya ce ta lantarki kuma tana da ƙwarewa a cikin harsuna huɗu wato Turanci, Malay, Dusun da Bajau. 1  2 
A cikin zaben 2013, jam'iyyarta, PKR ta gabatar da Munirah don fuskantar Abdul Rahman Dahlan na United Malays National Organisation  a kujerar majalisar dokokin Kota Belud amma ta rasa. 1 
A cikin zaben 2018, Munirah ta sake shiga, a wannan lokacin ta sabuwar jam'iyyarta, WARISAN don takara a kujerar majalisar dokokin Kota Belud, tana fuskantar dan uwanta Salleh Said Keruak daga UMNO kuma daga baya ta lashe. 2 
Isnaraissah Munirah Majilis  na daga cikin mata 33 'yan majalisa da ke kira ga Dewan Rakyat ta zama majalisa mai kula da jinsi daidai da jagororin kungiyar Inter-Parliamentary Union.
