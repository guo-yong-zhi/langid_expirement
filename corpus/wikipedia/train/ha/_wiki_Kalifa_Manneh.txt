Kalifa Manneh ,ɗan wasan ƙwallon ƙafa ne ɗan ƙasar Gambia wanda ke taka leda a matsayin winger ga ƙungiyar Serie B ta Italiya Perugia.
Yana da shekaru 15, Manneh ya tsere daga Gambia a cikin jirgin ruwa ya sauka a Sicily a matsayin dan gudun hijira. A wannan shekarar, ya nemi Catania kuma ya shiga makarantar matasa. 1   Manneh ya fara buga wasansa na farko na gwaninta da Catania a wasan da suka tashi 0-0 a Seria C da Juve Stabia a ranar 14 ga watan Mayu 2017.  2 
A ranar 3 ga watan Janairu 2020, Carrarese ya ƙare yarjejeniyar lamuni da Catania. 3  
A ranar 15 ga watan Yuli 2021, ya sanya hannu kan kwangilar shekaru uku tare da kulob ɗin Perugia. 4   A ranar 31 ga watan Janairu 2022, an ba da Manneh aro ga kungiyar Taranto. 5  
Manneh ya fara bugawa tawagar kwallon kafa ta Gambia a wasan sada zumunci da suka doke Guinea da ci 1-0 a ranar 7 ga watan Yuni 2019. 6
