Mizoram jiha ce, da ke a Arewa maso Gabashin ƙasar Indiya. Tana da yawan fili kimanin kilomita arabba’i 21,081 da yawan jama’a	1,091,014 . Jihar tarayyar Indiya ce daga shekara ta 1972. Babban birnin jihar da birnin mafi girman jihar Aizawl ne. P. S. Sreedharan Pillai shi ne gwamnan jihar. Jihar Mizoram tana da iyaka da jihohin uku  da ƙasar biyu .
