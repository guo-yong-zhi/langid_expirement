Moussa Traoré   ƙwararren ɗan ƙwallon ƙafa ne wanda ke taka leda a matsayin ɗan wasan gaba ga Rebeca. 1  An haife shi a Ivory Coast, ya wakilci Burkina Faso a duniya.
Traoré ya fara aikinsa tare da Commune FC kuma an ba shi aro ga Planète Champion a kakar 2008-09.  An aro shi a ranar 2 ga Yulin shekara ta 2009 daga Commune FC zuwa babban kulob na Belgium Standard Liège, tare da yarjejeniyar lamuni na shekara guda tare da zaɓin siyarwa.  2  Bayan komawar sa zuwa Commune FC, Standard ya ja zaɓin da aka siyar,  3  kuma ya ba shi aro wata rana don kakar daya zuwa SV Zulte Waregem . A kan 6 Nuwamba 2019, Traoré ya shiga RWDM47 akan yarjejeniya na sauran kakar.  4
