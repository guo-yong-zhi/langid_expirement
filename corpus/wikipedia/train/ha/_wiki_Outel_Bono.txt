Outel Bono  ya kasance ɗan ƙasar Chadi likita kuma ɗan siyasa.
Ya kasance likata babban darakta na asibitin babban birnin Chadi, Fort-Lamy , a shekarar 1963 lokacin da aka kama shi da laifin yin makarkashiya ga gwamnatin Shugaba François Tombalbaye. An yanke masa hukuncin kisa, an sake shi bayan gangamin kamfen na zanga-zanga mai ƙarfi wanda French Communist Party ta jagoranta. An wanke shi a shekarar 1965 kuma ya sami damar ci gaba da aikin sa likita.
