Rúben Sílvio Lino Gouveia  ƙwararren ɗan wasan ƙwallon ƙafa ne na ƙasar Angola wanda ke taka leda a ƙungiyar ƙwallon ƙafa ta ƙasar Portugal União Desportiva Alta de Lisboa a matsayin ɗan wasan tsakiya. 1 
An haife shi a Lisbon, Gouveia bai taɓa taka leda a matakin ƙwararru a ƙasarsa ba, ya tattara jimlar rukuni na uku na matches 186 da kwallaye 30 sama da shekaru goma ya wakilcin kungiyoyin Estrela de Vendas Novas, Real SC, Atlético SC, SCU Torreense, UD Vilafranquense, Casa Pia AC, Clube Oriental de Lisboa  da União de Santarém. A cikin watan Janairu 2009, ya tafi a matsayin aro zuwa kulob ɗin Halesowen Town na Gasar Isthmian Ingila.  2 
Gouveia ya rattaba hannu da kulob din Girabola CRD Libolo a cikin shekarar 2012. Ya ci gaba da fafatawa a gasar ta Angola a shekaru masu zuwa. 3  4   
Bayan da aka ba shi takardar zama dan kasa, Gouveia ya fara buga wa Angola wasa a ranar 28 ga watan Mayu 2014 yana da shekaru 29, a wasan sada zumunta da suka doke Morocco da ci 2-0. 5   6
