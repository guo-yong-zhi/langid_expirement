Mary Mambwe  ɗan wasan ƙwallon ƙafa ne na ƙasar Zambiya wanda ke taka leda a matsayin ɗan wasan tsakiya . Ta kasance memba a kungiyar mata ta kasar Zambia .
Mary Mambwe ya bugawa Nkwazi FC . 
Mambwe ta wakilci Zambia a gasar COSAFA U-20 ta mata ta shekara ta 2019.  A matakin manya, ta buga gasar neman cancantar shiga gasar cin kofin nahiyar Afirka ta mata ta shekara ta 2018 .  1
