Sulayman Bojang  ƙwararren ɗan wasan ƙwallon ƙafa ne wanda ke taka leda a ƙungiyar ƙwallon ƙafa ta Skeid. 1   An haife shi a Norway, yana wakiltar Gambia a duniya. 2 
A ranar 13 ga watan Agusta 2018, Sarpsborg 08 ya sanar da sanya hannu kan Bojang, daga Skeid akan kwangilar shekaru 3.5. 3  
A watan Yuni 2019, an zaɓi Bojang a ƙungiyar ƙwallon ƙafa ta ƙasar Gambia kuma ya fara buga wa ƙasar wasa a ranar 8 ga watan Yuni 2019 da Guinea, wanda suka ci 1-0 tare da Bojang a gefen hagu a gaba dayan wasan.  4  Ya buga wasansa na biyu bayan kwanaki hudu da Morocco.
