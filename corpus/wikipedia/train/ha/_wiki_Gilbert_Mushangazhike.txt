Gilbert Mushangazhike  ɗan wasan ƙwallon ƙafa ne na ƙasar Zimbabwe. Dan wasan kwallon kafa na kungiyar kwanan nan ya buga wasa a Swaziland ta Manzini Sundowns, a China ta Jiangsu Sainty, na Jamus Kickers Emden da kuma Afirka ta Kudu da Manning Rangers FC, Orlando Pirates da Mpumalanga Black Aces. 1 
Ya kasance memba a kungiyar kwallon kafa ta Zimbabwe a shekarar 2006, wacce ta kare a matakin karshe a rukuninsu a zagayen farko na gasar, don haka ta kasa samun tikitin zuwa matakin kwata. 2 
Gudanar da Kungiya
A cikin shekarar 2019, an nada shi a matsayin koci na Golden Eagles FC, ƙungiya ta uku a Zimbabwe.
