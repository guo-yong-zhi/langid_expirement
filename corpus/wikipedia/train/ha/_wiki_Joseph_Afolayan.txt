Joseph Afolayan ya fara aiki ne a Jami’ar Ahmadu Bello  da ke Zariya inda ya yi digirinsa na farko B.  3 Eng a fannin Injiniya a shekarar 1981. Ya sami M. Eng da PhD a Structural Engineering a ABU a 1984 da 1994 kuma ya zama bi da bi. Farfesa a 2004. Baya ga ABU, Afolayan ya kuma halarci wasu jami'o'i biyu na kasashen waje koyo dabarun ilmantarwa, bincike da gudanarwa.
Ya shiga Sashin Injiniya na Civil Engineering na Jami’ar Fasaha ta Tarayya ta Akure  a shekarar 2005 da kuma Jami’ar Landmark a 2014 daga nan ya shiga Jami’ar Anchor a matsayin Mataimakin Shugaban Jami’ar. A FUTA, ya kasance HOD kuma, Dean, Makarantar Injiniya da Fasahar Injiniya.
