Dato 'Haji Ahmad bin Lebai Sudin  ɗan siyasan Malaysia ne wanda a halin yanzu shi ne wakilin Bukit Lada  a Majalisar Dokokin Jihar Kedah . Ahmad memba ne na United Malays National Organisation  a cikin hadin gwiwar Barisan Nasional na Malaysia. Yana da digiri tare da girmamawa a cikin Sociology da Anthropology daga Jami'ar Malaya . Nan da nan bayan ya halarci UM, Ahmad ya tafi Cibiyar Kula da Gwamnati ta Malaysia  kuma ya sami difloma a cikin Gudanar da Jama'a . An yaba masa saboda inganta ingancin noma a Bukit Lada musamman a lokacin da yake Exco Kedah a 1999.
Ahmad ya fito ne daga Kampung Panchor, Pokok Sena, Kedah, Malaysia. Shi ne ɗan Hajah Yah binti Lebai Dahaman da Lebai Sudin bin Lebai Ahmad. Mahaifinsa ya sami Kampong Panchor a lokacin mamayar Japan a Tanah Melayu. Iyayensa biyu sun fito ne daga zuriyar ulama na yankin, saboda haka sunan Lebai .
Ahmad ya lashe karar cin zarafi a kan Datuk Ariffin Man, wanda shi ma Babban Sakataren Kedah Barisan Nasional . Babban Kotun ta umarci Ariffin Man da ya biya RM150,000 a cikin lalacewa ga wakilin Bukit Lada Datuk Ahmad Lebai Sudin don yin maganganun lalata a cikin jawabinsa a cikin Sri Dewan Tanjung, Santap, Pokok Sena, Kedah.
