Khab ƙaramin ƙauye ne a cikin jihar Himachal Pradesh, Indiya. Tana cikin kwarin kogin Sutlej kusa da iyakar Indiya da Tibet. National Highway 5 yana haɗa Khab da Shimla babban birnin jihar. Khab Sangam shine mahaɗar kogin Spiti da kogin Sutlej. Kogin Spiti da ke gudana ta kwarin spiti a nan ya hadu da Sutlej, wanda ya samo asali daga tafkin Mansarova na Tibet. Tsohon gidan sufi na Tashigang Gompa yana nan kusa. Kololuwar Reo Purgil, wanda ya tashi zuwa 22,400 feet , yana bayyane kuma hamada mai sanyi na Spiti ta ta'allaka ne a kan gada da ke kusa.
An haɗa Khab ta hanya tare da National Highway 5 daga Shimla babban birnin jihar. Hanyar Kasa ta 505 tana farawa daga Khab kuma tana ba da haɗin kai zuwa kwarin Spiti a Lahaul da gundumar Spiti na Himachal Pradesh. 1
