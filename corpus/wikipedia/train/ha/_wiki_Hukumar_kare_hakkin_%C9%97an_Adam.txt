Hukumar kare hakkin dan adam, wacce aka fi sani da hukumar hulda da jama'a, ita ce hukumar da aka kafa domin bincike, inganta ko kare hakkin dan adam. 1 
Kalmar tana iya komawa ga ƙungiyoyin ƙasa da ƙasa, na ƙasa ko na ƙasa da aka kafa don wannan dalili, kamar cibiyoyin kare hakkin ɗan adam na ƙasa ko  kwamitocin gaskiya da sulhu. 2 
An kafa kwamitocin kare hakkin dan Adam na kasa da na kasa a kasashe da dama domin ingantawa da kare hakkin ‘yan kasarsu, kuma galibin kwamitocin kungiyoyin jama’a ne amma suna da ‘yancin kai daga jihar. A wasu ƙasashe jami'in tsaro yana yin wannan rawar. 3  Kwamitocin da ke kasa jihar ce ta dauki nauyinsu sai dai inda aka nuna. 4
