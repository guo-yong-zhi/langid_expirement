Gidami daya ne daga cikin gundumomi a yankin Oromia na kasar Habasha . Yana daga cikin Kelem Welega Zone . Cibiyar gudanarwa ta wannan unguwa ita ce Gidami . An raba Dale Sedi da tsohuwar gundumar Jimma Gidami .
Kididdiga ta kasa ta shekarar 2007 ta bayar da rahoton jimillar yawan jama'a na wannan gundumar mai mutane 85,904, daga cikinsu 43,337 maza ne, 42,567 kuma mata; 5,602 ko 6.52% Da yawan jama'arta mazauna birni ne. Yawancin mazaunan sun lura da Kiristanci na Orthodox na Habasha, tare da 48.88% sun ba da rahoton cewa a matsayin addininsu, yayin da 37.22% na Furotesta ne, kuma 13.75% sun lura da Musulunci. 1
