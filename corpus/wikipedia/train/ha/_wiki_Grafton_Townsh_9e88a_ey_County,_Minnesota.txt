Grafton Township birni ne, da ke a cikin gundumar Sibley, Minnesota, Amurka. Yawan jama'a ya kai 259 a ƙidayar 2000.
An shirya Garin Grafton a cikin 1873. 1 
Dangane da Ofishin Kididdiga na Amurka, garin yana da yawan yanki na 39.1 square miles   , wanda daga ciki 39.0 square miles    ƙasa ce kuma 0.2 square miles     ruwa ne.
A ƙidayar 2000,  akwai mutane 259, gidaje 89 da iyalai 72 da ke zaune a cikin garin. Yawan yawan jama'a ya kasance mutane 6.6 a kowace murabba'in mil . Akwai rukunin gidaje 100 a matsakaicin yawa na 2.6/sq mi . Tsarin launin fata na garin ya kasance 98.07% Fari, 0.77% Ba'amurke, da 1.16% daga jinsi biyu ko fiye. Hispanic ko Latino na kowace kabila sun kasance 1.16% na yawan jama'a.
Akwai gidaje 89, wanda kashi 44.9% na da ‘ya’ya ‘yan kasa da shekara 18 suna zaune tare da su, kashi 76.4% ma’aurata ne da ke zaune tare, kashi 2.2% na da mace mai gida babu miji, kashi 18.0% kuma ba iyali ba ne. Kashi 15.7% na dukkan gidaje sun kasance mutane ne, kuma 12.4% suna da wanda ke zaune shi kaɗai wanda ya kai shekaru 65 ko sama da haka. Matsakaicin girman gida shine 2.91 kuma matsakaicin girman dangi shine 3.27.
Rarraba shekarun ya kasance 32.4% a ƙarƙashin shekarun 18, 6.6% daga 18 zuwa 24, 28.2% daga 25 zuwa 44, 20.8% daga 45 zuwa 64, da 12.0% waɗanda ke da shekaru 65 ko fiye. Tsakanin shekarun ya kasance shekaru 36. Ga kowane mata 100, akwai maza 102.3. Ga kowane mata 100 masu shekaru 18 zuwa sama, akwai maza 113.4.
Matsakaicin kuɗin shiga gida shine $45,250, kuma matsakaicin kuɗin shiga iyali shine $46,750. Maza suna da matsakaicin kudin shiga na $36,458 sabanin $15,833 na mata. Kudin shiga kowane mutum na garin shine $18,045. Kusan 2.5% na iyalai da 4.3% na yawan jama'a sun kasance ƙasa da layin talauci, gami da 2.2% na waɗanda ba su kai shekara sha takwas ba da 7.1% na waɗanda 65 ko sama da haka.
