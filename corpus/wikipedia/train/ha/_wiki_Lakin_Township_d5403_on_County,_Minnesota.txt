Lakin Township birni ne, da ke a cikin gundumar Morrison, Minnesota, Amurka. Yawan jama'a ya kasance 409 a ƙidayar 2000.
An shirya garin Lakin a cikin 1903, kuma an ba shi suna don girmama Fred H. Lakin, jami'in gundumar. 
Dangane da Ofishin Kididdiga ta Amurka, garin yana da yawan yanki na 35.8 square miles   , duk kasa.
Dangane da ƙidayar  na 2000, akwai mutane 409, gidaje 138, da iyalai 100 da ke zaune a cikin garin. Yawan yawan jama'a ya kasance mutane 11.4 a kowace murabba'in mil . Akwai rukunin gidaje 158 a matsakaicin yawa na 4.4/sq mi . Tsarin launin fata na garin ya kasance 98.53% Fari, 1.22% Ba'amurke, 0.24% daga sauran jinsi . Hispanic ko Latino na kowace kabila sun kasance 0.24% na yawan jama'a.
Akwai gidaje 138, daga cikinsu kashi 39.9% na da ‘ya’ya ‘yan kasa da shekara 18 suna zaune tare da su, kashi 58.7% ma’aurata ne da ke zaune tare, kashi 8.0% na da mace mai gida babu miji, kashi 27.5% kuma ba iyali ba ne. 23.2% na duk gidaje sun kasance na mutane ne, kuma 5.1% suna da wanda ke zaune shi kaɗai wanda ya kai shekaru 65 ko fiye. Matsakaicin girman gidan ya kasance 2.96 kuma matsakaicin girman dangi shine 3.50.
A cikin garin yawan jama'a ya bazu, tare da 29.6% a ƙarƙashin shekaru 18, 8.3% daga 18 zuwa 24, 32.3% daga 25 zuwa 44, 20.3% daga 45 zuwa 64, da 9.5% waɗanda shekarunsu suka kai 65 ko sama da haka. . Matsakaicin shekarun ya kasance shekaru 33. Ga kowane mata 100, akwai maza 117.6. Ga kowane mata 100 masu shekaru 18 zuwa sama, akwai maza 134.1.
Matsakaicin kuɗin shiga na gida a cikin garin shine $38,500, kuma matsakaicin kuɗin shiga na dangi shine $40,833. Maza suna da matsakaicin kudin shiga na $30,000 sabanin $21,875 na mata. Kudin shiga kowane mutum na garin shine $14,415. Kusan 13.7% na iyalai da 13.9% na yawan jama'a sun kasance ƙasa da layin talauci, gami da 20.7% na waɗanda ke ƙasa da shekaru 18 da 17.6% na waɗanda shekaru 65 ko sama da haka.
