Laleh Mehree Bakhtiar  Ba'amurkiya  'yar Islama ce kuma masaniyar Sufi, marubuci, mai fassara, kuma masaniyar ilimin halayyar É—an adam.  1  Ta fitar da fassarar tsaka-tsakin jinsi, The Sublime Quran, kuma ta kalubalanci matsayin da ake ciki a kalmar Larabci daraba, wadda aka fassara a al'adance da "bugu" - kalmar da ta ce an yi amfani da ita a matsayin hujja don cin zarafin mata musulmai.
