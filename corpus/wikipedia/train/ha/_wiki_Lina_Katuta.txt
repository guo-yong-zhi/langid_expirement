Iina Ndapewa Katuta , wanda aka fi sani da Ndapewa Katuta, ɗan wasan ƙwallon ƙafa ne na Namibia wanda ke taka leda a matsayin mai tsaron baya ga ƙungiyar ƙwallon ƙafa ta Namibia Women's Super League Khomes gwagwalada Nampol FC da kuma ƙungiyar mata ta Namibia . Ta kasance cikin tawagar a gasar cin kofin matan Afirka ta shekarar 2014 . A matakin kulob ta buga wa JS Academy a Namibia.
