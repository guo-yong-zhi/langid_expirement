Dangane da Ofishin Kididdiga ta Amurka, garin yana da yawan yanki na 33.3 murabba'in mil , wanda 33.0 murabba'in mil  kasa ce kuma 0.3 murabba'in mil   ruwa ne.
Dangane da ƙidayar  na 2000, akwai mutane 183, gidaje 68, da iyalai 59 da ke zaune a cikin garin. Yawan yawan jama'a ya kasance mutane 5.5 a kowace murabba'in mil . Akwai rukunin gidaje 75 a matsakaicin yawa na 2.3/sq mi . Tsarin launin fata na garin ya kasance 98.91% Fari, 0.55% Asiya, da 0.55% daga jinsi biyu ko fiye.
Akwai gidaje 68, daga cikinsu kashi 36.8% na da ‘ya’ya ‘yan kasa da shekara 18 suna zaune tare da su, kashi 82.4% ma’aurata ne da ke zaune tare, kashi 4.4% na da mace mai gida babu miji, kashi 11.8% kuma ba na iyali ba ne. Kashi 10.3% na dukkan gidaje sun kasance na mutane ne, kuma 1.5% suna da wanda ke zaune shi kaɗai wanda ya kai shekaru 65 ko fiye. Matsakaicin girman gidan ya kasance 2.69 kuma matsakaicin girman dangi shine 2.87.
