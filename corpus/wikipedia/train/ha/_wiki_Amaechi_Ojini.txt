Amaechi Ojini ta kasance ƴar wasan ƙwallon ƙafa ta mata ce, da ke buga wasa a ƙungiyar wardan a matsayin mai buga gaba. A matakin kulob din tana wasa ne ga Rivers Angels. tayi kwallo a garin jos a yayin farawarta. 1 
Ojini ta fara aiki a Tin City Jos a 2008, bayan haka ta yi wasa a kungiyar Adamawa Queens da Pelican Stars . Daga shekara ta 2013 har zuwa shekara ta 2015 ta kamu da kamfanin Confluence Queens a shekara ta 2015   sannan aka tura ta zuwa Rivers Angels a farkon shekarar 2016.
