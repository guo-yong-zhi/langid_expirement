Mohammed Wakil , karamin ministan wutar lantarki a Najeriya, ya koma aiki a ranar 5 ga Maris 2014. Har ila yau, dan majalisar dokokin Najeriya ne, mataimakin shugaban jam'iyyar People's Democratic Party , kuma lauya.
An haifi Wakil ne a ranar 6 ga watan Yunin shekara ta 1965 a karamar hukumar Damboa ta jihar Borno kuma ya kammala karatunsa na digiri a fannin shari'a a jami'ar Maiduguri, jihar Borno.  1 
A lokacin mulkin Obasanjo/Atiku na farko na dimokuradiyya  daga shekara ta 1999 zuwa shekara ta 2003, Wakil ya kasance shugaban masu rinjaye a majalisar wakilai ta Tarayyar Najeriya . Ya kuma kasance shugaban majalisar wakilai.
Wakil ya sami lambar yabo ta Jami'in Order of Niger  kuma abokin tarayya ne na Cibiyar Gudanarwa ta Najeriya, FNIM.
Wakil ya auri Hajiya Falmata Mohammed, kuma suna da ‘ya’ya shida.
