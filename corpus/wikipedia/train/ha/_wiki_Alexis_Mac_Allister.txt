Alexis Mac Allister  cikakken ɗan wasan ƙwallon ƙafa ne dan asalin ƙasar Argentina wanda ke taka leda a matsayin ɗan wasan tsakiya a ƙungiyar kwallon kafa ta Premier League ta Brighton   Hove Albion da kuma ƙungiyar ƙwallon ƙafa ta Argentina . 
An haifi Mac Allister ne a Santa Rosa, La Pampa .
Yayun Alexis Mac Allister Francis da Kevin suma ƙwararrun ƴan ƙwallon ƙafa ne.  Su ne 'ya'yan "Red" Carlos Mac Allister da 'ya'yan Patricio Mac Allister, dukansu 'yan wasan kwallon kafa da suka yi ritaya.   1  Mac Allister sunan iyali ne wanda ya samo asali daga Scotland da Ireland. Zuriyar Mac Allister ta kwanan nan ta fito ne daga zuriyar Irish.  2   A cikin wata hira da ta gabata, ya tabbatar da cewa wasu kakanninsa sun isa Argentina daga Ireland kuma ya bayyana cewa yana sane da alaƙa da Scotland. Ana iya danganta kakanninsa zuwa Donabate, Ireland. Hakanan zai iya gano zuriyar Fife a Gabashin Scotland, sannan zuwa Argentina.
