Yusuf Soalih shima ana kiransa Afa Ajura , malamin addinin musulinci ne dan kasar Ghana, mai waâ€™azi ne, mai rajin siyasa, kuma shine ya kafa kuma ya shugabanci wata kungiya a Ghana. Afa Ajura ya kasance mai goyan bayan musulmin sunna ne da ya guji ayyukan maguzawa na jahiliyya, wanda wasu kuma suka ambace shi a matsayin share fagen kawo canjin akidar wahabiyanci a Ghana. Ya kafa Cibiyar Musulunci ta Anbariyya a Tamale a cikin 1940s. Ya mutu a garin Tamale a ranar 22 ga Disamba, 2004. Saeed Abubakr Zakaria ya gaje shi a 2007 a matsayin shugaban Anbariyya Sunni Community.
