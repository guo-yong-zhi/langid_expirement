Tsaunukan Virunga  jerin tsaunuka ne a gabashin Afirka, a kan iyakar arewacin kasar Rwanda, Jamhuriyar Demokradiyyar Kongo , da Uganda. Tsaunin wani reshe ne na tsaunukan Kyautar Albertine, wanda kuma ke iyaka da reshen yamma na gabashin Afirka. Suna tsakanin Lake Edward da Lake Kivu. Sunan "Virunga" sigar Ingilishi ce ta kalmar Kinyarwanda ibirunga, wacce ke nufin "volcanoes".
Tsaunin ya kunshi manyan duwatsu masu aman wuta guda takwas. Mafi yawansu basuyi bacci ba, banda Dutsen Nyiragongo mita 3,462  da Dutsen Nyamuragira mita 3,063 , duk a cikin DRC. Fashewa ta baya-bayan nan ta faru ne a shekarar 2006, 2010 da Mayu 2021. Dutsen Karisimbi shi ne tsauni mafi girma da yakai mita 4,507 . Mafi tsawun tsauni shi ne Dutsen Sabyinyo, wanda ya hau mita 3,634  sama da matakin teku.
