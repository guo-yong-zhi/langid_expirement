Babba da jaka ko Borin tinke  tsuntsu ne.
Babban da jaka tsuntsune wanda yake da kafa biyu sannan yana da dogon baki wanda yake iya cakar halittun bakin ruwa kamar kwari tana kifi kaguwa dadai sauransu sanan a kirjinsa akwai wata jaka kamar laya wacce yake iya ajiye abinda ya samo akiwo damin kaiwa ya'yansa suma susami abin kalaci 
Anfi samun babba da jaka a gurin dayake da ruwa
.Tsunne mai dogon baki da kafafuwa. Yana cin dukkan abincin da tsutsun yake ci.
