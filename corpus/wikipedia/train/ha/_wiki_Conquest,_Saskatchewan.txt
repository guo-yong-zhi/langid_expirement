Nasara  ƙauye ne a lardin Saskatchewan na Kanada a cikin Karamar Hukumar Ƙauyen Kwarin No. 285 da Ƙungiyar Ƙididdiga ta 12 .
An haɗa cin nasara a matsayin ƙauye a ranar 24 ga Oktoba, 1911. 
 
A cikin ƙidayar jama'a ta 2021 da Statistics Kanada ta gudanar, Conquest yana da yawan jama'a 167 da ke zaune a cikin 75 daga cikin 76 na gidaje masu zaman kansu, canjin 4.4% daga yawan jama'arta na 2016 na 160 . Tare da yanki na ƙasa 1 square kilometre   , tana da yawan yawan jama'a 167.0/km   a cikin 2021. 
A cikin ƙidayar yawan jama'a ta 2016, ƙauyen Nasara ya ƙididdige yawan jama'a na 160 da ke zaune a cikin 70 daga cikin 78 duka gidajen masu zaman kansu, a -10% ya canza daga yawan 2011 na 176 . Tare da yanki na ƙasa 1 square kilometre   , tana da yawan yawan jama'a 160.0/km   a cikin 2016. 
Cin nasara shine saitin fim ɗin <i id="mwPA">Nasara</i> na 1998.
