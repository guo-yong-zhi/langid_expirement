A muṣḥaf  kalma ce ta Larabci don codex ko tarin zanen gado, amma kuma tana nufin kwafin Alqur'ani a rubuce.  surori na Kur'ani, waɗanda Musulmai suka yi imani an saukar da su a cikin shekaru 23 a rayuwar Muhammadu, an rubuta su a kan takarda daban-daban a zamanin Muhammadu. Shekaru ashirin bayan haka, an tattara waɗannan takardu zuwa juzu'i ɗaya a ƙarƙashin halifa na uku, Uthman ibn Affan, kuma wannan tarin ya zama tushen duk rubutattun kwafin Alqur'ani har zuwa yau. 1
