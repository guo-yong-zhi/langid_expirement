Milan Badelj 1 
Milan Badelj  an haife shi a ranar 25 ga Fabrairu 1989 ƙwararren ɗan wasan ƙwallon ƙafa ne ɗan ƙasar Croatia wanda ke taka leda a matsayin mai tsaron gida don ƙungiyar Serie A Genoa.  Ya kasance memba a cikin 'yan wasan Croatian da suka zo na biyu a gasar cin kofin duniya ta FIFA 2018 da kuma wani bangare na tawagar kasar a bugu na 2014 da bugu na UEFA Euro a 2012, 2016 da 2020.
