Opa Sanganté  ƙwararren ɗan wasan ƙwallon Kungiyar Kwallon Kafa wanda ke taka leda a matsayin ɗan wasan tsakiya ga Championnat National Club Châteauroux. An haife shi a Senegal, Sanganté yana buga wa tawagar kasar Guinea-Bissau wasa.
Sanganté ya koma Faransa daga Senegal yana da shekaru 6, kuma yana buga kwallon kafa tun yana karami. 1   Ya fara wasansa na ƙwararru a LB Châteauroux a cikin nasara da ci 3–2 a gasar Ligue 2 Stade Brestois 29 a ranar 28 ga watan Yulin 2017. 2 
An haife shi a Senegal, Sanganté dan asalin Bissau-Guinean ne, kuma an kira shi don wakiltar Guinea-Bissau a watan Oktoba 2020. 3   Ya buga wasa a Guinea-Bissau a 2-0 2021 2021 na neman shiga gasar cin kofin Afrika a hannun Senegal a ranar 13 ga watan Nuwamba 2020. 4
