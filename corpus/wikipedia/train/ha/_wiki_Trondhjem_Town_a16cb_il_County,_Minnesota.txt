Garin Trondhjem birni ne, da ke cikin Otter Tail County, Minnesota, Amurka. Yawan jama'a ya kasance 171 a ƙidayar 2000.
An shirya garin Trondhjem a cikin 1873, kuma an sanya masa suna bayan Trondhjem, a Norway. 
Dangane da Ofishin Kididdiga na Amurka, garin yana da yawan yanki na 33.9 murabba'in kilomita , wanda 33.1 murabba'in mil  kasa ce kuma 0.8 murabba'in mil   ruwa ne.
Dangane da ƙidayar  na 2000, akwai mutane 171, gidaje 62, da iyalai 51 da ke zaune a cikin garin. Yawan yawan jama'a ya kasance mutane 5.2 a kowace murabba'in mil . Akwai rukunin gidaje 68 a matsakaicin yawa na 2.1/sq mi . Tsarin launin fata na garin ya kasance 98.83% Fari, 0.58% Asiya, da 0.58% daga jinsi biyu ko fiye. Hispanic ko Latino na kowace kabila sun kasance 0.58% na yawan jama'a.
Akwai gidaje 62, daga cikinsu kashi 33.9% na da ‘ya’ya ‘yan kasa da shekara 18 suna zaune tare da su, kashi 74.2% ma’aurata ne da ke zaune tare, kashi 4.8% na da mace mai gida babu miji, kashi 17.7% kuma ba iyali ba ne. Kashi 14.5% na dukkan gidaje sun kasance mutane ne, kuma 6.5% suna da wanda ke zaune shi kaɗai wanda ya kai shekaru 65 ko sama da haka. Matsakaicin girman gida shine 2.76 kuma matsakaicin girman dangi shine 3.04.
A cikin garin yawan jama'a ya bazu, tare da 28.1% a ƙasa da shekaru 18, 7.0% daga 18 zuwa 24, 25.1% daga 25 zuwa 44, 25.1% daga 45 zuwa 64, da 14.6% waɗanda shekarunsu suka kai 65 ko sama da haka. . Matsakaicin shekarun ya kasance shekaru 38. Ga kowane mata 100, akwai maza 119.2. Ga kowane mata 100 masu shekaru 18 zuwa sama, akwai maza 105.0.
Matsakaicin kuɗin shiga na gida a cikin garin shine $26,750, kuma matsakaicin kuɗin shiga na dangi shine $28,750. Maza suna da matsakaicin kudin shiga na $22,500 sabanin $12,083 na mata. Kudin shiga kowane mutum na garin ya kasance $18,710. Babu daya daga cikin iyalai da kashi 1.9% na yawan jama'a da ke rayuwa ƙasa da layin talauci .
