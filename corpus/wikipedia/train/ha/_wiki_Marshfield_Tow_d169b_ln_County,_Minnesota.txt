Garin Marshfield birni ne, da ke a cikin Lincoln County, Minnesota, Amurka. Yawan jama'ar birnin ya kasance 231 a ƙidayar 2000.
Sunan garin shine haɗin gwiwar Charles Marsh da Ira Field, wasu ƙauyuka biyu na farko. 
Dangane da Ofishin Kididdiga ta Amurka, garin yana da yawan yanki na 36.0 murabba'in mil , wanda 34.8 murabba'in mil  ƙasa ce kuma 1.2 murabba'in mil   ruwa ne.
Dangane da ƙidayar  na 2000, akwai mutane 231, gidaje 83, da iyalai 63 da ke zaune a cikin garin. Yawan yawan jama'a ya kasance mutane 6.6 a kowace murabba'in mil . Akwai rukunin gidaje 90 a matsakaicin yawa na 2.6/sq mi . Tsarin launin fata na garin ya kasance fari 100.00%.
Akwai gidaje 83, daga cikinsu kashi 43.4% na da ‘ya’ya ‘yan kasa da shekara 18 suna zaune tare da su, kashi 71.1% ma’aurata ne da ke zaune tare, kashi 3.6% na da mace mai gida babu miji, kashi 22.9% kuma ba iyali ba ne. Kashi 21.7% na dukkan gidaje sun kasance mutane ne, kuma kashi 2.4% suna da wanda ke zaune shi kaɗai wanda ya kai shekaru 65 ko sama da haka. Matsakaicin girman gida shine 2.78 kuma matsakaicin girman dangi shine 3.25.
A cikin garin an bazu yawan jama'a, tare da 33.8% a ƙarƙashin shekaru 18, 2.6% daga 18 zuwa 24, 32.0% daga 25 zuwa 44, 24.7% daga 45 zuwa 64, da 6.9% waɗanda shekarunsu suka kai 65 ko sama da haka. . Matsakaicin shekarun ya kasance shekaru 35. Ga kowane mata 100, akwai maza 106.3. Ga kowane mata 100 masu shekaru 18 zuwa sama, akwai maza 121.7.
Matsakaicin kuɗin shiga na gida a cikin garin shine $35,625, kuma matsakaicin kuɗin shiga na dangi shine $45,000. Maza suna da matsakaicin kudin shiga na $30,179 sabanin $26,875 na mata. Kudin shiga kowane mutum na garin shine $18,092. Kimanin kashi 12.5% na iyalai da 10.8% na yawan jama'a sun kasance ƙasa da layin talauci, gami da 10.9% na waɗanda basu kai shekara sha takwas ba kuma babu ɗaya daga cikin waɗanda 65 ko sama da haka.
