Ismail ibn Musa Menk .
An haife shi a garin harare dake kasar yemen. inda ya cigaba da karatu tareda mahaifinsa moulana Musa inda yake haddan Alqur`ani mai girma tare da koyan larabci. bayan nan sai ya tafi kimiyyar St.John`s . dan yin makarantar gaba da primare. kuma ya zamo kwararre a bangaren fiqhu ta mazahabar hambali a jami`ar musulunci ta madina. kuma ya kasance basalafe ne, amma bai bayyanar da cewa shi basalafe ne a tafiyar ba.
