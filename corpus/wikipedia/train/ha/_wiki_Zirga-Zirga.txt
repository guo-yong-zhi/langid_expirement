Zirga-Zirga na nufin sufuri na mutane da kaya daga wani wuri  zuwa wani wurin na daban wanda a turance akafi sanida suna ]. Hanyoyin Zirga-Zirga sun kasu daban daban, se dai wanda akafi sani ko amfani dasu guda hudune , sune ta-ruwa , ta-iska , ta-titi ko kasa wacce akafi sanida , se kuma titin-jirgin kasa . Sannan kuma akwai wata hanya wacce akafi sanida pipeline transport.
