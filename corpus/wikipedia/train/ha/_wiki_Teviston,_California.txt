Teviston wuri ne da aka keɓe  a cikin Tulare County, California .  Yana da kusan mil 3. kudu da Pixley akan babbar hanya 99 tsakanin Fresno da Bakersfield. Teviston yana zaune a tsayin 272 feet  .  Kididdiga ta Amurka ta 2010 ta ruwaito yawan Teviston ya kai 1,214.
A cewar Ofishin Kididdiga na Amurka, CDP ya ƙunshi yanki na 2.2 murabba'in mil , duk ta kasa.
A ƙididdigar 2010 Teviston yana da yawan jama'a 1,214. Yawan yawan jama'a ya kasance mutane 559.2 a kowace murabba'in mil . Tsarin launin fata na Teviston ya kasance 449  Fari, 50  Ba'amurke Ba'amurke, 9  Ba'amurke, 10  Asiya, 0  Pacific Islander, 640  daga sauran jinsi, da 56  daga jinsi biyu ko fiye. Hispanic ko Latino na kowace kabila sun kasance mutane 1,039 . 
Akwai gidaje 295, 191  suna da ‘ya’ya ‘yan kasa da shekara 18 suna zaune a cikinsu, 181  ma’auratan maza da mata ne da ke zaune tare, 41  suna da mace mai gida da babu miji, 32  na da magidanci namiji da ba mace a wurin. Akwai 34  marasa aure tsakanin maza da mata, da kuma 0  ma'aurata ko haɗin gwiwa . Magidanta 26  mutum ɗaya ne kuma 11  suna da wanda ke zaune shi kaɗai wanda ya kai 65 ko fiye. Matsakaicin girman gidan ya kasance 4.12. Akwai iyalai 254 ; matsakaicin girman iyali shine 4.31.
Rarraba shekarun ya kasance mutane 477  'yan ƙasa da shekaru 18, mutane 144  masu shekaru 18 zuwa 24, mutane 329  masu shekaru 25 zuwa 44, mutane 193  masu shekaru 45 zuwa 64, da kuma Mutane 71  waɗanda suka kasance 65 ko fiye. Tsakanin shekarun ya kasance shekaru 24.0. Ga kowane mata 100, akwai maza 102.0. Ga kowane mata 100 masu shekaru 18 zuwa sama, akwai maza 104.2.
Akwai rukunin gidaje 352 a matsakaicin yawa na 162.1 a kowace murabba'in mil, na rukunin da aka mamaye 129  sun kasance masu mallakar kuma 166  an yi hayar. Matsakaicin guraben aikin gida shine 7.2%; Yawan aikin haya ya kasance 5.1%. Mutane 500  sun rayu a cikin rukunin gidaje masu mallaka kuma mutane 714  suna zaune a rukunin gidajen haya.
A cikin watan Yuni na 2021 famfon ruwan sha na Teviston ya karye ya bar garin ba tare da ruwan sha ba a lokacin fari mafi muni na California.
