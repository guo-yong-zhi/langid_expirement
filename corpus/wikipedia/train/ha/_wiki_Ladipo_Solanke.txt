Ladipo Solanke  ɗan gwagwarmayar siyasa ne haifaffen Najeriya wanda ya yi kamfen kan al'amuran Afirka ta Yamma.
Haihuwa da ilimi
An haife shi a Abeokuta, Najeriya, kamar Oladipo Felix Solanke, ya yi karatu a Kwalejin Fourah Bay da ke Saliyo kafin ya koma karatun koyan aikin Lauya a Jami’ar Landan, a 1922.
A Biritaniya, Solanke ya shiga Unionungiyar Daliban Desan Afirka. A cikin 1924, ya rubuta wa Afirka ta Yamma don yin korafi game da labarin a cikin Labarin Maraice, wanda ya yi ikirarin cewa cin naman mutane da bakar sihiri ya zama ruwan dare a Nijeriya har zuwa shekarun baya-bayan nan. Zanga-zangar tasa ta samu goyon bayan Amy Ashwood Garvey, wanda ya zama babban aboki, yayin da Solanke ya fara nazarin takardun Ingila don wasu rahotanni na cin mutunci.
Samun kansa cikin talauci, Solanke ya fara koyar da Yarbanci kuma ya fusata da rashin sha'awar al'adun gargajiyar Najeriya tsakanin sauran ɗaliban Najeriya a Landan. A watan Yunin 1924, ya zama mutum na farko da ya fara watsa shirye-shirye ta rediyo a yaren Yarbanci. Watan da ya biyo baya, tare da karfafawa Garvey, Solanke tare da wasu ɗalibai goma sha biyu suka kafa Progressungiyar Ci Gaban Nijeriya don inganta rayuwar ɗaliban Nijeriya.
A cikin 1925, Solanke da Herbert Bankole-Bright sun kafa Studentsungiyar Studentsaliban Afirka ta Yamma  a matsayin ci gaban zamantakewar jama'a, al'adu da siyasa ga ɗaliban Afirka ta Yamma a Biritaniya. Ya zama Babban Sakataren kungiyar kuma babban mai ba da gudummawa ga mujallar ta, Wasu. A cikin 1926, ya yi rikodin kiɗa a Yarbanci don Zonophone, kuma a cikin 1927, ya buga Westasar Afirka ta Yamma a Barungiyar lauyoyi ta Nationsasashe, yana kira ga ’yan Afirka su ji daɗin zaɓen duniya. Solanke ya kuma jagoranci yunƙurin WASU don gina gidan kwanan 'yan Afirka ta Yamma a Landan.
