Asalinta ita ce za ta kasance mai samar da Yarinyar Yarinya a Transit amma ta kasance jagora. Tun daga lokacin 1, Abimbola ya ci gaba don ba kawai yin jagoranci ba amma har ma ya samar da Season 2 - Season 6 na SGIT. Craig a halin yanzu yana aiki a matsayin Shugaban Production a Ndani Communications, wanda kuma aka fi sani da Ndani TV yana gabatar da shirye-shirye ciki har da Skinny Girl a Transit, Phases, Rumor Has It da The Juice ETC.
