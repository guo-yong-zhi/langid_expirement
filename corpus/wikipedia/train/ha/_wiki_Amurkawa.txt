Amurkawa guda daya Ba'amurke ko Ɗan Amurka, mace Ba'amurkiya ko Yar'Amurka, da yawa  kuma Yan'Amurka. Sune Mutanan yan'ƙasa kuma  mazaunan Tarayyar Kasashen Amurka. 1  Dukda yan'ƙasan da mazauna sune mafi yawan Amurkawa,akwai wasu dake da shaidar zama dan'ƙasa da dama, expatriates, da permanent residents, suna Amurkawa ne. 2  3  ƙasar Tarayyar Amurka takasance gida ce ga mutanen jinsi da al'adu daban-daban. Haka yasa, Al'adar Amurka da dokan zama dan'ƙasa na tarayyar Amurka bai daidaituwa da nationality tare da jinsi ko ethnicity, amma dai tareda citizenship da kuma allegiance na din-din-din. 4  5  6  
Masu magana da Harshen Turanci, dama wasu masu magana da wasu harsunan, suna amfani da sunan "Yan'Amurka" da nufin kawai mutum daga kasar Amurka;wannan yasamo asali ne din asalin amfani da kalmar dan ban-bance mutanen dake amfani da turanci na American colonies daga asalin mutanen ingila. 7  Kalmar "Amurkawa" na'iya nufin mutane dake daga Americas. 8  .
