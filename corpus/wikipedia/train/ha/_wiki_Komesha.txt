Komesha na daya daga cikin gundumomi 20 na kasar Habasha, ko kuma gundumomi, a yankin Benishangul-Gumuz na kasar Habasha. Wani ɓangare na shiyyar Asosa, yana iyaka da Asosa a kudu, Kurmuk a arewa maso yamma da Menge a gabas. Mafi girman mazauni a wannan gundumar shine Komesha. Sansanin 'yan gudun hijira na Sherkole yana ɗauke da mutane 9,526 da suka rasa matsugunansu daga Sudan da Sudan ta Kudu, kuma yana cikin Komesha. 
Kididdiga ta kasa ta shekarar 2007 ta bayar da rahoton jimillar yawan jama'a na wannan gundumar mai mutane 21,744, daga cikinsu 11,219 maza ne, 10,525 kuma mata; 875 ko 4.02% na yawan jama'arta mazauna birni ne. Yawancin mazaunan sun ce su Musulmai ne, inda kashi 69.28% na al'ummar kasar suka bayar da rahoton cewa sun lura da wannan akida, yayin da kashi 19.03% na mutanen Furotesta ne, kashi 7.03% na Katolika ne, kuma kashi 4.39% na addinin kiristanci na Habasha ne. 
Bisa kididdigar da hukumar CSA ta samu a shekarar 2005, wannan gundumar tana da adadin yawan jama'a 12,948, daga cikinsu 6,613 maza ne, 6,335 kuma mata ne. Tare da kimar fadin kilomita murabba'i 645.78, Komesha tana da yawan jama'a 20.1 a kowace murabba'in kilomita wanda ya fi matsakaicin yanki na 19.95. 
Ƙididdigar ƙasa ta 1994 ta ba da rahoton jimillar yawan jama'a na wannan yanki na 9,762 a cikin gidaje 2,261, waɗanda 5,008 maza ne kuma 4,754 mata; ba a rubuta mazaunan birni ba. Kabila mafi girma da aka ruwaito a Komesha ita ce Berta mai kashi 99.5% na yawan jama'a; daidai rabon Berta ya yi magana , kuma 99.5% na yawan jama'a sun ce su musulmi ne . Game da ilimi, 15.97% na yawan jama'a an dauke su masu karatu, wanda bai kai matsakaicin yanki na 18.49%; 14.29% na yara masu shekaru 7-12 suna makarantar firamare; 1.01% na yara masu shekaru 13-14 sun kasance a makarantar sakandaren ƙarami; da kuma ƙarancin adadin mazaunan shekaru 15-18 sun kasance a babbar makarantar sakandare. Dangane da yanayin tsafta, kashi 2.8% na dukkan gidaje suna da tsaftataccen ruwan sha, kuma kashi 16.8% na da wuraren bayan gida a lokacin da ake ƙidayar.
