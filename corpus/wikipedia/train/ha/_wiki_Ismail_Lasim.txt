Ismail bin Lasim É—an siyasan Malaysia ne wanda ya yi aiki a matsayin memba na Majalisar Dokokin Jihar Negeri Sembilan  na Juasseh daga Mayu 2018 zuwa Agusta 2023 kuma na Senaling daga Nuwamba 1999 zuwa Mayu 2018. Ya yi aiki a matsayin memba na Majalisar Zartarwa ta Jihar Negeri Sembilan  a cikin gwamnatin jihar Barisan Nasional  a karkashin tsohon Menteri Besar Mohamad Hasan daga Maris 2004 zuwa faduwar gwamnatin jihar BN a watan Mayu 2018. Shi memba ne na United Malays National Organisation , wani bangare na jam'iyyar BN.
