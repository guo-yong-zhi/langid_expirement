Mound Prairie Township birni ne, da ke a gundumar Houston, Minnesota, Amurka. Yawan jama'a ya kasance 661 a ƙidayar 2000.
An shirya Garin Mound Prairie a cikin 1860, kuma an sanya masa suna bayan wani tudu da ke kusa da kewaye. 
Dangane da Ofishin Kididdiga ta Amurka, garin yana da yawan yanki na 36.8 murabba'in mil , wanda 36.2 murabba'in mil  kasa ce kuma 0.5 murabba'in mil   ruwa ne.
Dangane da ƙidayar  na 2000, akwai mutane 661, gidaje 228, da iyalai 186 da ke zaune a cikin garin. Yawan yawan jama'a ya kasance mutane 18.2 a kowace murabba'in mil . Akwai rukunin gidaje 233 a matsakaicin yawa na 6.4/sq mi . Tsarin launin fata na garin ya kasance 98.64% Fari, 0.15% Ba'amurke, 0.45% Asiya, da 0.76% daga jinsi biyu ko fiye. Hispanic ko Latino na kowace kabila sun kasance 0.15% na yawan jama'a.
Akwai gidaje 228, daga cikinsu kashi 42.1% na da ‘ya’ya ‘yan kasa da shekara 18 suna zaune tare da su, kashi 68.4% ma’aurata ne da ke zaune tare, kashi 6.1% na da mace mai gida babu miji, kashi 18.0% kuma ba iyali ba ne. Kashi 12.7% na dukkan gidaje sun kasance na mutane ne, kuma 5.3% suna da wanda ke zaune shi kaɗai wanda ya kai shekaru 65 ko fiye. Matsakaicin girman gidan ya kasance 2.90 kuma matsakaicin girman dangi shine 3.19.
A cikin garin yawan jama'a ya bazu, tare da 31.0% a ƙasa da shekaru 18, 7.0% daga 18 zuwa 24, 30.4% daga 25 zuwa 44, 23.8% daga 45 zuwa 64, da 7.9% waɗanda shekarunsu suka kai 65 ko sama da haka. . Tsakanin shekarun ya kasance shekaru 36. Ga kowane mata 100, akwai maza 110.5. Ga kowane mata 100 masu shekaru 18 zuwa sama, akwai maza 110.1.
Matsakaicin kuɗin shiga na gida a cikin garin shine $50,234, kuma matsakaicin kuɗin shiga na dangi shine $52,159. Maza suna da matsakaicin kudin shiga na $32,969 sabanin $26,667 na mata. Kudin shiga kowane mutum na garin shine $19,487. Kusan 1.6% na iyalai da 2.4% na yawan jama'a sun kasance ƙasa da layin talauci, gami da babu ɗaya daga cikin waɗanda ke ƙasa da shekaru 18 da 9.0% na waɗanda shekarunsu suka kai 65 ko sama da haka.
