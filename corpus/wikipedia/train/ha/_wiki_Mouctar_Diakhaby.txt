Mouctar Diakhaby  ƙwararren ɗan ƙwallon ƙafa ne wanda ke taka leda a Valencia a gasar La Liga. An haife shi a Faransa, yana buga wa tawagar kasar Guinea wasa. Ya kuma wakilci kungiyoyin matasa na kasar Faransa da suka fara daga kungiyar kwallon kafa ta Faransa ta kasa da shekaru 19. 1 
Diakhaby ya buga wasansa na farko a kungiyar Lyon a ranar 10 ga Satumba 2016 a gasar Ligue 1 da Bordeaux, ya buga wasan gaba daya a gida da ci 3-1. 2   A ranar 30 ga watan Nuwamba 2016, ya zira kwallonsa ta farko a gasar Lyon a wasan farko da suka tashi 6-0 Ligue 1 na Nantes. 3   A ranar 23 ga watan Fabrairu 2017, Diakhaby ya zira kwallo a raga  a cikin shekarar 2016-17 Europa League zagaye na 32 na biyu 7-1 nasara a gida a kan AZ Alkmaar a yin rajistar aikinsa na farko na UEFA Europa League ko UEFA Champions League burin. 4  Ya kuma zura kwallo a raga a cikin kowane kafa biyu na 2016–17 Europa League zagaye na 16 da suka yi da Roma.
A ranar 28 ga watan Yuni 2018, Diakhaby ya sanya hannu kan yarjejeniyar shekaru biyar tare da Valencia ta Spain. 5 
An haifi Diakhaby a Faransa kuma dan asalin kasar Guinea ne.  Shi tsohon matashi ne na duniya a Faransa. Hower, a cikin 2022, ya yanke shawarar wakiltar ƙasar mahaifansa, Guinea kuma ya yi musu muhawara a kan Masar. 6
