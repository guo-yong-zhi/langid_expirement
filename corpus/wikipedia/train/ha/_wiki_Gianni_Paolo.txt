Gianni Paolo 1 
Gianni Vincent Paolo an haife shi a ranar 27 ga watan Mayu a shekarar 1996, ɗan wasan kwaikwayo ne Ba’amurke, marubuci kuma darekta sananne don taka rawar Brayden Weston akan "Power" da jujjuyawar sa da mabiyi, Power Book II: Ghost. Ya kuma shahara wajen taka rawar Chaz a cikin  fim mai ban tsoro Ma a shekarar 2019.
