Dam ɗin Mokolo,  wani nau'in madatsar ruwa ne da ke kan kogin Mokolo, kusa da Lephalale, Limpopo, Afirka ta Kudu . An kafa shi a shekarar 1980.  1  Kogin Malmanies da kogin Bulspruit, magudanan ruwa biyu na Mokolo, su ma suna shiga dam daga gefen hagu. Dam ɗin yana samar da ruwa ga garin Lephalale . Dam ɗin ya fi yin aiki ne don dalilai na birni da masana'antu kuma an sanya haɗarin haɗarinsa a matsayi mai girma na .
