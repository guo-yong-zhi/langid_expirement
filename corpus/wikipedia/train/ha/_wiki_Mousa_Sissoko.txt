Moussa Sissoko 1 
Moussa Sissoko haifaffenn dan kwallon kafa neh dan Faransa a 16 ga watan Agusta a shekarar 1989.  Yana taka leda a matsayin dan wasan tsakiya na kungiyar  Nantes a Ligue 1. Yana wasa a matsayin dan wasan tsakiya na akwatin-zuwa a tsakiyar filin, kuma yana da ikon yin wasa a kowane tsakiyar tsakiya.  rawar, ko ma a matsayin mai kai hari na tsakiya, gefen dama ko dama.
