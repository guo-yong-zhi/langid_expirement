A watan  Afrailu na Oktoba an saki Nnamdi daga fursuna akan beli, amma tun daga nan bai dawo ba kuma an kulle shi fiye da shekara dukda samun umurnin kotu daban daban da yayi na a sake shi Sanda yake kotu, yabayyana akai akai cikin tufafin ibadar Yahudawa na Jewish prayer shida lullubin fuskar Ya fadi a kotun cewa yayi  imani da in Judaikuma yana kallon kansa amatsayin bayahude kuma yakan jagoranci mutanensa na his Bi danyin ibadar yahudawa da wasu abubuwan addinoni. 6  a ga watan  afirilun a shekara ta ,ansaka nnamdi kanu a gidan gyara hali wato fursuna . 7 
A ranar, ga watan Oktoba, a shekara ta , Nnamdi Kanu ya fara shari’ar sa. An kama shi a karshen watan Yuni a Kenya sannan aka dawo da shi Najeriya inda aka daure shi. Nnamdi Kanu ya tsere daga kasar a shekara ta ,  bayan harin da jami’an tsaro suka kai gidansa. A ranar,  ga watan Oktoba shekara ta,  an sake kai shugaban ƴan aware na Najeriya wato Nnamdi Kanu a babbar kotun Abuja ya fuskantar shari'a akan tuhumar sa da ake da ƙoƙarin tada zaune tsaye da kuma taka haƙƙin Ɗan Adam sai dai an sake maida shi gidan gyara hali ,  bayan zaman da akayi ba'a cimma wata yarjejeniya ba. 8
