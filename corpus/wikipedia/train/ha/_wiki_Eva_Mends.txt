Ta halarci Jami'ar Ghana, inda ta sami digiri a Kimiyyar Siyasa da Tattalin Arziki. Tana da digirin digirgir a fannin Gudanar da Jama'a daga Ghana Institute of Management and Public Administration. A Jami'o'in Harvard da Duke, ta ɗauki wasu gajerun darussa. 1  3  4  6 
Mends ta shiga Ma'aikatar Kudi a matsayin mai hidimar kasa a shekarar 1991 kuma tun daga wannan lokacin ta yi aiki a fannoni daban -daban da suka hada da, Shugaban Kwamitin Amurkan a 1998, Shugaban Ci gaban Kasafin Kudi a 2006 da Shugaban Rukunin Manufofin da Gudanar da Harkokin Jama'a a 2013. A watan Mayun 2017, an nada ta daraktan kasafin kudi a ma'aikatar kudi, mukamin da ta rike har zuwa watan Janairun 2019. An san ta ta jagoranci manyan sauye -sauye da suka haɗa da kasafin kuɗi mai dacewa da jinsi, Ƙaddamar da Tsarin Shirin da haɓaka sabuwar Dokar Gudanar da Kuɗi ta Jama'a.
