 
Munawar Iqbal Faruqui  ɗan wasan barkwanci ne kuma ɗan wasan rap na Indiya.  A shekarar 2022 ya fito a matsayin dan takara a shirin Kangana Ranaut na gaskiya na gidan talabijin na Lock Upp Season 1 kuma ya fito a matsayin wanda ya lashe wasan. 
An haifi Faruqui a Junagadh, Gujarat,   a ranar 28 ga watan Janairu shekarata alif  1992 ga dangin musulmin Indiya.  Iyalinsa sun ƙaura zuwa Mumbai bayan da aka lalata gidansa a lokacin rikicin kabilanci na Gujarat a 2002 kuma mahaifiyarsa ta mutu ba da daɗewa ba, yana ɗan shekara 13. 
Sa’ad da yake ɗan shekara 13, an tilasta masa ya yi wa iyalinsa aiki sa’ad da mahaifinsa ya yi rashin lafiya. Ya yi aiki a kantin sayar da kayan aiki yayin da yake makaranta.  
A cikin farkon shekarunsa ashirin, ya yi aiki a matsayin mai zanen hoto . A cikin shekarar 2017, lokacin da dandamali daban-daban na OTT ke gabatarwa a Indiya ya sami masaniyar wasan barkwanci kuma ya fara aiki a matsayin ɗan wasan barkwanci.  
A cikin watan Afrilu Shekarata 2020, ya sanya wani bidiyo mai ban dariya mai suna " Dawood, Yamraaj & Aurat " a tasharsa, wanda ya ba shi damar zama tauraro na farko.  Ya fitar da waƙarsa ta farko " Jawab " tare da haɗin gwiwar wani mawaƙin Indiya Spectra a watan Agusta shekarata 2020.
A ranar 28 ga watan Fabrairu shekarata 2021, ya loda wani bidiyo mai ban dariya mai suna "Labarin fatalwa" a tashar YouTube. 
A shekarar 2022 ya zama dan takara a shirin talabijin na gaskiya na Kangana Ranaut Lock Up .   A ranar 8 ga watan Mayu shekarata 2022 an ayyana shi a matsayin wanda ya yi nasara a wasan.
