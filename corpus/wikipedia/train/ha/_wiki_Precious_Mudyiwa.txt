Precious Mudyiwa  ɗan wasan ƙwallon ƙafa ne ɗan ƙasar Zimbabwe wanda ke taka leda a matsayin mai tsaron gida ga Black Rhinos Queens FC da kuma ƙungiyar mata ta ƙasar Zimbabwe .
Precious Mudyiwa ya buga wa kulob din Black Rhinos Queens na Zimbabwe wasa a Gasar Cin Kofin Mata ta CAF ta shekarar 2021 COSAFA Qualifiers . 
Mudyiwa ya taka leda a Zimbabwe a babban mataki yayin gasar COSAFA ta mata ta shekarar 2021 .
