Agbarha Otor  Gari ne, kuma ɗaya daga cikin masarautun Urhobo ƙaramar hukumar Ughelli ta Arewa jihar Delta. Garin gida ne ga attajirai kuma mashahuran dangin Ibru. Bayan makarantun firamare da sakandare, Agbarha-Otor yana da jami'a mai zaman kanta, wato Jami'ar Michael da Cecilia Ibru da wani filin jirgin sama mai zaman kansa wanda Michael Ibru ya gina a shekarar 1972 wanda Michael Ibru  ya gina kuma yana dauke da bataliya ta 222 na sojojin Najeriya da kuma SuperBru,kamfanin samar da malt.
