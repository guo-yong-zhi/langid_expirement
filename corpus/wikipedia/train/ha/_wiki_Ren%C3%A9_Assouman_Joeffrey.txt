René Assouman Joeffrey ɗan wasan ƙwallon ƙafa ne na ƙasar Rwanda wanda a halin yanzu yana taka leda a Hillerød IF na ƙungiyar Danish 2nd Division, da kuma tawagar ƙasar Rwanda. 1  
An haifi Joeffrey a Rwanda  mahaifinsa ɗan DR Congo da Mahaifiyarsa 'yar Ruwanda. Iyalinsa sun ƙaura zuwa Denmark lokacin yana ɗan shekara tara. Ya fara buga kwallon kafa tun yana dan shekara sha biyu.
A cikin watan Yuli 2021 Joeffrey ya karɓi kiransa na farko na ƙasa da ƙasa a Rwanda gabanin gasar 2021 CECAFA U-23 Challenge Cup. Koyaya, Rwanda ta fice daga gasar saboda cutar ta COVID-19. 2   An kira shi zuwa babban tawagar a watan Disamba mai zuwa don wasan sada zumunci da Guinea a cikin Janairu 2022. 3   Ya yi babban wasansa na farko a duniya a ranar 3 ga Janairu 2022 a wasan farko na jerin.
