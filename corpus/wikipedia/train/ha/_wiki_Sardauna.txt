WANNAN SHAFIN ZA'A GOGE SHI! . Dalili: Babu bayani mai ma'ana, ko Talla ko rashin Manazarta
Idan akwai rashin amincewa game da goge shafin, kana iya fadin hujjar ka a Shafin tattaunawa.  Idan wannan shafin bai cancanci gogewa ba, kokuma zaka iya gyarawa, to kana iya goge wannan sanarwar, amma kuma kada ka goge sanarwa a shafin da ka kirkira da kanka.
Masu gudanarwa, su tuna da bincika  ko akwai wata mahada a nan da tarihin shafi  kafin a goge shi.
Sardauna shi ne wanda aka fi sani da Sir Ahmadu Bello,wato sardaunan sokoto, yan-mazan jiya wadaanda suka sha gwagwarmaya wajen kwato mana yanci, kuma ya kasance jigo ne a fannin ilimi a wannan kasa ta Najeriya ta hanyar assasa katafariyar jami'ar da ake kira jAMI'AR Ahmadu Bello dake Zariya a cikin garin Samaru wanda wannan ne yasa ake kiran shi 'Namiji Uban Yanboko'. An haifi Sardauna ne a garin Rabah a cikin jahar Sokoto a watan yuli, shekarar alif da dari tara da goma  kuma ya rasu a shekarar alif da dari tara da sittin da shida
