Tijjani Abdullahi Asase wanda aka sani da Tijjani Asase Jarumi ne kuma furodusa wato mai shirya fina-finai a masana'antar Kannywood.
Jarumin ya daɗe ana damawa da shi a masana'antar ta kannywood kuma yayi fice wajen fitowa a matsayin ɗan daba a cikin fina finan hausa na kannywood. Mutane sun fi saninsa da alkunya . Yana da abokanan damawa a wasan hausa na kannywood irn su Kumurci, Momo, Kazaza, Dubadis da sauransu.
Jarumin yayi fina-finai da dama ga wasu daga ciki su ne;
Yana da mata guda daya 1 da `ya`ya tara 9
