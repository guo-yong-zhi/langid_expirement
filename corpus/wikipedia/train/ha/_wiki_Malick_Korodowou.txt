Abdou-Malick Korodowou  tsohon ɗan wasan ƙwallon ƙafa ne ɗan ƙasar Togo, wanda yake taka leda a matsayin ɗan wasa mai tsaron gida. Ya buga wasa daya ga tawagar kasar Togo a shekarar 2010.  1 
An haife shi a Lomé, Korodowou ya fara aikinsa tare da Planète Foot  2  kuma ya sanya hannu a lokacin rani 2006 a AS Cannes.    3  Bayan bayyana hudu kawai a cikin shekaru biyu na AS Cannes a cikin Championnat National ya sanya hannu a kulob ɗin FC Wiltz 71 a cikin kwallon kafa na Luxembourger. 4  Ya buga wasanni shida a cikin kakar 2010 – 11 a Wiltz a cikin BGL Ligue kuma ya dawo a lokacin rani 2011 zuwa Faransa Championnat de France amateur club Monts d'Or Azergues Foot.  5 
Korodowou ya buga gasar cin kofin duniya na 'yan kasa da shekaru 17 a shekara ta shekarar 2007 a Koriya ta Kudu yana da shekaru goma sha biyar.  6  A ranar 13 ga watan Mayu 2010, ya sami kiransa na farko a tawagar kasar Togo don gasar cin kofin Corsica 2010 kuma ya fara buga wasa a ranar 21 ga watan Mayu 2010 da Gabon.
