Tawagar wasan badminton na kasar Maroko  tana wakiltar Maroko a gasar wasan badminton na kasa da kasa kuma kungiyar Badminton Royal ta Morocco ce ke sarrafa shi.  1  Tawagar kasar na yin atisaye a babban birnin kasar Casablanca. Tawagar Morocco ta halarci gasar cin kofin Sudirman a shekarun 1995 da 2001.
Tawagar maza ta kai wasan daf da na kusa da na karshe a gasar cin kofin duniya ta maza da mata ta Afirka ta shekarar 2018 amma ta sha kashi a hannun Najeriya da ci 0-3.
