Datuk Mohd Nasir bin Ibrahim Fikri  ya kasance memba na majalisar dokokin Malaysia. An zabe shi a majalisar dokoki na mazabar Kuala Nerus a Terengganu a shekara ta 2008, a matsayin memba na jam'iyyar United Malays National Organisation  a cikin hadin gwiwar Barisan Nasional. 1  An kayar da shi don sake zaben a shekarar 2013 da kuri'u 610. Kujerar ta fadi ga Mohd Khairuddin Aman Razali na Jam'iyyar Musulunci ta Pan-Malaysian . 2
