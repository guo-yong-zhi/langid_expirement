Hasumiyar Sansanin William yana kan Dutsen Dawson a tsohuwar cibiyar Cape Coast, a Yankin Tsakiyar Ghana. Ana kiran Hasumiyar Masarautar Cape Coast, kodayake ba ta cikin hadaddun Castle na Cape Coast. Yana da nisan mil 600  daga Masarautar. 1  2 
Burtaniya karkashin Gwamna Hope-Smith ne ya fara gina ginin a 1820, kuma aka sanya masa suna Smith's Tower. A cikin shekarun 1830, an sake gina shi tare da ƙarin kayan dindindin kuma an sake masa suna Sansanin William. Tun daga wannan lokacin, yana aiki azaman hasumiya. 3 
A halin yanzu, ginin yana ɗauke da ma'aikatan Gidan adana kayan tarihi na Ghana . Ana kula da Sansanin William kuma yana buɗe wa baƙi.
