NYPD 1 
Sashen 'Yan sanda na Birnin New York , bisa hukuma Birnin Sashen 'yan sanda na New York, ita ce babbar hukumar tabbatar da doka a cikin Birnin New York.  An kafa shi a ranar 23 ga Mayu, 1845, NYPD ita ce mafi girma, kuma ɗaya daga cikin tsofaffi, sassan 'yan sanda na birni a Amurka.
NYPD tana ɗaukar mutane sama da 50,000, gami da jami'ai sama da 35,000 marasa kayan aiki watau wadanda basu saka . Dangane da tushen bayanan CompStat na hukuma, NYPD ta amsa rahotanni kusan 500,000 na laifuka kuma ta kama sama da 200,000 a cikin 2019. A cikin 2020, tana da kasafin kuɗi na dalar Amurka biliyan 6. Koyaya, ainihin kashe kuɗi na NYPD yakan wuce kasafin kuɗinta.
