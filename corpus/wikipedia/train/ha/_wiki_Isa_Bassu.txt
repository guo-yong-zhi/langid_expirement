 
Issam Bassou  ɗan wasan judoka ne na kasar Moroko. Ya lashe lambobin zinare a wasannin Afirka na 2019 da kuma gasar Judo ta Afirka ta shekarar 2018 a bangaren maza–60 kg.  1   2  Dan uwansa Imad Bassou shima ɗan wasan judoka ne.  3 
A cikin shekarar 2020, ya lashe lambar azurfa a gasar tseren kilo 60 na maza a gasar Judo ta Afirka ta 2020 da aka gudanar a Antananarivo, Madagascar. 4  
A gasar Judo ta Afirka na shekarar 2021 da aka gudanar a Dakar, Senegal, ya lashe lambar zinare a gasar tseren kilo 60 na maza. 5  
Ya lashe daya daga cikin lambobin tagulla a gasar tseren kilo 60 na maza a gasar Mediterranean ta shekarar 2022 da aka gudanar a Oran, Algeria. 6
