Lakshadweep ko Laccadive ko Laquedives yanki ne, da ke a tekun Lakshadweep, a ƙasar Indiya. Yana da yawan fili kimanin kilomita murabba’i 32 da yawan jama’a 70,365 . Yankin tarayyar Indiya ce daga shekara ta 1956. Babban birnin yanki da birnin mafi girman yanki Kavaratti ne. Dineshwar Sharma shi ne shugaban yanki.
