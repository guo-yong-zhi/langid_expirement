
Imɣi ,  d tagrawt tagejdant n imuddiren amdya: isekla, ijeǧǧigen, Tuga, Timedkin d ifilkuten.
Ttugzamen yemɣan ar yemɣan imsifsan, imɣi aragran d ifilkuten.
Deg 2011 tetwafser-d yiwet n tɣuri d akken llan-t 8.6 n imelyan n telmas g tgelda n yemɣan, 6,5 n imelyan deg telmas-a ttidiren-t deg wakal, 2,2 n imelyan n telmas ttidiren-t deg yilel, Ar useggas n 2004 ttwasismlen wazal n 287,655 n telmas, Deg-sent 258,650 ǧǧuǧugen ma d 15,000 n telmas d iragranen, imɣan d imuddiren imsečmanen .
