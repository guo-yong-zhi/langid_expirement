Victor Hugo d amyaru afransis,  ilul di Besançon ass n 26 di furar 1802, yemmut di Paris ass n 22 mayu 1885. Yella d anaggal, d amedyaz, d amsezgan am akken yekcem daɣen deg tudert tasertant d tmeɣnest.
Yettuneḥsab d yiwen seg imyura imeqqranen maḍi n tutlayt tafransist, yettwassen ladɣa s wungal-is mucaɛen di ddunit  Imeɣban   i yesseẓreg deg usegass n 1862. Ɣer tama n tira yelha-d d waṭas n lecɣal-iḍen izuyaz am umennuɣ ɣef yizerfan n wemdan, tugart ayen yerzan tafgurt s tmettant .
