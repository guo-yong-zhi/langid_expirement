Di tmura n yitargiyen amnukal  neɣ tamnukalt ma d tameṭṭut  , d aɣella n tgelda tatergit , yettwafran seg yimawlan n umnukal i t-id-yezwaren . Ttaken-as daɣen aɣara n aṭbel  am ucraḍ i uglad-is .. Awal-agi yezmer ad yemmel " amenkad " . Amnukal atergi yettwasnen aṭas d tagellidt Tin hinan , imi d tamezzwarut i wumi yettunefka uɣara-agi .
