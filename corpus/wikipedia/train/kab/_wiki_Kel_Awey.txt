Kel Awey  D takunfidralit tatergit . Seg tasut tis 18 armi d anekcum afransis deg tazzwara n tasut tis 20 , llan d agdud bu tezmert deg yidurar n Ayer deg ugafa alemmas n Nnijer . 
Llan wat Kel Awey , akka am yiderman n yitergiyen nniḍen , d adrum meqqren yernan tiwaculin nniḍen . Deg 1740 , usan-d wat kel Awey ɣer unẓul seg Lezzayer n wass-a thudd tamdint n Assudi , tuker Agadiz , terra taselṭanit n Agadiz s ddaw n tmehla-nsen sebḍan at Kel Ayr ɣer unẓul d umalu . Tella tkunfidralit s ddaw n tmehla n ugdud n Anastafidet , amɛellem n wat Kel Awey .
