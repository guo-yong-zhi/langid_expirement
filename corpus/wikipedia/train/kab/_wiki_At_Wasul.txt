At Wasul neɣ Imedraren d yiwet n tedfert tamaziɣt tineslemt i isneɣden neɣ yeṭṭfen kra n imuren seg Umeṛṛuk yerna tessers tamnaṭ n Isgelmasen d tamenrut-is. Ɣas akken akerwa-ines amenzu d Belqasem Semku, maca amenhar amezwar adettan n tgeldit-a d Ɛisa At Yazid i d-tt-iṭṭfen i wazal n 15 n iseggasen .
Tageldit n At Wasul tezga teqqim azal n snat n tsutin alammi i d-ten-yerna  Ɛubayd Allah Ulmahdi i inɣan amenhar aneggaru deg-sen .
