Afus wan aɣil   deg tuget n tikwal, iga d itri wis mraw  netta yettfejjijen s waṭas di tegnawt n yiḍ, akken i iga d itri afejejja wis sin deg tezdemt n umanar s timmad-is. Afus wan aɣil ittumeggez d itri amanfal, azal-is amaskad igda +0.0 d +1.6.
Afus wan aɣil d itri yettusesmel d acafcal azeggaɣ seg yiylalen n telmest M 2-1. Afus wan aɣil iga d yiwen seg yitran meqren, yettwaskaden s tiṭṭ, ma nessugen-it-id d ammas n unagraw anafuk, ad tili tiggi-ines beṛṛa n ubaggus n izunyuren, anda ara ad yelmeẓ imtiwgen n uziweɣ d tetrit d tegnit d Mezweɣ, akken i yezmer daɣen ad yelmeẓ timezzit n Yebder.
