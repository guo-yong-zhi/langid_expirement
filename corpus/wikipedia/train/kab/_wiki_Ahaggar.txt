Idurar n Ahaggar  d yiwen azrar n idurar i d-ezgan di tlemmast n ] swaswa deg unẓul asemran n tmurt n Lezzayer. Tajumma-ines tessaweḍ ɣer 550.000 km² , yerna yeẓẓel ɣef usaneṭ n uxenẓir i d-t-ibeṭṭun ɣef ugafa.
Imucaɣ neɣ Imajeɣen  ssawalen i yidurar-nni , D isem i d-yekkan seg wawal Ahaggar  i wumi qqaren Ihaggaren  gar Imucaɣ n temnaṭ-a.
Tamnaṭ tamedrart n Uhaggar ɣur-es tajumma azal n 540 000 km2, ugar tin n Fransa. 
Taqacuct n yidurar-a tella d Tahat, deg 3004 lmitrat af yilel.
