Amussu i ufraniman n tmurt n Yiqbayliyen  ilul-d deg wayyur n Yunyu 2001, sin akin yuɣal yisem-is Amussu i uguccelman n tamurt n Iqbayliyen send mi i d-tella temlilit n useqqamu-ines aɣelnaw deg wass n 4 tuber 2013 di taddart n Wat Ḥemdun. Mak d amussu amunan Aqbayli i d-yekkren deg tlemmast n tedyanin d tegrawla yettwasemman tafsut Taberkant di tmurt Taqbaylit gar n 2001 d 2003. Amussu-a yeqqar-d ɣef yiman-is d akken-it d amelwi, yettnaɣ i wakken ad issuddes aɣdaɣar n uguccelman di tmurt n Yiqbayliyen ara ad tt-yawin ɣer timunent tummidt.
Deg useggas n 2016, Mak tezdi yakk d Anavaḍ Aqvayli Awmiran i wakken ad ssilɣen MAK-ANAVAD.
