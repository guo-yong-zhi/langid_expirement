Asɣunef d tamhalt n usufeɣ n wezwu seg  uɣerɣur aberfaw yellan di turin sin akin ad yekk seg tregwa n wezwu yellan deg unagraw amesgunef send ma ad yeffeɣ ar beṛṛa n tfekka. 1  2  3  Agaz n usɣunef d win yeččuren s Asinuksid n ukaṛbun . Dɣa agaz-a yettmag-d mi ara ad tzerbent tikwatin deg temhalt n usgunef agensas i wfaris n adinusin akredfusfat, Asɣunef d anemgal n uzizzer, snat n tallalin-a xeddmen yakked  i wakken ad aggẓent ɣef umnekni agensas n tfekka
