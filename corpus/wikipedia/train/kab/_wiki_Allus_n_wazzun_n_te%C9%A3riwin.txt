Deg yitilifunen n ufus n GSM/3GSM , talugent n GSM tesbaday-d ingalen n wallus n wazzun n teɣriwin 1  ara d-yettwabedren swadda-a. Tilugan-a d ETSI ay ten-id-igan, ttwabnan ɣef yengalen uṛufiyen izɣen n wallus n wazzun n teɣriwin yerna cuban ingalen ay yettwasseqdacen deg umur ameqran n yizirigen n utilifun urkid 2  deg Tdukli Tuṛufit.
Deg yitilifunen n ufus, tawuri-a tezmer ad tili d aḥric seg asefran 3  n yemsektayen.
Ma yella azwir 9  n tenzaḍt 10  n wallus n wazzun netta d "**" , uṭṭun n utilifun ad yettwakles deg uẓeṭṭa. Lemmer, sdeffir waya, imseqdec ad yessens allus n wazzun s tenzaḍt s yiwen n "#", ad yezmer yemseqdec, sakkin, ad yales ad yessermed allus n wazzun-a s tenzaḍt n "*" war ma yella deg-s wuṭṭun n utilifun. Allus n wazzun ad yales ad yettwassermed deg wuṭṭun ay yettwakelsen deg uẓeṭṭa. Ɣef umedya, lemmer yiwen ad yesseqdec angal n beṛṛa n yiger deg tenzaḍt n wallus n wazzun: 
**62*7035551212#
sakkin ad yessens allus n wazzun: 
#62#
sakkin, ad yezmer ad yales ad yessermed allus n wazzun n beṛṛa n yiger war ma yura uṭṭun n utilifun: 
*62#
Mi ara tettweg tenzaḍt-a ay d-yettwabedren sufella, akk tiɣriwin ara yettwawehhin ɣer utilifun mi ara yili beṛṛa i yiger ad ttwasɛeddint ɣer 7035551212. Yezmer yemseqdec ad yessermed tawuri 11  ɣer kra n wuṭṭun ay yemgerraden ɣef wuṭṭun-nni ay yettwakelsen deg wakud aydeg ara yeǧǧ uṭṭun-nni ay yettwakelsen akken ad t-yesseqdec sakkin. Ɣef umedya, lemmer ad yeg tanzaḍt-a: 
*62*7185551212#
tiɣriwin ad ttwasɛeddint ɣer 7185551212 .  D acu kan, lemmer ad yeg tanzaḍt niḍen: 
*62#
tiɣriwin ad qqlent ad ttwasɛeddayent ɣer wuṭṭun ay yettwakelsen 7035551212 .
Nnig waya, deg yiẓeḍwan n GSM, am wid n T-Mobile d AT&T Mobility deg Yiwunak Yeddukklen d wakk iẓeḍwan n utilifun n ufus deg Tdukli Tuṛufit, yezmer yemseqdec ad d-yesbadu amḍan n tsinin aydeg atilifun ad yesserser uqbel ma yesɛedda tiɣri. Imseqdec yezmer ad d-yesbadu aya s tira n  "*SC*XX" uqbel "#" aneggaru mi ara yaru tanzaḍt n wallus n wazzun, aydeg "SC" d angal n tewsit n umeẓlu , yerna "XX" d amḍan n tsinin ay yettwaḥsaben s 5 n tsinin. Ma yella ad yettwakkes "SC" , dɣa akk tiwsatin n yimeẓla ad ttwasɛeddint. Ɣef umedya, allus n wazzun n teɣri lemmer ur tettili tririt yezmer ad d-yili s: 
*61***#
Deg kra n yiẓeḍwan, tezmer ad tili talast n 30 n tsinin ma aṭas uqbel allus n wazzun .
