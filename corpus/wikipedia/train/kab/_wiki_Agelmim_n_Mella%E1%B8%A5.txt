Agelmim n Mellaḥ d agelmim amerɣan i d-yezga-n deg ugafa n ugezdu n Ṭṭaref . Agelmim-a yezga-d ɣef yiri n Yilel Agrakal anda yebṭa gar-asen yiwen kan n usaka n yijdi ayen yeǧǧan aman-is d imerɣanen
Agelmim-a d adeg anda ttekken waṭas n yigṭaṭ iminigen deg yinig-nsen gar Tferka d Tuṛuft. Tajumma n ugelmim-a 860 km². Tudes ɣer-s yiwet n teẓgi n taydiwin.
