Iflisen Umellil  neɣ Iflisen n Wedrar d taqbilt i-d yezgan deg tama utrim n wezrir n Ǧerǧer. 
Iflisen llan gar yidurar n Tmezrit  d wid n Tigremunt , af izuɣar d yessafen n Sabaw d Yesser.
D taqbilt i yettnaɣ aṭas deg umezruy-yines, imezdaɣ-nsen d imnaɣen mgal Baylik n Iṭṭurken d yirumiyen. 
Irruman ufan-d semmus n temwayin g yidurar n Ǧerǧer gar-asen : Faraxen , Iflensès  d Azus . Qqaran Iflisen yusan-d seg tarwa n Isaflensès neɣ Iflensès.
