Kursika  d tegzirt n Fransa, tezgad deg Ilel Agrakal. Kursika d tegzirt wis sin i meqqren akk deg Ilel Agrakal, deffir Sičilya, Sardinya ed Qubrus, tajumma-ynes 8,681 km² 1 , 2 . Deg 2018, tesɛa 343 726 n yimezdaɣ 2 .
Tezgad deg deg Ilel Agrakal, Kursika teqṛeb 170 km ɣer unẓul n Fransa, 90 km ɣer agafa-umalu n Ṭṭelyan ed 11 km ɣer Sardinya. Tegzirt n Kursika deg-s aṭas n idurar, i tesɛa 20 n tiqucac ugar n 2 000 n lmitrat, adrar n Cinto i d adrar ɣezzifen akk deg tegzirt-nni akked ahat 2 710 n lmitrat 1 .
Deg-s isafen imeqqranen n tegzirt-nni yella : Golo, Tavignano, Liamone, Granove, Tarova ed Profiano. Kursika tesɛa anezwu agraklan, taẓɣelt tanammast d 10,5 °C deg tagrest, ed 21 °C deg anebdu 1 .
