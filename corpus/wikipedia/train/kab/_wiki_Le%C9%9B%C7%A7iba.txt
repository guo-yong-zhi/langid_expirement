Leɛǧiba d yiwet n tɣiwant n Tmurt n Leqbayel i d-yezgan deg twilayt n Tubiret.
Tḥud-itt deg ugafa twilayt n Tizi Wezzu ɣer tama n Wegwilal, deg ugafa-wegmuḍ tella taɣiwant n Ṣaharij ,deg wegmuḍ s tiɣiwanin n Imceddalen ed Weḥnif, deg wenẓul s tiɣiwanin n At Leqṣer ed At Raced, deg utaram  s tɣiwant n Beclul.
