
Buǧamal neɣ Buǧemmal  d yiwet n tewsit n icabrucen yeṭṭafaren tawacult n tcabrucin. Aɣersiw-a yettusesmel deg yiwet n tfesna igebren seg yiwet n twacult kan ur tengir ara, s wakka yettumeggez buǧamal d yiwet seg kra n tewsitin  i d-issiliɣen tawacult n tcabrucin. 
Buǧamal d agḍiḍ iminig llant degs 4 n telmas deg Temrikt akken i llant degs snat n telmas deg umaḍal amzik .
