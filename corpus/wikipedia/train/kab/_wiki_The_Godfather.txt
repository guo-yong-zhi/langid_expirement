
The Godfather d asaru amarikani n tejrimt, yeffeɣ-d deg 1972, yessufeɣ-it-id Francis Ford Coppola i yuran asinaryu akked Mario Puzo, yebna ɣef wungal sɣur Puzo "The Godfather" i d-yeffɣen deg 1969. Asaru-agi ttekkan deg-s yisegbaren am Marlon Brando, Al Pacino, James Caan, Richard Castellano, Robert Duvall, Sterling Hayden, John Marley, Richard Conte, akked Diane Keaton. Tidyanin n teqṣiḍt-agi ḍrant-d di twala seg 1945 ɣer 1955, taqṣiḍt-agi tḥekku ɣef twacult n Corleone i yḍefren Vito Corleone .
