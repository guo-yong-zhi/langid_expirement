Tibenferriwin  d tawacult n yigṭaṭ meẓẓiyen yesčewčiwen. Tawacult-a teṭṭafar tafesna n yijqiwen. Tilmas n Tawacult-a tettent ifsan, Amur ameqran deg-sent ttidiren-t deg wezgen agafan n tegnit.
