Tizzadfarin  d tafesna n ibureɣsen yeṭṭafaren adu-smil n tarfriwin. Asmil-a yebṭa ɣer 4 n twaculin igebren seg 370 n telmas.
Ibureɣsen n tfesna ayi sebṭent, Tifekkiwin-nsent ččurent d tikebrit deg waṭas n tegnatin. Akken i lant iɣunab n umyaraw.
Ibureɣsen n tfesna ayi d urmiden s waṭas. Ttidiren deg yixxamen d tenwalin d ger n yidlisen imziken . Akken i llant telmas niden ttidiren deg yideggan niden beṛṛa n wexxam.
