Aɣeddiw amellal neɣ Aderyun neɣ Taysa  d yiwet n tewsit n yimɣan imseggasen d yimezgiyen yeṭṭafaren tawacult n tetrawt. Aṭas n telmas n tewsit-a yettili-d wuẓu-nsent i wakken ad ilin d imɣan n wedlag. Tawsit-a d tewsitin i as-d-ittilin zrin-d ɣef waṭas n iceggiṛen isensartuyen.
Tilmas n uderyun d tid yeflalen seg 50 alamma d 120 cm. Ageṭṭum-is d ageddisan, d uzzif, yerna ila drus kan n ifurken, ma d iferran-is d imsennenen.
Mi ara ad yejjujug uderyun, tusskawayen ijeǧǧigen-is i wakken ad yili usufeɣ n yiwen n uferdis deg-sen yettwassnen s yiylanen-is isgellsanen, acku ijeǧǧigen n udryun ččuren d tanga n Stiṛul tamɣawt yakk d wuddisen imɣaren yemmugen am kulistiṛul yellan di tfekka. Dɣa iferdisen-a d widak yelhan i taggaẓt n weglim.
