At Wemɛuc  d taɣiwant n Tmurt Leqbayel, tezga deg tawilayt n Bgayet. 
Taɣiwant agi, tezga ar aḍar n udrar Actug. Armud agejdan n tamnaṭ agi, d tafellaḥt . Yerna, tettwassen aṭas s tbexsisin is akkw d zzit uzemmur.
Menɣir Truna, adeg-aɣella ines, taɣiwant n At Wemɛuc tessuddes s tidiganin agi : Bwikni, Tizeɣt, Agemmun, At Waɛmar, Tawrirt, Tiwal, Iɣil Watu, Iɣelden, Ǧebla Kelaɛ, Akur, Tagnit Iɣil, Bubrek, Iɛmaren, Ibudraɛn, Ibaɛzizen, Mzita, Laɛzib U Aɛlwac, Iǧedaren, Amegrud, Awrir, Tiksarin, Taɛzibt, Tala Tinzar, Laɛzib Sidi Sadeq, Iɣzer Ubelluṭ d At Buǧellah.
Tamnaṭ tettwassen i taɣara n tbexsisin ikkawen n At Wemɛuc. Ilaq yiwen attekki, di 1986, ar timzikkent n Cherbourg iwakken tabexsist n tamnaṭ nni at rmes asteɛṛef n imuzzag dɣa terbeḥed arraz amezzwaru deg umsazwar n timzikkent. 1 . Amssifeḍ Tamzali issifeḍ afaris agi alama d taggara n iseggwasen sḍiset n tmerwin , deg 14 timura n Turuft. Tajumma n aqrib 1000ha yeḥeṛṛ ala i usfulleḥ asnudran n tabexsist ikkawen, ammad amseggas yeskazal ar 9000 iqenṭaṛen
