Baga  yella d agellid amaziɣ n Muretanya deg 225 ST . Ur yettwassen ara aṭas , yella d aɛeggal deg ccetla meqqren deg yila .
Agellid Baga Iban-d deg tira n umezruy deg tedyanin n ṭṭrad Abuniqi wis sin . Mi d-yuɣal Masensen ɣer Tefriqt ilaq as ad iɛeddi ya seg tmurt tamasizulit , lɛerc n yiɛdawen-is , neɣ seg wid n Muretanya . Mi yextar tis snat , yuwi yefka-as ugellid Baga amuretani 4000 n ymnayen Imuriyen , i yeqqimen d Masensen armi yekcem tilisa n tmurt-ines . Yefka-d Baga daɣen lemɛawna s yiserdasen deg yixef aneggaru n Ṭṭrad Abuniqi wis Sin deg umennuɣ mgal Ḥannibaɛl .
