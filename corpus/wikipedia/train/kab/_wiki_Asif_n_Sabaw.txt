Asif n Sabaw d asif agejdan i d-yezgan di tmurt n Iqbayliyen, Aɣbalu n wasif-a d idurar n Ǧerǧer yakk d iɣezṛawen i d-yettaken seg yignan niden deg ugezdu n Tizi Wezzu, Yettazzal  alamma yewweṭ  ar Adles anda iy kettem iman-is deg yilel agrakal. Sabaw yefka-d isem-is ar uzaɣar i d-yezger wasif-a, azaɣar-nni anda tella waṭas n tkerrazt  seg Tirẓaẓin ar Tadellest.
Sabaw d amkan adeg aman n Ǧerǧer d Wekfadu ittemlillen. Akk n isfaen ar  ufella d yiɣezran ḍeggren deg wasif n Sabaw akwd Wasif n Messuya di tama n Iferḥunen.
