Gaius Julius Qayser   d nettat ay d amezwaru n ugellid Aṛuman. ilul deg 100 BCE. Yettwaḥseb-it d yiwen seg isertayen imeqranen akk deg Roma Tamezwarut, d yiwen seg yigennalen imeqranen imeqranen akk deg umezruy n umaḍal. Ula d netta yettwassen daɣen ɣef uxeddim-nnes d amasih, d ameskari ed umezruy.
Mi yerbeḥ imnaɣen-nnes aserti deg yiwen n wemgaru n temdint iweɛṛen, Qayser yeḥkem cwiṭ akken d asertar n Roma uqbel ma yenɣa-t deg 44 BCE. Mi d-yenɣa tmenɣiwt-nnes, Usinat Aṛuman yewwi-d asaḍuf, yeqqar-d dakken Qayser yeqqel d Ṛebbi.
Aɛdaw n Qayser ɣef yisensanen isertiyen ed yisɣanen n Roma yella yeɣtes yerna yeqqim aṭas. Ass-a, axeddim-nnes mazal-it yessefhamen-d iman-nnes d leɛjeb ed ukṛeh. Ma yella yebɣu yili, medden zemren ad d-ḍefren tazmert n uxeddim n Qayser deg tefsirt n waṭas n temdinin n umaḍal, am Fṛansa ed Yiwunak Yeddukklen.
Amezwaru n tanglizit William Shakespeare, yellan yezdeɣ deg wass n Renaissance, yura-d yiwen n wurar ɣef Qayser isem-nnes Julius Caesar. Ugaɣ, urar-a yeḍḍef tilelli igerrujen d tameqrant seg umezruy n tidet.
