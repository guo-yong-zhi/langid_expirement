Tamdint n Prag  ) d tamaneɣt ed tamdint meqqren akk deg Čik, tezga-d deg wasif n Vltava 1 . Deg 2022, Prag tesɛa ugar n 1,3 n yimelyan n yimezdaɣ 2 , tajumma-ynes 496 km² 3 .
Tabdert n tira id taqburt n tamdint n Prag tella-d seg 965, deg adlis n yinig di Brahim U Yeɛqub  ar , isemma tamdint : Farāga neɣ Barāga. Aṭas n teẓriwin i yellan ɣef laṣel n isem Praha s tačikiyt, yiwet deg-sen teqqareḍ beli awal práh yekka-d seg umyag prahnout; yekkaw/yettkaw s taqbaylit 4 .
Prag qqaren-as tamdint n wuṛeɣ, tayemmat n timdinin, neɣ tamdint n mya n yininayen  3 .
Prag tezga-d deg ul n Turuft talemmast, deg tlemmast n ameccaq gar Ilel Aderyati ed Ilel n Ugafa , teqṛeb ɣer temdinin meqqren maḍi deg Turuft talemmast  3 .
Prag tesɛa inezwi gar anezwu agarwan ed anezwu amenẓaw agrawi 5 .
Seg Tallit-Tanammast , Prag tella d yiwet seg timdinin icebḥen maḍi deg Turuft ɣer taɣult n tasegda, akked yexxamen-is, tiglisyiwin-is ed yiɣarimen-is 1 .
Deg 1992, temdint taqburt n Prag tekcem ɣar wumuɣ n Tigemmi tamaḍlant n UNESCO 3 .Lberǧ n Malá Strana.
