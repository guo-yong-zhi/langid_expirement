Malawi s yisem unsib Tamurt n Malawi  d tamurt n Tefriqt n unẓul-utram yettwasnen zik s yisem n Nyasaland. Tezzin ɣer Zambya ɣer ugafa-utram, Tanzanya ɣer ugafa-utram, Mozambiq ɣer usammer, agmuḍ d unẓul. Tamurt-a tefreq-itt-id seg Tanzanya d Muzambik s ugelmim n Malawi. 
Tesεa 118.000 km2 ma d agdud-is ugar n 13.900.000 n yimezdaɣen. Tamaneɣt-is d Lilongwe, d tamdint tis snat meqqren deffir Blantyre, ma d tamdint tis tlata d Muzuzo. Isem-is yekka-d seg Marafi, isem aqdim n wegdud n Nyanga i izedɣen tama-nni. Tamurt-a tettusemma daɣen s yisem n 《leḥma n wul n Tefriqt》. 1
