Ajeǧǧig neɣ Taslawit d agman agensas ɣef temhalt n umyaraw n tmesdurifsanin. Tawuri tasnudrant n ujeǧǧig tettgensis deg umahil-is n urway n tlekmast tamawtemt yakk d ubalal timtewtemt i lmend n ufares n yifsan.
Tamhelt-a tbeddu s ufarer i d-yesṭafaren asebrer i d-igellun deg taggara ɣer usnulfu n yifes.
