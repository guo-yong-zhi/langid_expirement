Ṣufyan Zermani, yetwassnen s  yisem unaznur Sofiane neɣ Fianso, Ilul ass n 21 yulyu 1986 di Saint-Denis deg Franṣa, d asegbar ,d anfares , d accenay uɣanib n rap  afransis. 
Segmi tejja lzzayer deg isseggassen n 60, yemma-as n Sufyan Zermani tersa di Nanterre u tqeddech taneflust ar France Loisirs. Ma d vava-s , Abdelqader Zermani, imir-en yella d ameksa di tmurt, yewwedh ar fransa deg 1981 akken ad isenz iselsa deg ugaduzen.
Sufyan Zermani iwala tafat di Saint-Denis asseggas n 1986.yezdegh di teghremt n  Clos Saint-Lazare deg Stains  almi d awetay krad mraw iggadj ar Le Blanc-Mesnil.
iffeghd seg ughervaz af semmus d maraw iseggassen u yessenti tawuri netta d vava-s deg igaduzen.
Sufyan yevda icennu rap deg tarbaɛt les affranchis, uqvel ad issufegh di 2007 tasfift maxi tamezwazrut :premiere claqsue.di 2009 issufeghd la vie de cauchmar i snat di yiwen label Karizmatik ig yedja di 2010.
Blacklist, tasfift maxi tis krad , teffegh deg 2011. deg assugas 2013 tedefred tasfift Blacklist 2  tettef amur wis 4 di temzizlt n tesfifin di itunes.
Di tefsut n 2016, Sufyan yevda asulef n tezrart inezriten deg ugmmamen imekhalafen n fransa isemmayas #JeSuisPasséChezSo .di tugets n ihricen iɛerdhed ighennayen nrap nidhen ur netwassen ara atas ,tazrart agi inezriten sɛeddant di menwalla ar ithri deg cna rap afransis,di furar 2017 tazrart tessawdh 80 imelyan uwali di youtube
di wember 2016, yestenya akked axxam usekled ameqran Universal Music af label Capitol.
di 27 yannayer 2017, sofiane issefghed tasfift mixtape #JesuispasséchezSo mbaɛd tazrart-is, tettef amkan wi sin deg senzi tesfifin di fransa s 14 526 di ddurt tamzewarut .16 meɣres 2017, asenfar icertifi uregh, sakin ctember icertifi plantinum..
