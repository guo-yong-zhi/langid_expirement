Sidi Neɛman d taɣiwant n Tmurt Leqbayel, tezga deg twilayt n Tizi Uzzu. 
Taɣiwant n Sidi Neɛman tezga deg ugafa-amalu n twilayt n Tizi Uzzu.
Taɣiwant n Sidi Neɛman tesɛa 19 tudrin 1  :
Sidi Neɛman d taɣiwant tamectuḥt n tamnaṭ n Tizi Uzzu. Tezga deg iɣzer n wasif n Sabaw. Amrawa, iɣzer agi yečuṛ d umezruy seg ṭrad "punik" . Amrawa n wadda  d tamnaṭ i ttwaḍlemen s tizririn n tagelda n Kuku, Iɛetmaniyen, Iṛumiyen akkw d izba imeẓwiyen mgal taslekt n iṛumiyen.
