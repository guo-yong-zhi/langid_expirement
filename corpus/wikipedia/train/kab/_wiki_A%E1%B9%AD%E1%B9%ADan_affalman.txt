Aṭṭan affalman d tagrawt waṭṭanen i d-yettilin s usrag webrir  d tamumt n unagraw anaffal n tfekka n umuddir deg tussna d tukza ɣef yigmanen-is d imuren-is igensasen. Anda ur izemmer ara ad yakez tukkest n ADN n tekwatin n tfekka sin akin ad yessalas  yid-sent amakken-itent d ajenṭad neɣ d uffiɣ ayen yettaǧǧan ad yenṭeg fell-asent  s tekwatin tfekkiwin n waffal 
Dɣa anecta i gellu-d s tiɣessirin  meqqren g tfekka, tiɣessirin-a zemrent  ad ilint deg yiwen n ugman am taɣbelt neɣ deg yigmanen yemgaraden i wakken ad yeglu s waṭṭan neɣ s waṭas n waṭṭanen 
Tella tdersi meqqren n yimdanen deg umadal ilan  aṭṭanen-a yettilin d imezrayen deg tuget n tegnatin, 
Istigen sskanayen-d d akken aṭṭanen iffalmanen gan gar 10 n waṭṭanen imezwura deg yisragen n tmekkasin  ɣer tmeṭṭuyin seg waddaren yakk alamma d 65 n iseggasen. 1  Tella tdersi n yimdanen ilan uguren-a n waṭṭanen igan d imezrayen deg tuget n tegnatin, akken illan ugar n 80 n waṭṭanen asrag-nsen d aṭṭan affalman. 2
