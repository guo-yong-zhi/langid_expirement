Imzalen d yiwet n taddart di tama n Yeflisen Umellil. 
Anamek n wawal-agi, yekka-d seg Amzak , seg zik, lɛarc-agi i d-yuskan di tama n yeflisen uɣen cciɛa imi tugett gar-asen d Iḥeddaden . Seg ayagi i d-yekka AMZAL: Win iseqdacen uzzal. Ula d tamacahutt-nni n uḥeddad uqalus, tbeggen-d anect-a. AQALUS di zzman tuɣ-itt t-d-tekka ar Yeflisen.
