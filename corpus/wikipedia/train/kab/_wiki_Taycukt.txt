Taycukt  d tawsit tamyiwent i d-yesgensisen tawacult n teycukt . Tawsit-a tegber seg 8 n telmas. Taycukt d yiwen n wegḍiḍ meqqren, yettidir deg yideggan anda llan waman yettwassen s tilin n temzit s ddaw n uqamum-is. Tiycukin ttidirent deg yimenẓawen yakk anagar Antraktika.
Isem ussnan Pelecanus yekka-d seg tutlayt tagrikt tamzikt πελεκάν . Deg waṭas n tikkal yettili-d userwi  gar n teycukt d tkikla. Dɣa Carl Von Linné yella d netta i d amezwar i d issisemlen tawsit n teycukt deg wedlis-is anagraw n tgama deg teẓrigt-is tis mṛaw deg useggas n 1758, anda i d-yukez timeskanin-is timaẓlayin: , Dɣa tabadut-a temken ula d ufergaṭ d ufelcu yakk d umessuṭ.
