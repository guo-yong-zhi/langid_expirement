Kamal Ddin Fexxar ilulen ass  1u deg yennayer 1963 deg Tɣerdayt, yemmuten ass n 28 deg mayu 2019 deg Blida, d ṭṭbib, d ameɣnas asertan azzayri. D win yennuɣen ɣef izerfan n wemdan d tmeḥeqranit i ddren wid-is deg tmennaṭ n Tɣerdayt. Yettuḥebbes gar 2015 d 2017, ɛawden ṭṭfen-t tagara n meɣres 2019 ɣef kra n tmuɣli i d-yefka deg yiwet n tvidyut, dɣa iga asunded n laẓ armi yemmut deg sbiṭar Frantz Fanon n Blida.
