Tanzanya neɣ Tagduda tedduklent n Tanzanya , d tamurt n Tafrikt usamar ɣef ṭṭerf n Agaraw Ahendi deg unẓul n Izirig n usebges. Seg 1974, tamanaɣt-ynes Dar es Salaam, d tamdint meqqren ed usagen meqqren akk deg tamurt, tajumma n Tanzanya 940 000 km², tesɛa 58 005 463 n yimezdaɣ deg 2019 1 , 2 .
Tanzanya tesɛa tilist akked Muzambik ed Malawi ɣer wenẓul, Zambya, Tagduda Tamegdayt n Kungu, Burundi ed Rwanda ɣer umalu, Uganda ed Kenya ɣer agafa ed akked Agaraw Ahendi ɣer usamar 2 .
