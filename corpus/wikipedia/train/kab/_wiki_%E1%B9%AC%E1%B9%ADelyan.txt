Ṭṭelyan neɣ Tagduda Taṭelyant,  tezga-d di Turuft. Ṭṭelyan tekcem di Tadukli n tmura n Turuft. Tutlayt tasirant  ines d taṭelyant.
Tamurt n Ṭelyan d tamurt tameqqrant deg umezruy n umaḍal: d agi i tulli tamdint n Roma.
Ṭṭelyan tesɛa kraḍ n yeḥricen n anezwu 1  :
Tagduda n Ṭṭelyan deg-s 20 n timnaḍin 2 .
Nnig n taṭalyanit , tidersiwin deg Ṭelyan iheddṛen 12 n tutlayin nniḍen steɛṛfen seg uwanak : talbanit, takatalant, talmant, tagrigit, tasluvinit, takrwatit, tafrensist, arpitan, furlan, ladin, tuksitant ed sardu 3 .
