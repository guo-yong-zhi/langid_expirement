Tinerf "ameqqran", D asɣad ameqqran yernu d agellid n ugdud n Gwanec  n tegzirt n Tinerfe nnan-d diɣ yedder deg tasut tdi mraw d ukkuz n tlalit .
D mmi-s n ugellid Sunta , i ynebḍen tigzirt deg wussan id-yezwaren anekcum n yiqectaliyen ɣer tegzirin ikanariyin . Tella tmanaɣt-is d Aǧe akka am akk wid it-id-yezwaren , akken uqbel twines n yiseggasen uqbel anekcum  meqqren n 1494 .
