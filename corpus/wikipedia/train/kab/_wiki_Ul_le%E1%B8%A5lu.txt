Ul leḥlu , isem-is unṣib Abderahmane Lahlou, d acennay i ilulen ass n 9 ɣuct 1963 deg Taqaṛabt, taddart n taɣiwant n Iɣil Ali di twilayt n bgayet. Yeffeɣ-d si tesdawit n Qsenṭina, dinna i d-yeṭṭef agerdas n tasnafsit.
Yerbeḥ tamelt-is s tizlatin tigrawliwin am "Pouvoir Assassin" d "Ulac Smaḥ Ulac" i icenun imesbaniyen n Tefsut Taberkant n 2001 mgal adabu azzayri.
D acennay aɣewwaɣ, i summel asgufsu. I nnegem tilelli i wegdud aqbayli, akaz n tamagit n Tamurt n Leqbayel d akaz n tutlayt tamaziɣt am tutlayt tunṣibt. Yesɛedda lɛasker-is ar Rrɣaya.
