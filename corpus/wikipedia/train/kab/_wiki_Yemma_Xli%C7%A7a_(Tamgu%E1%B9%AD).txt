Yemma Xliǧa neɣ lalla Xliǧa neɣ Tamguṭ akken i s-sawalen yimezdaɣ n tudrin i s-d-yezzin, d taqacuct tunnigt deg yidurar n Ǧerǧer, teflal s wazal n 2308 m ɣef weswir n yilel. Deg beṭṭu adeblan, taqacuct n Yemma Xliǧa tezga-d deg tɣiwant n Ṣaḥariǧ deg ugezdu n Tubiret.
