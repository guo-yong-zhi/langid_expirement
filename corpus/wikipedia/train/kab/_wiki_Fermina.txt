
Firmina    d mmi-s n ugellid Sifaks . Tebḍa Ruma tagelda n baba-s gar-as d Masensen . Tefka-as Lmasisil tagelda n baba-s .
Fermina d mmi-s n uɛedaw n yirumaniyen yuwi-d seg-s karuh-is d reffu-ines  , ɣas akken fkan-as Lmasisil ur tebɣin i Masensen ameddakel-nsen . Imi llan ugaden lukzn ad yegled Masensen Masilya d Masisilya ad asen-iruḥ aṭas n usirem deg Tefriqt . Maca mallan sin n yigeldunen imaziɣen ulac tuggdi , imi ad d-ggrin di ccwal kan . 
Ur d-nufi ara ayen nniḍen deg tudert n ugellid-agi d wayen yexdem ass mi yella . ur d-nufi daɣen awalen ɣef taggara-s kra n yimazranen nan-d belli Masensen yegled masilya d Masisilya deg useggas n 158 iban belli deg useggas agi i yeɣli Firmina , imi ur nesli s ugellid n Masisilya anagar Masensen ; yeǧǧa-d yiwen n mmi-s d ageldun qqaren-as Arxubazan 
Akka i teɣli ccetla n Sifaks .
