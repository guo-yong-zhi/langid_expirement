Acenyal amaziɣ neɣ Asenǧaq amaziɣ yettwanulfa-d deg iseggasen isamrawen  sɣur Agraw Imaziɣen . Deg 1998, s ifassen n Agraw Amaḍlan Amaziɣ  i igan agraw-nsen amenzu deg Tigzirin Tiknariyin deg Tmurt n Spenyul, asenǧaq agi yuɣal d asenǧaq unṣib n Imaziɣen akken ma ttilin ani ttidiren deg umaḍal. 
Ɣur-s krad  n yiniten :  aẓerwal , azegzaw , awraɣ . Deg tlemmas ines yettwars usekkil s yini  azeggwaɣ  s Tifinaɣ.
