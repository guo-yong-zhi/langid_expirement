Monica Vitti, s yisem-is n ṣṣaḥ Luisa Maria Ceciarelli, taktrist taṭelyanit tlul ass n 3 wamber 1931 di Roma.
Monica Vitti tekcem g Konservator n taẓuri tamugit n Roma  g useggas n 1953, umbeɛd-agi tevda lxedma taktrist-inse g uteatro d tiliẓri. 
Asaru amezwaru-ines, "Le dritte", iffeɣ g 1958.
G iseggasen 1960, turar aṭas d-id umseḍru umeqran Michelangelo Antonioni, surtu-as g usaren "L'avventura", "Di eklips", "Iḍ", neɣ "Aneẓruf azeggaɣ".
Texdem tanit d imseḍruwen imuqranen wiyaḍ, am Pier-Paolo Pasolini neɣ Luis Bunuel.
