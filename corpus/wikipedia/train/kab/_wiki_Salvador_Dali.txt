Salvador Dali , d amekla  mechuṛen n Spenyul. Dali yella d imsuneɣ  igerrzen, d win ay iḍefren aɣanib aflalwaẓri . Iqeddicen-nnes sskanayen-d tugniwin  d tiɣwaliyin  yerna ssewhament. Leqdic-nnes yettwassnen mliḥ d tafelwit  n La Persistencia de la memoria  ay d-iga deg 1931. Dali iger daɣen iman-nnes deg ssinima, deg tesreɣta  akked usseknew , d inurar aydeg yeqdec ɣer yidis n yinuẓar niḍen.
Dali yella iḥemmel akk ayen yellan d imdehheb, am wakken ay iḥemmel iceṭṭiḍen igmuḍanen , yerna yella yettwali lejdud-nnes d Imuriyen . Leqdicat-nnes n teklut  uwyen-d daɣe cwiṭ seg teklut n tallit n Tsidert , yerna yella yesɛa tasugint  d tameqrant.
