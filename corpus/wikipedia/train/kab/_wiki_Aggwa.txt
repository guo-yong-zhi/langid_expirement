Aggwa neɣ Aylal d irem i yettwanefken i yal iyunen yettemcabin deg iylanen yettwaberzen kan s unect n tmerniwt neɣ n tdersi n tesmekta. S umata, aggwa isgensis-d tehri neɣ aɣawas i tneẓẓi i uzenzar  am umḍafar n tesmekta, neɣ tasmekta n umussu neɣ teɣzi n temdeswelt neɣ asnagar neɣ jean tesmekta niḍen yettaken assaɣ. Amedya, aggwa n tzelɣiwin n bita isgensis-d aẓuni  n tfasa tasintant taliktṛunant tibawt n waggay i d-ittfɣen s uwurman seg yinukliden urmiden n izenzaren.
Mi ara ad yeglu kra n uferdis aṛadyurmid s tzelɣiwin nAlfa, ad fersent aggwa seg tzelɣiwin n Alfa ilan yiwet n tfasa neɣ ugar.
