Tazemmunt  d yiwet n telmest n yimɣan isaɣuren yeṭṭafaren tawsit n urjagnu.
Talmest-a tettemɣay-d deg Tferka n Ugafa am Ugafa n Lezzayer, agafa n Umeṛṛuk, agafa n Tunes yakk d tmurt n Yiqbayliyen, akken i d-tettemɣay daɣen deg yideggan n Unẓul n Uruppa am Malṭa, Tazunegzirt taybirt, Ṭelyan yakk d Qubrus.
