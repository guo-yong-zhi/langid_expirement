Amseddas'  d win i d-yeskarayen aẓawan s telmas-is yemgaraden. Acku d amseddas neɣ ameẓẓawan iy kettun   i yettarun aẓawan iy lan  kra n umahil anyan yettṛaǧun tilin  umsuɣel i wnayna-nni ama d ameẓẓawan neɣ d allalen i ixeddmen s tmellit. Yessefk fell-as ad iseggem iferdisen n uẓawan deg ussizeɣ ilan  tasnamekt 
Iferdisen-nni rzan: amsasi asaddas, anya, tiqqrit d unayna n uẓawan.
Tamidrant n umaska n umeẓẓawan d win yemgaraden amgired s waṭas deg talliyin n umezruy yemgaraden. 
Tamusni tamezwarut tuɣal ɣef tudert n yimeskaren n uẓawan ɣer tasut tis 14, Deg tallit-nni tuget n yimeskaren n uẓawan xeddmen i lmend n tmezdayt te neɣ i yimisseɣ d turna tageldant s umata weyyi xeddmen tiwuriwin nniḍen , imedyazen neɣ d imarayen 
Aṭas n uẓawan i yuran yimeskaren imẓawanen deg taggara n tallit talemmast d tazwara n tallit taleslalit , yezmer ad tt-xedment tuɣac neɣ allalen n uẓawan s umbaddal.
