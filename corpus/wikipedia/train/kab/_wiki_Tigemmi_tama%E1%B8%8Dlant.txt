Tigemmi tamaḍlant neɣ Adeg n tigemmi tamaḍlant , d umkan ḥerzen s Tuddsa n yeɣlalen yedduklen i usegmi, tussna d yidles , em « azal anmeɣrad », ddaw umtawa  n uḥuddu n tigemmi adelsan ed agaman n umaḍal  2 .
Llan kraḍ n leṣnaf n tigemmi tamaḍlant : adelsan, agaman ed sin-nni akken  2 .
Deg 2021, llan1154 n idegen n 167 n timura  win ijerrden em tigemmi tamaḍlant s UNESCO 3 . Deg 2021 daɣen, Ṭṭelyan tesɛa amḍan ameqqran n tigemmi tamaḍlant ,  t-ḍefren s Ccinwa , Lalman , Fransa , Spenyul  ed Lhend  4 .
