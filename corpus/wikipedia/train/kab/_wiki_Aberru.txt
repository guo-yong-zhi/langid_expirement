Aberru neɣ Ajṛad  d aburɣas yeṭṭafaren tafesna n tserdefriwin dges ugar n 20,000 n telmas yettidiren deg yidgan yemgaraden deg umadal, Aberru iga d yiwet n telmast n yebẓaẓ ilan  iṭarren udfiren  ttallen-t i wenttag anda tezmer ad tentteg azal n 20 n tikkal n teɣzi n tfekka-ynes.
Aberru d aɣersiw ilan  aṭas n icenga d ingiren  am iylalen d iɣeṛdayen d izerman d izunzar akken daɣen i llan kra n izdayen d iɣerfan i t-itetten am kra n iɣerfan n Asya d imaziɣen n Sus.
