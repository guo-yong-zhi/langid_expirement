Timibrikt n Liṭṭunya d iri azegraw aliṭṭuni. Isem-is agraɣlan d latvian brown dɣa isem-is aliṭṭuni d Latvijas brūnā neɣ buraïa latviskaïa s trusit.
D iri aqdim, yexleq-d seg usegraw n tijlibin tidiganin tiqdimin n taciṭṭa tazegwaɣt n Baltik. Teḍḍfed tazrirt n angeln deg timiḍi wis 19 uqbel alday n usaklas asutwalan deg useggwas 1885 dɣa teḍḍfed isem-is amiran deg useggwas 1922. Seg zik, nettat d iri n uyefki deg aẓi, mazal t-genses aqrib 75 % n uctal azegraw aliṭṭuni. Iri yettwebḍa s kuẓ ifurkawen yettweṛebban yal yiwen deg umkan is iwakken ad ssineḍen imeccir asiwan. Afurk BL-1 yettwefren s tixxutert taɛlayant deg isubxuzen. Tettaked tikessawin n tafarest n uyefki am imzufɣawen. Tafest n iketmen tesgader. Seg 1 147 000 deg useggwas 1980, Tagrawt yeṣṣub ar 556 000 deg useggwas 1990 1 . Deg useggwas 2000, amḍan n imzufɣawen BL-1 yessiggezen ɣef asaklas yella d 60 000 tifunasin d 300 iramulen.
Telsa taqendurt tazegwaɣt s tisignayin timibriktin ugar-neɣ-ddaw tibuḍḍlamin. Ini n mukus ines d ini n uksum dɣa ur tesɛa ara acciwen. Tafunast taɛlay ar 130 cm deg timezsmeḍt i 520 kg dɣa aramul 140 cm i 800 kg 1 .
D iri i mmizweren i uyefki. Tettfaras 5200 kg s usuḍeḍ n uyefki yesɛan aṭas n tinegwa uzuren  d tifṛutinin.
