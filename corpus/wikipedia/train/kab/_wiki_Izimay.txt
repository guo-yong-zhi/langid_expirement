Izimay  d aburɣes yeṭṭafaren tafesna n Ephemeroptera , dɣa tettusesmel deg tegrawt n temzifriwin igebren daɣen seg  tatlulleɣt yakk d taẓewt n tala igan d ibureɣsen n waman deg tallit ur newwi ara , tettidir yiwen n useggas deg waman iẓidanen. Ma d aburɣes amawaṭ yettidir seg kra n tesdatin alamma d kra n wussan s unect n telmest.
Ar ass-a, llant wazal n 2,500 n telmas yettwassnen deg umadal, gar-asent 630 n telmas ttidirent deg temrikt tagafant.
Tiddi n izimay iga ger n 4 ar 50 mm
