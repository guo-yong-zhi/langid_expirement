Tabarka  d yiwet tamdint n Tunes, deg utaram n tmurt-a, teqreb ar tilisa gar Tunes d Lezzayer . Deg useggas n 2004, ɣur-s azal n 15,634 imezdɣen. D amɣiwan-is  d Cuqri Zwawi. 
Tezga-d ar 175 km si Tunes Tamaneɣt deg twilayt n Janduba, deg wul n Yidurar n Xmir. Agdud-is deg tigti seg-sen d At Xmir i ṣubben seg idurar ar temdint n Tbarka.
Snuflan adeg n "Tabraka" inumiden deg wakud n tigeldin tinumidin n Masnsen. 
Deg 702 yeḍra deg Tbarka tikkelt nniḍen imenɣan gar imaziɣen  d inekcamen aɛraben n Ḥasan Ibn Numan i yesɛa azal n 50,000 iserdas ugar i yimenɣi seg axalif Ɛebdelmalik mgal imesluben imaziɣen.
