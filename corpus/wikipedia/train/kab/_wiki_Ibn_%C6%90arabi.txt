Muḥyeddin Ibn Ɛarabi, s taɛrabt محي الدين ابن عربي, yettwasen kan s yisem n Ibn Ɛarabi, d amedyaz, d amusnaw n ajjed, u wayen umi neqar Mystisism. D yiwen ger ibabaten n tasnakta Taṣufit. Ilul deg usegas n 1165, di Mursi, tamurt n Andaluz, di Sbanya. 1  Imut deg 1240, di Dimecq, tamurt n Surya n tura. Qaren-as daɣen « Cix amuqran », isɛa azal n 846 n yidlisen. Deg isefra ines, yettmeslay-ed af tayri, cbaḥa, iba, aramsu, atg.
Asmi isɛa 14 isegasen, imlal di Kurdu  akw d Averroès, sɣur ilmed aṭas n tmusniwin d tfiluṣufit.
