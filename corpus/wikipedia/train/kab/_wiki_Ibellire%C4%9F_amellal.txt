Ibellireǧ amellal  qqaren-as tewzel s taqbaylit : Abellireğ neɣ Ibellireğ,  d talmest n iylalen, yeṭṭafaren tawacult n iswuwen 2 . 
Abellireğ yesɛa ifrawn d imellalen, akked uberkan deg afriwen, lqedd-is seg 1 m ar 1,20 m, lqedd n ufriwen-is yezmer ad yesɛa seg 1,55 m ar 1,65 m. Abellireğ-nni yezmer ad yewzen alma d 4,4 kg 2 .
Abellireǧ yettidir deg Turuft, Asya ed Tafrikt. Deg ɣuct ibellirğen ad d-ruḥen seg idgan n awdef-ines ɣer Tafrikt ɣef wamber d dujember n wayyuren. Deg meɣres d yebrir ad qqlent ɣer awdef-ines 2 .
Ibellirğen n Turuft Umalu ad inigen ɣer Tafriqt Ugafa ed Tafriqt tanẓunezruft si Adrar n Ṭariq. Ibellirğen n Turuft Usamar ad inigen ɣer Maṣer ed wenẓul n Tafrikt si Tturk ed Legrig 2 .
Ibellirğen tetten iburɣesen, Ijiremḍen  fr , tikelmanin, timraradin timecṭuḥin, tiruca, islemen, timseɣẓaẓ timecṭuḥin ed tikwal imsrifgen. Učči n ibellireǧ iḍfer inezwi; deg Turuft talemmast abellireǧ yečča ahat n 60% n timsuṭad timecṭuḥin ed 30% n timraradin, deg tmennaḍin akken ḥman d amitti 2 .
