Tiddi-ines tettawaḍ alamma d 3 m, ini n iferran-is d azegza imalen ɣer tizzegzut. 
Imɣi-ayi yettenfufud s iweggiren akken i yettwassen s gemmu-ines arurad d ufaris-is yugten, ayen i d-t-yeǧǧan ad ittumeggez d yiwen seg imendiyen n tfasa yettusxedmen i ufares n imerɣuder.
