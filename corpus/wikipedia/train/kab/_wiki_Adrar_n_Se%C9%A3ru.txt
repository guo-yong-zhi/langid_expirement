Adrar n Ṣeɣru yella di ugmuḍ n Aṭlas ameẓyan, tamnaḍt-a deg-s aṭṭas n imukan n ugama yebhan s waṭas di temanḍt-a, deg-s taddagin n yisiɣid d imendi d tezdayin d iblaḍen di mkul amkan. Imezdaɣ ineṣliyen bnan  yiwen uglmam azegza yeɣzzifen s cḥal n ikilumiṭren.
Adrar n ṣeɣru deg-s anezwu aneẓraf. Taskfelt n terɣi da tebddel s waṭas, da ttawḍ ar 6-9 deg tegrest, ar 27 d 30 n teskfelt deg unebdu, tiskfal n terɣi ttawḍen-t ar 50 n teskflt.
Yettwassen ṣaɣru s temanaɣt n teqbilt tamaziɣt si At ɛeṭṭa، yiwet si teqbilin yettwanɣan mgal ankcam afransis i lemɣrib.
