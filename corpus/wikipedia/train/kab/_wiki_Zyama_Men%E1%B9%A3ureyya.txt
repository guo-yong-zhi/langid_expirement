Zyama Menṣureyya , d yiwet taɣiwant n Tmurt Leqbayel i tezga-d al 40 km g umalu n Tawilayt n Jijel, irif n ilel. Imezdaɣ-ines d Isaḥliyen, ar ass-a tmeslayen  Tasaḥlit  akk d Taɛrabt . Ɣur-s azal n 12,642 imezdaɣ . Di tallit n tuddfa n Fransa llan qqarn-as "Douar Mansouria".
