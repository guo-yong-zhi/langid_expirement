Aqur neɣ Aqqur  d talmest n yijqiwen yeṭṭafaren tawsit n waqqur deg twacult n tmesmineqṛin. Talmest-a tettidir di tẓegwa d yideggan yudsen ɣer waman, maca yettili daɣen deg imudaɣ d wurtan.
Tawtemt tessrusay seg 4 alamma d 5 n tmellalin, ini-nsent d tizegzawin d tiberbacin, ttefruruxen-tt-d seld n 14 n wussan.
