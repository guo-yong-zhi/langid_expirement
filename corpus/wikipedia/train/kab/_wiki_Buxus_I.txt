Buxus , neɣ Buxus I akken ad d-iban d anwa-t akken iwata , yella d agellid n Muretanya seg 111 -80 ST . Yella d aḍeggal n ugellid anumidi Yugurten , ukkud yeddukel mgal yirumaniyen deg ṭṭrtad n Yugurten .
Yexdeɛ ugellid Buxus Yugurten yessenz-it i yirumaniyen deg 105 ST . Yettzqṭṭef Yugurten yettzqḥbes deg usekrqf di Ruma , ma d netta yefreq tagelda n yirumaniyen d yirumaniyen .
Cukken kra n yimussnawen n umezruy belli isem n Buxus d abeddel alatini n yiwen n yisem amaziɣ , ahat d Wekkus . Isem-agi yezmer ad yesɛu assaɣ d wawal atergi Aweqqas , id-yemmalen "izem" , yezmer ad yili d ise'm amalay .
Ur yettwassen ara aṭas ama d Buxus ama d tagelda n Muretanya . Yella ahatt d yiwen seg warraw n ugellid Baga amuretani , yiwen n umeddakel n ugellid Masensen anumidi .
Tagelda n Buxus id-yezgan deg tefriqt n ugafa tella gar ugram atlenṭi d wasif n Melwiya 
Buxus yesɛa-d aṭas n tulawin d ukkuz n warrac : yelli-s  , i yuɣen Yugurten anumidi ; amekkasu-ines Mestanususus  , Buluks d Bugud.
Mestanususus yeqqel d agellid ardekal lmut n baba-s yefreq tagelda d watmaten-is Buxus II d Bugud , i yferqen s yixfan aglad n Muretanya . Ddan deg sin n yiberdan yemgaraden mmi d-yennulfa nnfaq arumani n daxel , yegled Bukus ixef n Bugud , mi yemmut Buxus II deg 33 ST tuɣal Muretanya d tagelda tasɣant n Ruma.
