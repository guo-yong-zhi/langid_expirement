At Σidel  d yiwen lɛerc aqbayli yezgan gar yidurar n Yiwannuɣen akk d Udrar n Tbabuṛt, deg usamar n Tmurt n Leqbayel tama n Asif Ɛebbas, di tmurt n Lezzayer.
Deg wakal n lɛerc-ayi i ttemlilen Asif n Saḥel akk d wasif n Busellam, deg yiwen umkan qqaren-as Tasfart, anda i yettlal Asif Asemmam, neɣ Asif Ɛebbas.
Zgan-d At Ɛidel deg usammar n temdint n Uqbu, deg ugafa n lɛerc n At Ɛebbas.
Yezger-iten Asif n Busellam. Sɛan di tlisa nsen deg unẓul At Ɛebbas, deg usammar At Yeɛdel d Wat Wertiren, deg ugafa At U-Mɛuc, Imula d Wat Yemmel, u deg umalu U-Zellagen d Illulen Usammar. 1 
Deg-s tiɣiwanin n Tamuqra, Buḥemza, Amalu, Sedduq d kra n tudrin n Imsisen, deg ugezdu n Bgayet, rnu yiwet taddart n tɣiwant n Ilmayen, deg ugezdu n Berǧ Buɛririǧ.
