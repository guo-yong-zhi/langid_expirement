Dato' Siri Anwar Ibrahim , d aneɣlaf amezwaru n Malizya seg 1993 ar 1998. Anwar Ibrahim yettwafren ula d aneɣlaf amenzu n Malaysia deg 25 wamber 2022.
Deg tazwara n umahil-is, yella yettwaqebbel sɣur uneɣlaf amatu n tikelt-nni, Mahathir Mohamad, maca ssya ɣer deffir, Anwar yuɣal yettwassen d anmegal ameqqran n tsertit-is.
Deg useggas 1999, yettwaḥkeṛ ɣer 6 n yiseggasen n lḥebs ɣef yiwet n temsalt i d-yessufɣen ccwal, deg useggas 2000 yettwaḥkeṛ ɣer 9 n yiseggasen nniḍen n lḥebs ɣef leḥsab n tnezzit akked yiwen n umsedyaz. Maca, deg useggas n 2004, Tazɣa Tameɣrawt n Malizya tessebleɛ taḥbult tis snat yerna yeffeɣ seg lḥebs mbeɛd 6 iseggasen.
Deg yulyu 2008, tettwaṭṭef ɣef ccekk belli tessexdem d umseqdac-is n 23 iseggasen. Deg mayyu 2018, mi yerbeḥ Mass Mahathir Mohamed deg tefranin, yefka-d awal i Mass Anwar Ibrahim ad t-yesemḥaseb deg tallit n zik. Taggara,n ayyur-a, Anwar Ibrahim yettwasemḥel sɣur unabaḍ n Malizya, akken ad yuɣal ɣer tasertit.
