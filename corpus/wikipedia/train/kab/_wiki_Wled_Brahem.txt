Wled Brahem d taddart daɣen deg twilayt n Burǧ Bu Ɛririǧ.
Wled Brahem   d taddart n wenẓul n Tanebḍit  n Burǧ Bu Ɛririǧ.
Taɣiwant-a tella deg tadayra n Ras Lwad d idurar n ḥuḍna .
Taɣiwant n Wlad Brahem tettwassen s amassan n ddin : Cix Muḥamed Lbacir Ibrahimi.
Wlad Brahem d Taɣiwant tagrawliwt, aṭas deg yimezdaɣen-is yudfen deg ṭṭraḍ n tlelli n Lezzayer.
