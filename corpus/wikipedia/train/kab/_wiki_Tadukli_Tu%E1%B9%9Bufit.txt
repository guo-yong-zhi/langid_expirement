Tidukli Tuṛufit (UE d tadukli tagraɣlant n tmura n Tuṛuft i yesɛan 27 n tmura, taneggarut deg-sent d Kerwatya, i d-yedduklen deg 1 yulyu 2013. Yettwabna ɣef lsas n umsefham yettwassnen s yisem n Amtawa n Maastricht i d-yettwassen deg 1991, maca aṭas n tiktiwin-is llant seg yiseggasen n xemsin n lqern yezrin.
