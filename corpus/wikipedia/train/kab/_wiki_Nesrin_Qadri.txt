
Nesrin Qdri   d tacennayt n tmurt n Israyel d taɛrabt n usamar alemmas tella d tineslemt tekcem deg wajjed uday amur ameqqran n ccna - ines s thibrut llan kra s Tutlayt taɛrabt akka am tid n Um Keltum 
tlul-d Nesrin Qadri di Hayfa di Israyel d inselmen  baba-s inehher tasunnyt n utaksi ma d yemma-s d tamasawt n umejjay 
tecna di tmeɣriwin n twacult d nnwadi imectah tewwi-d talwit meqqren deg useggas n 2011 deg Iban Gulan ma nessawal-ak-d  
tessufeɣ-d Lalbum-ines amezzwaru deg useggas n 2014 
di 4 ctumber 2018 tessufeɣ-d lalbum wis 3 
di 2017 tessawel-as tneɣlaft tasrayilit n yedles akken ad tecnu di temdint n Uṛacalim 
di 2019 tettekka ɣer The voice Israel 
I mraw n yiseqqasen tella d umusiqi uday asrayili Afɛizer ben Muḥa ; xedmen kullec i tmeɣra n rrcil teffeɣ si tneslemt tuɣal d tudayt zewǧen di yulyu 2017 maca msebran di ctunber 2017 . 
Di 2018 nnan-d belli tkemmel ankcum-ines ɣer tudayt tekcem ajjed amaynut maca ur steɛrfen yiss la iḥaxamen  wala taneɣlaft n daxel deg Israyel
