Kazaxestan neɣ Tagduda n Kazaxestan , d awanak n Turuft Usamar ed Asya talemmast 1 , d tamurt tis tẓa  i imeqqren deg umaḍal 2 , tajumma-ynes 2 717 000 km² 1 . Tamanaɣt-ynes d Astana 2 .
Kazaxestan tesɛa tilist akked Rusya ɣer agafa, Mungulya d Ccinwa ɣer usamar, Kirgizistan, Uzbakistan, Illel n Aral ed Turkmanistan ɣer wenẓul ed akked illel Caspium ɣer umalu 1 .
