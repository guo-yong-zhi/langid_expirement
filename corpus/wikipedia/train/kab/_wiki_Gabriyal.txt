Nettmeslay-d dagi si Tamasiḥit d Tudayt wali Tineslemt dagi : Jebril
Deg  yiɣbula udayen d yirumyen  isem n Gabriyil yebɣa ad d-yini ) isem-a yebɣa ad-d-yerr ɣer Rebbi nezmer ad neqbel belli awal agi d akildani neɣ d acuri suqlent akken yella ɣer tegrigit d tlatinit 
gabriyel d lmelk meqqren di Tamasiḥit d Tudayt yettwabder-d di Tibibelt iban-d di tazzwara deg udlis n Daniyel yettbedd d seddis n lmalaykat sdat Rebbi seg ufus-is azelmaḍ yeqqar-d tiẓallitin n yigerramen i netta ; yettɛassa igenwan ; d asleway amezzwaru n lǧennet ; d netta i d bu wassaɣ s wazal meqqren . yetthuddu icerriren. Yessufuɣ cwaṭen yessenqad agama . Di Tudayt Gabriyel d lmelk ixeddmen d imceyyeɛ . ixeddem ayen is-d-yenna Rebbi i wid id-yexleq d wagi is-tbeddu  tadɣemt , d netta i yuwin Yusef ɣer watmaten-is , yennuɣ d Yeɛqub , itekka deg umḍal n Musa 
di lɛehd ajdid yettwabder-d sin n yiberdan yeffeɣ-as-d i Zakariya yednn-as-d belli ad d-ilal mmi-s Yeḥya . Iban-d i Meryem : yenna-as-d belli ad d-arew agrud mebla rrcil . Qaren-d belli yefka-as-d linǧil i Ɛisa . deg wajjed n tmasiḥit Gabriyel d lmelk ameqqran yettban-d am umdan i yimceyyɛen seg Yillu id-yettban .
