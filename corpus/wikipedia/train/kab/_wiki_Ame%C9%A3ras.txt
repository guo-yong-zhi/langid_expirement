Ameɣras d win yemmuten i wakken ad yessiweḍ ɣer yiswi i ebɣa ugdud-is, ieggura-agi ttarran-t d ameqqran ttaken-as isem-a.
Di tudayt yella wawal n Qedduc Hacem  id-yettmalen tigain timeqqranin ara yisḥemmlen lɛebd ɣer yillu-ines, lmut lǧal n tudayt d yiwen seg yixfan igejdanen-is. Imeɣrasen n tudayt amur ameqqran deg-sen d wod yemmuten deg nnfaq amekkabi neɣ deg yimenɣiwen nniḍen mgal Ruma, yal in yettwanɣan seg yimiren imi kan yeḍfer tudayt yettwaḥseb d ameɣras.
Qqaren yimasiḥiyen awal n umeɣras i yal win yemmuten imi yumen s tmasiḥit neɣ yessawel i yimdanen akken ad kecmen deg-s.Timezdayin timasiḥiyin sɛant kra n yiṣellaḥen d imeɣrasen ttrran ussann lmut-nsen d leɛyud ɣer-sen.
Ɣer yinselmen yal win yemmuten deg umennuɣ) liǧal n tneslemt d ameɣras, yal win yettwanɣan imi yegmen ad yamen s tneslemt neɣ imi yessawel i yimdanen akken ad uɣalen d inselmen d ameɣras, yernu yal win id-yekkren ad iḥudd ɣef uxxamis d usɣan-is s yin yemmut yettwaḥseb d ameɣras ula d netta, taggara-s di lǧennet mebla tarelkint. Llant daɣen liḥalat ideg ameɣras ur yettwanɣa ara akka am win ara yemmten  uɣraq neɣ s uṛɣu.
Am kraḍ-agi i yɛeddan akk isɣanen id-yeggran qqaren i yal win yemmuten liǧal n usɣan-is "ameɣras"
