Amenferriw awraɣ neɣ Turiɣt  d yiwet n telmest n igṭaṭ igan d iminig s talɣa tamurant. Aylal-a yeṭṭafar tawacult n tbenferriwin
Amenferriw awraɣ iga d yiwen gar telmas meẓẓiyen yakk deg twacult n ibenferriwen anda ableɣ-is amnummsan 12 cm. Am ifrax am imawaṭen tifekkiwin-nsen d tid yeččuren. Afriwen-nsen d iberkanen yakk d uzawag d awraɣ uddir  ma d tameccact neɣ tadfart d tawraɣt tezzegzu
