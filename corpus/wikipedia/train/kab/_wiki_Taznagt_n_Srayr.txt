Taznagt n Srayr  d yiwet n tantala Tamaziɣt ttmeslayen s yis iznagen n srayer deg tama n warif id yezgan s agafa n ameṛṛuk, Tantala ayyi tudes  s waṭas ar tmaziɣt n waṭlas ɣas akken tettwasemdu s trifit.
Tantala taznagt d tin yettwaggzen s ungar acku gran-d anagar n 50,000 neɣ 70,000 n ifganen i zeddiɣ  ttmeslayen s yis.
