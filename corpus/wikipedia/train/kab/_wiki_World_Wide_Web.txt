World Wide Web, D agrudem, i seqdacen deg Internet. Anamek n wawal-a , agrudem yessawḍen ad yeldi azeṭṭa Internet i yal amdan. WWW neɣ Web, yennulfa-d deg useggas 1991, sɣur umazzag aglizi Timothy Berners-Lee . Deg tazwara, Web yeffeɣ-d akken ad awḍent tilɣa i yal amagmay di ddunit; maca yal aseggas llan wid i d-teqqnen ɣer uzeṭṭa Internet.
