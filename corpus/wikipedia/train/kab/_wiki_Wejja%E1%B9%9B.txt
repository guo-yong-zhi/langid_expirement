Wejjaṛ neɣ Wejjiṛ d afray  s uwezwez d uskikeṭ war asemdu asenwuran. Afray-a yezmer ad yili d imezgi neɣ d imezri. Dɣa talmest timezdit yakk deg wejjaṛ d afray-nni yettwassnen s yimessaken d tissegnatin. Akken tella telmest niden d timezdit maca ur tettwassen ara s waṭas , afray ayi yemmug amakken d aburɣes i yteddun ɣef weglim.
