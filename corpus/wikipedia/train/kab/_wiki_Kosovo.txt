Kosovo d akal i d-yezgan deg wenẓul n Serbya. Ugar n 88% seg yimezdaṣen n Kosovo d Albaniyen imselman, ma d 7% seg-sen d Iserben. 
Deg 1999, nnuɣen akked Yiserben akken ad awin timunent-nsen , d aya i yejjan iɣallen n Temsisit Taṭlasit akked Yeɣlanen Yeddukklen ad d-gren iman-nsen. Deg 2008, anabaḍ  n Kosovo tesseɣret-d  timunent, maca aṭas n tmura deg umaḍal ur steɛṛfent yes-s yerna Serbya mazal-itt tettwali-t d aḥric seg wakal-ines yellan ddaw udabu n Yeɣlanen Yeddukklen. Tajumma-ines 10.908 km2 . Zedɣen-t 1.804.838 n yimezdaɣen. Tamaneɣt-ines d Pristina.
