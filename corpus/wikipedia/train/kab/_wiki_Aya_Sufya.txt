Aya Sufya , d taglisya taqburt n Istambul bnan-t deg lqern wis 6 s tenmehla n amenkad abizanṭi Justinianus I, Aya Sufya yettuɣal lǧameε seg 1453, am iɛetmaniyen kecmen ɣer tamdint n Istambul. Ardeqqal, yettuɣal usalay seg 1934. Deg 10 yulyu 2020, Aya Sufya yettuɣal lǧameε tikkelt nniḍen 1 , 2 .
Aya Sufya yazga-d deg temnaḍt tamazrayt n Istambul, d Tigemmi tamaḍlant n UNESCO seg 1985 3 .
