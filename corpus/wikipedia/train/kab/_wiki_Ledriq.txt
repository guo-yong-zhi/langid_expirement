Ledriq , yemmut di yulyu 711 deg Spenyul d agellid aneggaru n yigutiyen n umalu imi tamurt-is kecmen-tt yinselmen s ddaw n tmela n Tareq u ziyad d Musa bnu Nusayr .

Tamnaḍt taǧaret n Tmazɣa kecmen-tt yisnemen s ddaw n tmehla n Musa bnu Nusayr . Aneggaru-agi yerra yiwen n umaziɣ kra nniḍen nnan-d d amwali  aẓar-is d amaziɣ , d Ṭareq u Ziyad d anembaḍ ɣef Ṭenǧa , yernu imehl-as akken 7 000 n yimnayen yettwasuqqlen ɣer tneslemt mebla tarlkint . 
Lkunt Yulyan , anembaḍ n Sebta , qi wumi ssawalen wid n Turuft Julyan ,yella yekreh Ledriq yernu yettemsefham d Ṭareq . Yules-d umazran amaṣri Ibn Ɛebd El Ḥakam snat n tasutin umbeɛd belli yelli-s n Yulyan : Flurinda terza ɣer tgelda n Ledriq akken ad d-lmed maca yekkes fella-s lḥerma-s tuɣal-d ɣer Sebta s tadist .
