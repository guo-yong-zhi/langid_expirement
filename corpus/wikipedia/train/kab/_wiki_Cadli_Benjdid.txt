Cadli Benjdid yella d aselway n Tegdut n Dzayer seg 7 furar 1979 almi 11 yennayer 1992. Ilul deg Sebɛa  ass n 14 ibrir 1929 yemmut ass n 6 tuber 2012 deg sbiṭaṛ n Usaka n Qsenṭina .
Di lweqt n ṭṭrad mgal Fransa yekcem deg ALN. Akked usarug n dzayer yuɣal ad yexdem g lɛesker. Asmi yemmut Hewwari Bumedyen, Cadli yella d yiwen amdan i yesɛan legrad ameqqeran akk: Colonel. Lɛesker deg lweqt-nni bɣan amdan am netta deg wemkan n Bumedyen.
Cadli yebɣa ad yeffeɣ seg Tanemla  axaṭer dzayer texser aṭas idrimen, meɛna azal n lpitrul iṣubb aṭas di lḥekm-is. Idzayriyen inṭarren  ffɣen-d ɣer iberden ass n 5 tubar 1988 anida yekker umennuɣ ameqqran. Ayen i d-yeḍran ussan-nni yettwasen s Tubar 88 . Ihi Cadli yexdem tamendawt  tamaynut, yeǧǧa akk ikabaren, seg-sen FFS, RCD, FIS. Cadli yebɣa ad yerbeḥ lvoṭ s ukabar ines  lameɛna idzayriyen voṭan FIS.
Xaled Neẓẓar, i yellan d aneɣlaf n lɛesker g lweqt-nni, yura g wedlis ines belli yanna-yas i Cadli "Ilaq-ak ad teǧǧeḍ amkan-ik akken ad neḥbes lvoṭ". Cadli yeqbel ayen yenna-yas-d Nezzar. Akabar n FIS ur yeqbil ara, bdan rrebrab mgal lɛesker u yebda ṭrad gar-asen. 
Ɣer taggara n tudert-is, Cadli yettili d tmeṭṭut-is deg Wehran.
