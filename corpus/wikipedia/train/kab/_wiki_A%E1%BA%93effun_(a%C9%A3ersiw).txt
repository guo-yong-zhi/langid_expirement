Aẓeffun neɣ Azaffan  d yiwet n twacult meqren n truca tiwlalin, Aẓeffun ila  tefekka ɣezzifen, Kṛad  gar smus  n iṭaren-is lan ikacbaren, gar-asen tayuga tamenzut i yettilin d tayuga iy meqren ɣef tyugiwin i d-yezgan deffir-is, Aẓeffun yettidir deg ifexsiwen  yakk d isegunen s ddaw n yilel.
Deg ugezdu n Tizi Wezzu deg Tmurt n Iqbayliyen tella yiwet n tɣiwant tettusemma ɣef uɣersiw-a dɣa awal itezzi ɣef tɣiwant n Uẓeffun.
