Asebdad n Tlelli , d asebdad agehguh tesɛa 93 n lmitrat deg asawen-is, Fransa tefka asebdad-nni i Marikan deg 1886. Asebdad n Tlelli yezga-d deg usagen n New York 1 , 2 , 3 .
Imuhal n lebni n usebdad bdan deg 1875 deg Fransa akked tenmehla n asreqqat Frédéric-Auguste Bartholdi, asebdad-nni yemmugen s Eugène-Emmanuel Viollet-le-Duc ed Alexandre-Gustave Eiffel 2 .
Deg 1984, asebdad n Tlelli yekcem ɣar wumuɣ n Tigemmi tamaḍlant n UNESCO 3 .
