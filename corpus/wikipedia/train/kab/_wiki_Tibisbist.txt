Tibisbist neɣ Abusbis  d tawsit meẓẓiyen n yijqiwen yeṭṭafaren tawacult n tbuzegrayzin. Dɣa abusbis yessiliɣ-d  tawacult n tbuzegrayzin. Adfar n telmas-is d win yemgaraden seg umnummsan ɣer uɣezfan. Tawsit-a tettidir deg tuget n yideggan n umadal, anagar tineẓrufin d taganin tinanẓarin yakk d Antraktika.
Tafekka-ines d tusdidt, d tiwriɣt. Seg yiylan n tewsit-a d akken tilmas-is gant d tiynarcalin. Bennunt isekkiyen-nsent ɣef tegnit , sin akin ssrusayent alamma d 6 n tmellalin. 
Seld n yiwet n tezrawt i d-yessers Davies d Peacock deg useggas n 2014, tella-d tukksa n snat n telmas i d-ittugelmen taggariwin-a sɣur n weswir amsaylal agraɣlan . Abusbis busallaf  yakk d ubusbis n Kimberley  anda ttwaẓiẓirfen s umṭafar deg ubusbis n Vaal  yakk d ubusbis n tferka . Dɣa ittbin-d d akken tilmas-a gant d tigrawin timinigin kan n telmes i d-ittwabdaren.
