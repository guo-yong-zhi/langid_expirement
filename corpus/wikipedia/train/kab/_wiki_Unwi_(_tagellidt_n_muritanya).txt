unwi mura  d tagellidt tamurit d tamyercalt n ugellid Bugud d tameɛcuqt n Yulyus Qayser 
unwid yiwet sezg yinesbaɣuren meqqren gar yimaziɣen, tetteka ɣer wedrum n Lmur aẓar-ines si wilili n tura di Lmerruk dqqaren-d belli tedder di zman n tgellidt kliyubatra
xas akken isem-is s tutlayt n Legrig id-yusa anamek-ines tin yelhan ,maca nettat seg yimaziɣen isem-is n n tidet d Usnufa 
temlal-d i ubrid amenzu agellids bugud deg uxxam-is di de Tin iggi di yiwet lweɛda i deg yemlal d yimɣaren n yakk iderman-ines . ters-d tayri gar-asen tebɣa agellid-a dɣa yuɣ-itt  
terna-d tayri gar-asen mi yezger wergaz-is i yilel atlanti yuwi-as-d yal lkenz id-yufa . ayen werjin slin yis medden yella di teɣremt n unwi kkes-d d acu ur-s-d yuwi ara Bugud 
deg tedyanin n Spenyul, i deg yedda Bugud d umeddakel-is César ,yefka-as iserdasenimiren sizar yessen d anta i d Unwi .Sizar yella yesɛa tameɛcuqt d tagellidt n Maṣerkliyubatra maca Unwi tewwi-as ula d nettat ul-is akken texdem Kliyubatra di taggara yessemlal-iten wusu Bugud ur d-yenni acemmek mi d-yuwed ar Tefriqt Ugafa banen-d  gar yielliden i ykerrem Qayseunwi d bugud farsen tagnit ttfen yal araz si tama n Julius César
tayri n yulyus qayser d unwi tewwed lejnas merra nufa-ten deg ungal n Fransa anda tettwabder-d Unwi xilla n yiberdan amaru n ungal agi yemmeslay-d ɤef wamek tettes Unwi d Qayser kter n wamek yelɛeb qayser s wallaɤ n bgud
