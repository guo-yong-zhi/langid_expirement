Gabryil kamps ilul di 20 meɣres 1927 yemmut di 7 ctember 2002 d amusnaw n tmetti afransis, d anemhal n Encyclopédie berbère, yettwaḥseb ula d amussnaw ameqqran deg umezruy n wegdud amaziɣ.
Gabriel Camps ilul deg Misserghin, deg Lezzayer tafransist. Yeɣra di tinmel deg temdint Wahran,  yeɣra ula deg Lezzayer tamanaɣt. Deg useggas n 1961, yesffeɣ-d seg tesdawit n Lezzayer yiwet tezrawt isem-is Aux Origines de la Berbérie. Tezdi s Iẓuran n temḍelt n umezruy amezwaru, yesffeɣ-d ula tezrawt tis snat ɣef Numidya n ugellid Masinissa.
