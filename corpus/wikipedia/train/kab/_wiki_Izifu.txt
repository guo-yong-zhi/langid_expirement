izifu   d talmest n yimɣan isaɣuren yeṭṭafaren tawsit n tzifut yettekkin g twacult n tetrawt 
Izifu d imɣi yettemcabin ar tɣeddiwt d yisri dɣa tilmas-a yakk zdint deg yiwet n tewsit .
Talmest d tin id yettemɣayen deg yideggan yezzin i yilel agrakal am Tmurt n Iqbayliyen, agafa n Umeṛṛuk, Tunes, Ṭelyan, Fransa, Turkya ...
