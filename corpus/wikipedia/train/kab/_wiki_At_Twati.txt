At Twati  d taddart deg tɣiwant n Cemmini.
amek i necfa nnan-aɣ-d, belli ussan-d deg wedrar n ǧerǧer, win zik i yellan semmeḍ yerna ulac aṭṭas n wakal n tfellaḥt, ihi, unagen-d deg ǧerǧer, ger wexxam-nsen amezwaru deg Tawrirt n Buɛli. ar tura mazal deg twacult n at Twati widak i yeqqimen dinna, ur d-unagen ara deg 1940's ar 1960's. ar assa yiwen deg twacult izga i truḥ ɣer imawlan , i ttzur-iten.
