Atmelli  d tagnit tafarit deg wemdan deg-s aṭas n yiglamen yettilin gar n tilwit  deg wermud n wemdan ar kra n telmas n wefray s tekma tamaẓwalt s usrag n kra n uxeddim iy tetten tafasa. Atmelli yezmer ad yili d afekkan neɣ d anellay  
Atmelli afekkan d tagnit andaamdan ur izemmer ara ad yeg gra n umahil  i tennum tezmert-is txeddem-iten. Dɣa asken-a d win yellan d imezdi s tuget ɣer yimdanen yal ass maca asemdu-ynes yettili d anamsad ugar mi ara ad yili wadad meqqren ɣef talest tagamant.
