Timzerɣlin  d tafesna n ibureɣsen itetten aksum, azal n 5900 n telmas id ittugelmen deg tfesna ayyi sɣur n yimusnawen.
Timzerɣlin d tid ittumeggzen d tadlafriwin, anda ilant  sin n wafriwen ifraranen yessufuɣen tidi s waṭas yerna deg yiri n zdat n yal afriw tella tkerrust ibeṭṭun afriw ɣer sin n wamuren . Timzerɣlt tettett ibureɣsen meẓẓiyen deg waman. Anda daɣen id tessrusay timellalin-is deg yimɣan id yettemɣayen deg waman yakk deg wakal yudsen ɣer waman.
