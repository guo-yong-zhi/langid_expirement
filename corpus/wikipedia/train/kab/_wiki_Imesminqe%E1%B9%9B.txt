Imesminqeṛ  d tawsit n yigṭaṭ yeṭṭafaren tawacult n tmesmineqṛin.
Tilmas n tewsit-a d tid-ak yettidiren deg yakk tamiwin n Uruppa d Tferka d Asya, yerna tuget deg-sent ttidirent deg tẓegwa. Dɣa aṭas deg-sent ttumeggzent d tilmas timinigin acku deg yal tasemhuyt n umwan ttwliwlent seg Uruppa d Asya ɣer unẓul i wakken ad ssezrint tagrest send ma ad uɣalent deg tefsut.
Tilmas n tewsit-a d tid meẓẓiyen, tiddi-nsent tga ger n 9 alamma d 15 cm, aqerruy-nsent d win meqqren, adfar-nsent meẓẓiy ma d iqamumen-nsent d amnummsan yettizhur yal mi ara ad neddu s azadur-is, afriwen-nsent yettili d aras neɣ d azunɣid.
