Ifeɣmes  neɣ Tiggaz d talmest gar telmas n tuɣmas i yellan deg uɣesmaṛ n ifganen d temsuṭaṭ s umata, yella ar zdat n uɣusmar Afellay d wadday
Ɣer waṭas n timestuga yakk d irammaten am ufgan ifeɣmasen gan d tuɣmas yessezgan i wegzam n wučči, maca ɣer temcac d uglan iy gezzemen aksum d wučči acku ifeɣmasen-is meẓẓey-it
Amdan n ifeɣmasen d win yemgaraden  s unect  n telmest d twacult n uɣersiw, amdya ar ufgan  llan 8 n ifeɣmasen ma ɣer temsuṭaṭ iden am yimcac d iysan llan 12, ɣer temseɣẓaẓ 4 . Ma ɣer iburaɣ 9
