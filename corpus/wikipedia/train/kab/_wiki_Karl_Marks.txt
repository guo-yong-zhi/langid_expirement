Karl Heinrich Marks, ilul deg 5 si Mayyu deg usseggas 1818 di tmurt n Trier yezdeɣ di Rheinland tamurt n Lalman yemmut di 14 di Meɣres 1883 di teɣremt n London. Marks yella d argaz n tsertit u d amesnagay ameqṛan, d netta i d-isnulfan ayen i wumi neqqar Tazdukla "le communisme", i d-yeqqaren belli ulac amgarad ger imdanen ɣas ulamma yella deg ixeddim-nsen n yal ass.
Ilul Karl Heinrich Marx di Trier aseggas n 1818. Yella d wis sin ger 8 Tam watmaten-is. Baba-s, Heinrich Marx , d abugaṭu, yefrurid deg yiwet twacult twudayt Ackenaz. Jedd-nsen Meier Halevi Marx yella d uday, ma d mmi-s d warraw n mmi-s uɣalen d imasiḥiyen, ẓẓiẓrgen isem-nsen ɣer Heinrich iwakken ad izmiren ad xedmen, yemma-s n Karl, Henriette Pressburg , d tudayt n tmura n wadda.
