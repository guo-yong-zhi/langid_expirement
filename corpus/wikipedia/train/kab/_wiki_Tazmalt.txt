Tazmalt    d tamdint d tɣiwant di Twilayt n Bgayet tezga-d ɣef 80 km wenẓul-amalu n tmanaɣt .
Zik yella yisem n Tazmalt d "Tazerraǧt".
Di tɣiwant n Tezmalt ad naf Tineswin ibehlal, Iḍriqen, Ruḍa, Ḥiruc, Ixerban, Lbir leqṣar, Mzerzur, Iciqer, Tazrivt, Ibejjuten Ufella , At lyutna , Allaɣen, Laɛzib Sidi Ɛli, Timetḍit, Tasergant, Bubalwi, Tigwnatin, At Sidi Ɛmer, Tabɛalt, At Muḥend Weɛli. 
Ma yella d tid yezgan di temdint: Taddart Ufella, Taddart g-wadda, La cité, Merlot ,Tiwririn ,"4 chemins", Ssaɛa, Lfilaǧ, Axxam Yerɣan.
