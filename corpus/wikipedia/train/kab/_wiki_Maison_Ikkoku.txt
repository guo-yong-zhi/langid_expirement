Maison Ikkoku  d yiwen n umanga n Rumiko Takahashi. Yeffeɣ-d di Japun sɣur n Shôgakukan seg 1980 ɣer 1987.
Yusaku Godai, yiwen unelmad i ixseṛen akk tikelt timzizlin akken ad yekcem ɣer tasdawit, textes yiwen n wass ad yeǧǧ tamezduɣt anda i yettili netta akked kṛaḍ yimedanen . Acku kṛaḍ-agi ur tettaǧǧan ara ad iɣer. Dacu kan, ass-nni, tusa-d tamasayt tamaynut n tmezduɣt-nni n Yusaku. Yiwet n taǧǧalt n 30 iseggasen, sawalen-a Kyoko Otonashi. Yusaku tehwa-as aṭas Kyoko. Yerna, yesɛa yiwen amenzay ameynut, Shun Mitaka, yiwen uselmad n Tennis. Yecbaḥ yernu d ameṛkanti. Tamellayt tanafrayt-agi ad tt-nzeg sa n iseggwasen. Aniwa ara yawin ul n Kyoko...
