Abu Nuwas al-Ḥasan ben Hani Al-Ḥakami , d yiwen seg yimedyazen imeqranen akk ay yessefran s taɛṛabt takelsakt, yerna yella yessefruy, tikkal, ula s tfarsit. 1  
Baba-s d Aɛṛab, ma d yemma-s d Tafarsit. Abu Nuwas yella yessen ad yessefru deg waṭas n tewsatin  n tmedyazt taɛṛabt, yerna yeqqel mechuṛ ladɣa imi ay d-yettwabder deg Wedlis n Wagim Waḍan ed Yiwen Yiḍ.
