Smegred gar Buxus I d ugellid Buxus II
Buxus Ii yella d agellid amaziɣ n Muretanya deg tasut tamezzwarut ST . D mmi-s n Mestanususus , i yemmuten deg 49 ST , imiren i yuli Buxus ɣef lɛerc .
Yella d mmi-s amenzu n Mestanususus , agellid n Muretanya . Baba-s d Mestanususus , uran-d fella-s s tlatinit Rex Bocchus Sosi f neɣ Sos id-yemmalen " Buxus mmi-s n Susus " , deg useggas n 49 St tettwafreq tgelda n Susus  gar Buxus II d Bugud , i yellan d atmaten . Yegled Buxus II aḥric n usammer n Muretanya tella Yul d tamanaɣt-is ma d gma-s Bugud yegled aḥric n umalu tamanaɣt-is d Bubulis .
Ayen nessen kan ɣef unbaḍ-is d kraḍ n tedyanin : assaɣ-is d Sityus , ixef-ines deg ṭṭrad mgal Yuba I , d rennuy n Muretanya n umalu . Yeddukel netta d gma-ds d ugellid Yuba I n Numidya .
