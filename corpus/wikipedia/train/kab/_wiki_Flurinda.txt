Flurinda d tageldunt d "lkuntissa" n tasut tis ṭam d yelli-s n Yulyan anembaḍ amaziɣ ameṣmudi n Sebta , yiwen seg yinembaḍen imasiḥiyen ineggura deg Tefriqt n ugafa yuɣ-itt Syus mmi-s n ugellid n Spenyul Ɣiṭca .
Flurinda d yell-is n Yulyan tekker-d d tageldunt deg Sebta , iceyyeɛ-itt baba-s ɣer Spenyul ad d-lmed akka am tgeldunin nniḍen , tella tzad di tfulki , mi tt-iwala ugellid teɛǧeb-as yekkes fella-s lḥerma , d ayen i tt-yesserfan terra ɣer baba-s yegmen ad-s-d-yerr ttaṛ .
Kra nniḍen nnan-d fella-s : 
Iwala-tt ugellid n yiquṭiyen n umalu Ledriq  teccucuf deg wasif ihi yekkes fella-s lḥerma tuɣal s tadist seg-s 
Ssawalen-as yiɛdawen n baba-s " la cava " anamek-is lferga , d ayen id-yuwin ddel i baba-s , seg reffu yemla-as i uɣella ineslem Ṭareq u Ziyad tiwwura akken ad yekcem Spenyul , ardekal nnfaq-a yeɣli Ledriq .
