Kaway neɣ Kawai , d tigzirt taqburt akk deg warxabil n Hawaii. D tegzirt tis rebɛa meqqren deg umkan-a s ugar n 1430 n km2, d tis tnayen gar tegzirin n Marikan.  Tettwassen daɣ s yisem n tegzirt n tebḥirt, tesεa 170 km deg teɣzi, tezga deg ugafa n Oahu.
Imezdaɣ n Kaway qqimen deg tegzirt-a qbel ad awḍen Iwuruppiyen s waṭas n yiseggasen.  Agezzum wis sin n Iwuruppiyen yewweḍ s lbabuṛ 
Deg useggas 1778, yewweḍ-d uselway James Cook ɣer Waimea Bay, d netta d amenzu n Yirubiyen i d-yusan ɣer tegzirin n Hawaii.
