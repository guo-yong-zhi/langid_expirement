Ursu aras  d talmest n ursuten yettidiren deg Uruppa d Asya d Marikan n ugafa , Ursu aras yezmer ad yazen gar 300 ar 780 kg, Dɣa tadulmest tameqrant yakk n telmest-a d ursu n Kodiak iy zemren ad yaweṭ ar wazuk n ursu amellal igan d aɣersiw ameqran yakk deg twacult n ursuten d iɣersiwen imseksumen iy teddun deg tegnit  
Ɣas akken anfufed n ursu aras idrus maca maca tiddukla tagreɣlant i taggaẓt n tgama tessers-it d akken-it d talmest ur tettwaggez, Acku amdan n ursuten yellan deg umadal yakk yessaweṭ ar 200,000 n ursuten azal ameqran deg-sent ttidiren deg Rusya d Alaska d Kanada d idurar n Karapat  
Ursuten arasen d iɣersiwen imeqranen d icafcalen. Tifekkiwin-nsen ččuren d acebbub yirna ẓẓay-it, Deg tuyat-is lan  kra n iɣes yecban ameddis, ikacbaren-is ɣezzif-it mesden ayen is-yettaken tezmert i tɣuzi
