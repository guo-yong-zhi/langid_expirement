Asaflaw n urtan neɣ Abazul  d yiwet n telmest n igṭaṭ igan d ayla n twacult n tberdeffilt  yessegdafen  deg wamur ameqran n Uruppa d tlemmast n Asya. Afriwen-is ur lin ara  ula d tanmegla neɣ amsekni aneflus, Dɣa asaflaw n urtan iga d tiberdeffelt tamsudemt n tewsit n Sylvia, Yuddes yerna ila iferran d udfar ɣezzifen
Tamuɣlit tuffiɣt 
Asaflaw n urtan ila tamuɣlit yuddsen s wassaɣ, Akat-is yettawaṭ ar 14 cm deg teɣzi, d udinan n wafriw ilan seg 7,6 cm ar 8,4 cm, Azuk-is yettili gar n 16 ar 22 g. Maca yezmer ad yaweṭ ar 35,5 ɣer Iylalen yesuddusen  ad inigen, Asaflaw n urtan ila adfar d wafriwen ɣezzifen
