Tagerja neɣ Tifiɣra taderɣalt  d yiwet n telmest n yizerman yeṭṭafaren tawsit n uɣezfiɣer di twacult n iɣezfiɣren. Talmest-a n yizerman ur tessrusay ara timellalin, maca tettarew srid, tettidir s ddaw n wakal d yiẓra deg yideggan n yijdi.
Talmest-a tettili deg wenẓul utrim n Uruppa, Tafriqt n Ugafa d Wenẓul n Asya.
