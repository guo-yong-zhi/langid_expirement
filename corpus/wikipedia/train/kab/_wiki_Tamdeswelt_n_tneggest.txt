Tamdeswelt n tneggest d tamdeswelt i d-ittmagen mi ara ad tembiwel tfekka gar n tissiwin n wezwu s tazzla yegdan neɣ yugaren tazzla n temdsewlin n yimesli, tga am werway neɣ am uṭerḍeq deg wezwu.
Mi ara ad tezri temdeswelt n tneggest seg tenga, aɣrud n tfasa ur yettfettit ara, maca tafasa i d-ittusufɣen d tawuri ad teftuts.
