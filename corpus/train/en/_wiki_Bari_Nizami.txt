
Bari Nizami was a lyricist from West Punjab .
 1  Nusrat Fateh Ali Khan, Attaullah Khan Esakhelvi, Noor Jehan and Ghulam Ali sang his qawwalis and songs.
Barri Nizami  was born on 26 December 1937 in Gojra in British Punjab. 2  1  Gojra now falls under Toba Tek Singh District of West Punjab . 
He had become friends with Nusrat Fateh Ali Khan. 
He died on 14 May 1998 due to lack of money for his treatment, as he was very poor. 2
