
Labrisomus bucciferus Poey, 1868
Gobioclinus bucciferus, the Puffcheek blenny, is a species of labrisomid blenny native to the western Atlantic Ocean and the Caribbean Sea.  It can be found on reefs, seagrass beds and in algal mats along rubble or rocky shores down to a depth of 5 metres .  This species can reach a length of 9 centimetres  TL.  This species can also be found in the aquarium trade. 2
