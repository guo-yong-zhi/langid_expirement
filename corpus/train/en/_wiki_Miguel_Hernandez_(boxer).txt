Miguel Hernandez  is an American boxer.
Known as "Macho", Miguel began his professional career in 2003.  When he stepped up in competition, he lost both fights: to Raúl Márquez and to Luis Ramon Campas in 2006. He won two titles, the WBC USNBC title and the Illinois state title. He is currently when?  beginning his career as a boxing trainer.
