Mukino  is a rural locality  in Nebylovskoye Rural Settlement, Yuryev-Polsky District, Vladimir Oblast, Russia. The population was 13 as of 2010. 2 
Mukino is located 8 km east from Nebyloye, 33 km southeast of Yuryev-Polsky  by road. Tartyshevo is the nearest rural locality. 3
