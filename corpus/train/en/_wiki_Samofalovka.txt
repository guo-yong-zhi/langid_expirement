Samofalovka  is a rural locality  and the administrative center of Samofalovskoye Rural Settlement, Gorodishchensky District, Volgograd Oblast, Russia. The population was 1,877 as of 2010. 2  There are 27 streets. 3 
Samofalovka is located in steppe, 27 km northwest of Gorodishche  by road. Grachi is the nearest rural locality. 4
