Idol Street  is a Japanese record label and music project specializing in idols. 2  3  4 
It was launched by Avex on October 2, 2010 as a dedicated idol record label. 5  The first artist on the new label was the idol group Super Girls, formed in June 2010. 4  5  As of March 2015, four girl groups have debuted on the label: Super Girls, Cheeky Parade , GEM  and The World Standard. 6  7  8  9 
The project includes 13 members: 9 members of Super Girls and 4 members of The World Standard.
