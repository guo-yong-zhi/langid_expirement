Dr. Mary Hill Fulstone  was an American physician. When she retired from practicing medicine at the age of 91, she was the longest-practicing physician in the state of Nevada. She was born in Eureka, and graduated from University of California, Berkeley . Known as "Dr. Mary", 1  Fulstone was honored as Nevada's Mother of the Year , Nevada's Doctor of the Year , and Distinguished Nevadan . 2  She died on December 2, 1987 at her Smith Valley home. 3
