Parlasco  is a comune  in the Province of Lecco in the Italian region of Lombardy, about 60 kilometres  north of Milan and about 20 kilometres  north of Lecco. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 147 and an area of 3.0 square kilometres . 3  Parlasco is one of the smallest Italian towns and it is located in the Parco Regionale della Grigna Settentrionale  in Valsassina, just a few minutes from Lecco. It is on the main road connecting Lake Lecco to Valsassina and joins Alpe Cainallo with Cortenova.
Colourful frescoes on house walls tell the story of Lasco the bandit, taken from a historical novel set in the 17th century that tells of the difficult life of inhabitants who were at the mercy of historical events and popular beliefs and oppressed by local landlords. The church of S. Antonio Abate. with paintings dating back to the 15th century, is also of interest.
Parlasco borders the following municipalities: Bellano, Esino Lario, Perledo, Taceno, Vendrogno.
