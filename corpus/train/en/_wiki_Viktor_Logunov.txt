
Viktor Alekseyevich Logunov  1  was a Russian track cyclist. He competed at the 1964 Olympics in the tandem and 2000 m sprint won a silver medal in the tandem with Imants Bodnieks. 2  In retirement he coached track cycling and prepared the Unified Soviet team to the 1992 Olympics. 3
