
Rampur Barara is a village in Dalmau block of Rae Bareli district, Uttar Pradesh, India. 2  It is located 11 km from Dalmau, the block headquarters. 3  As of 2011, it has a population of 633 people, in 109 households. 2  It has one primary school and no healthcare facilities. 2 
The 1961 census recorded Rampur Barara as comprising 6 hamlets, with a total population of 223 people , in 39 households and 38 physical houses. 4  The area of the village was given as 162 acres. 4 
The 1981 census recorded Rampur Barara as having a population of 370 people, in 61 households, and having an area of 60.70 hectares. 3  The main staple foods were listed as wheat and rice. 3
