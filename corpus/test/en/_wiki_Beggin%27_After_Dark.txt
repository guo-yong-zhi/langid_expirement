Beggin' After Dark is the second studio album by American R&B group H-Town, released November 8, 1994, on Luke Records. 1  The album showcases a more sensual romantic sound on ballads such as "Much Feelin' ", "One Night Gigolo", "Full Time", and "Baby I Love Ya" featuring Roger Troutman.The first single Back Seat " peaked at #18 while the album's second single "Emotions" peaked at #11 on Billboard's Top R&B Songs chart. 2
