Michi Himeno  is a Japanese animation artist and character designer. Her most famous work is on the TV series The Rose of Versailles and Saint Seiya.
In 1973 she began her collaboration with Shingo Araki working on Cutie Honey and went on to work at Toei Animation before forming Araki Productions with him in 1975. Together they have worked on many successful films and series. Typically, she works on the female characters while he works on the male. 1 
Some of her works are Lupin III , The Rose of Versailles , The Flower Child Lunlun , Ulysses 31 , Arcadia of My Youth: Endless Orbit SSX , and the OVA versions of Fūma no Kojirō .
International accreditation came with Saint Seiya , for her elegant drawing style along with Araki's dynamic drawing style. 1
